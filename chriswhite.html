Arise New York

The Central Park Concert @ the Naumberg Bandshell, Sunday, May 4, 2008, 2pm-5pm

by Evangelist Chris N. White

Chris N. White, an author of the booklets "Judgment or Revival, Which Will It Be?" and "God and Man and Monkey at Yale" and is a graduate cum laude from Yale College, a member of Yale Standard, a guest speaker sponsored by the Yale Standard, Intervarsity, Campus Crusade, assist with Bible Studies at Columbia University and an Evangelist to Bogota, Columbia.

His chief burden has been for NYC, for the Ivy League campuses, and for Colombia. His desire is to see genuine revival at a time when the Gospel of Jesus Christ is unfamiliar or misunderstood by far too many souls. He believes that revival will not come as the result of one person’s work or even the work of several independent workers. Revival comes when believers from various churches and backgrounds set aside personal agendas in order to work and pray together to build the Body of Christ, enabled by the anointing of the Holy Spirit.

Pray with us for New York City

New York is, like Jerusalem of old, a city of many nations, languages, and ethnic groups, with people from almost every country on earth. New York is a mission field indeed, a city that needs a genuine move of the Holy Spirit to turn many wandering souls back to God. Pray with us that the Lord will move powerfully for this great city, bringing revival once again.

Pray with us for the College Campuses.

Many renowned American universities, especially in the Ivy League, have long histories as Christian institutions where God worked powerfully (see www.yalestandard.com). In these schools, students were prepared to serve the Lord in ministry, in missions, and in other academic and secular fields. Pray with us that God will again bring revival on these campuses.

"Give up yourselves therefore with a cordial confidence to Christ and the great work of life is done." Timothy Dwight, President of Yale (1795-1817).

Join Chris N. White and Guest Performers: Robin Leigh Massie, Soloist & Opera Singer, The Kings Army, Men's Chorale, The Paul Paoli Band, Contemporary Christian, Debra Wood & the Guiding Steps Praise Dance Company.

Raindate: Sunday, Sept. 7, 2008.

Evangelist Chris N. White will be speaking at the World Journalism Institute in the Empire State Building, Tuesday-Wednesday, May 27-28, 2008.

For more information, email to AriseNew York@cnwhite.org or visit us at www.cnwhite.org
