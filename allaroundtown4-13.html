FEATURES

AllAroundTown
April 2013

by Stacy Wendell

The Praise Ship has landed again and our first stop was in Freeport, NY for the “Revival on the Island,” at the Perfecting Faith Church, where Pastor Donnie McClurklin is the Senior Pastor. My goodness, what a night to be able to bask in His glory and hear the heavenly sounds all night; you had to be there in the flesh at Perfecting Faith. This particular night was none other than the ball of fire Pastor Tamara Bennett of Sacramento Calif., where she is the Pastor of Pentecost Fellowship Ministries. She may be small in stature, but she is like a little tornado when she preaches the word of God. This woman of God is anointed and appointed to do what she does and she does it in excellence. I really enjoyed myself immensely.

Next stop Brooklyn Hospital for a long extended visit that I was not prepared for. I stayed in this hospital one day shy of a month, but my God is a healer. Thank you all for your prayers, calls, and concerns; through it all I learned to depend on my God. Love you all.

The next stop I slid back into Brooklyn for a stop that my body said no, but my spirit agreed. We stopped at the Tropical Paradise Ballroom; one of Brooklyn’s well kept secrets. A beautiful place if you ever want a nice place to have an event and the food is well made and wonderful for the taste buds. I had accepted this date last year and they were not letting me rescind. This awesome women’s conference, “Fabric of a Woman" was given by Tabernacle of Praise where my big brother Bishop Eric Garnes is the senior Pastor. If you want to be taught how to do a women’s conference in excellence I am sure Tabernacle of Praise would not mind giving you a few pointers. From the beginning to the end, it was a level of excellence, whether you’re listening to poetry, psalmist, dancing and Sister with Voices, it was a day of giving the best and being the best of showing the excellence. But the day was to honor women who have made their niche in our communities and given the best of their service. These women honored First Lady Karen S. Daughtry, Ms. Lidia Leudo and Judge ShawnDya L’ Simpson. I personally would like to thank First Lady Dr. Delicia B. Garnes you are definitely a precious jewel, the ladies of TOP and to the Holy Spirit for remaking and reshaping me before man. Congrats to all the women and the honorees. Thank you for allowing me to be your mistress of ceremonies and allowing God to use me for his glory and my good.
