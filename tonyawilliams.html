Intercessory Prayer Summit 2014
The Tears of Intercession:

The Cry that Moves the Heart of God

By Dr. Tonya Williams

Are you an Intercessor, Prayer Warrior or Gap Stander? If so, then the Intercessory Prayer Summit is for YOU. I, Dr. Tonya Williams the host of this auspicious event, would like to invite you to our 2nd Intercessory Prayer Summit on Thursday, July 31st thru Saturday, August 2nd at the Doubletree by Hilton Tarrytown. There is a clarion call to all who will heed to the voice of the Lord for a time of intercession, supplication, teaching and impartation as we GET-A-WAY with God. 

The theme this year is “The Tears of Intercession: The Cry that Moves the Heart of God.” It is the season our liquid prayers of intercession open the flood- gates of heaven and bring forth God’s ordained manifold blessings in our lives. As we pour out and give of ourselves in prayer, we release the power to push open hea­vens reservoir causing a trembling and a shaking in the spirit that can’t be contained.

We will have various events slated at the summit: Prayer Walks, A Prayer Room, Intercessory Prayer Luncheon ($65) and a Po­wer Prayer Dinner ($40) to name a few. The day sessions will be held from 9am to 3pm and services will be held nightly at 7pm sharp. Please note the sessions and services are subject to the Holy Spirit.

Don’t miss our Intercessory Prayer Luncheon, The Bridge Over Trouble Waters, on Saturday, August 2nd at 2pm, where we will take time to honor the intercessors in our lives. There are individuals the Lord has placed in our lives to pray us through and get us over to the “other side.” Honor the intercessor, prayer warrior or gap stan­der in your life today. Let them know their prayers are appreciated. Register at dr tonyawilliams.com/luncheon

To lead us to the prayer room, we have some of God’s dynamic prayer warriors such as Apostle Aretha Wilson, Reverend Jacqueline Gates, Evangelist Anastasia Peart, Pastor Joseph Brown, Psal­mist Evangelist Trina Johnson and First Lady Valerie Boyd and a surprise guest “The Mother of Prayer.”  

The summit registration is currently $125, however if you would like to attend the summit, apply the discount code “LOVEXPRESS” and you will receive the early bird rate of $99. You can register online at drtonyawilliams.com or by phone at 1-888-950-8048.

Position yourself today to go after God like never before. The dry parched places in someone’s life is yearning for your interceding tears to cause an avalanche in the spirit that can bring  forth the overflow of healing, deliverance, and power they so desperately need.  This will be a life changing weekend as we embark on an opportunity to answer the call of urgency in the spirit. It’s time to weep between the porch and the altar and cry out for a mighty move of God for those in our families, communities, chur­ches and nations. Don’t miss this great Pouring Out before the Lord.

For more information visit drtonyawilliams.com/ summit.
