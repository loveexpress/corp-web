Living From The Inside

One Day Conference - March 7th

Dennis and Jackie Bambino

The Church of Grace and Peace located at 1563 Old Freehold Road, Toms River, New Jersey will be hosting a one day conference, “Living from the Inside Out” with speakers Dennis & Jackie Bambino on Saturday, March 7, 2015.

A continental breakfast is included in the price of $20. For groups of 10 or more the cost will be $15. Registration begins at 8am. Worship will begin at 9am and the conference will im­mediately follow. Hosts: Walt Healy, Apostolic Over­seer / Found­ing Pastor / Trustee Board President and Maureen Healy / Co-Founding Pastor. Lead Pastor Jim Wehrer / Trus­tee / Board Vice Presi­dent.

Sign up online at www.graceand peace.org or call the church office at (732) 349-1550 x 201.
