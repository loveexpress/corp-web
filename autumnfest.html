Autumn Fest 2010

Calvary Temple International Assembly invites you to join us for our annual “Autumn Fest.” This family-oriented evening of fun will take place on Sunday, October 31st from 4pm to 8pm come rain or shine. The night will feature door prizes, games, bounce houses, a marshmallow roast & a hayride. Wristbands for the event are $6 and can be purchased at the door. Wristbands include all activities, cotton candy, popcorn and a bag of candy to take home upon departure. There will be a full snack bar serving dinner at an additional cost. Please note that this event is designed for children between the age of 3 and 12, however, children under 7 years of age must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. Don’t miss out on this fun evening for the whole family!

If you have any questions, please contact Alina at 973-694-2938 ext. 260 or visit us on the web at www.illuminatenow.com. Calvary Temple International, 1111 Preakness Ave, Wayne, NJ 07470
