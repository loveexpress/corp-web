25 Years of Unity
Concerts of Prayer Greater New York

Rev. Dimas Salaberrios

As the author of Street God and President of Concerts of Prayer Greater New York I have the privilege of traveling from cities to cities and examine prayer movements. The Tri-State has the unique distinction of praying toge­ther for 25 years. Most cities have prayer movements that rise and fall but very few have clergy that come toge­ther annually to pray faithfully for more than 10 years. We are grateful for bringing the founder of Concerts of Prayer, David Bryant back this year and Mac Pier who launched Concerts of Prayer in the greater NY area. David Bryant has been involved in mobilizing prayer movements across America and has done so more than anyone alive today.

This year is an extreme­ly important year as an orga­nization to celebrate with the clergy what God has done over these past 25 years. The prayer movement and unity of pastors watched the crime and homicides drop in New York City and New Jersey. Even the strong NYPD confessed that they did not do anything different to see the homicides drop so drastically in our city.

Concerts of Prayer Greater NY worked closely with the Redeemer Church Planting Center to introduce Tim Keller to NYC pastors to kick off the strong church planting movement we have today with the now renown City to City. Now hundreds of new churches have been planted throughout the boroughs. Mac Pier the founding president of Concerts of Prayer Greater NY has brought in hundreds of thousands of dollars into the hands of church planters to start new churches in NYC as The Church Multiplication Alliance.

In partnership with World Vision, Concerts of Prayer Greater NY help mobilized NYC pastor to sponsor children across the world in the figures of over $3,000,000. This money did not go to Concerts of Prayer this mo­ney supported children worldwide.

When 911 hit New York City the network of pastors was already in place to mobilize efforts to serve victims in churches and I was employed by World Vision who I met through once again Concerts of Prayer to give checks of $5,000 to victims. Billy Graham benefited through the network of Concerts of Prayer during his organizing of his last crusade. Just recently Luis Palau benefited from a 23 year pastors network in the city. Most ci­ties the leadership is divided no list of clergy exist and years of work goes into planning a Luis Palau Festival. In this case the vision was birthed at Movement Day a joint effort of New York City Leadership Center formed by Mac Pier and Concerts of Prayer Greater NY. The network was set up to put Luis Palau's vehicle on the tracks to move quickly down Midtown.

This year is huge for the clergy of Greater NY we are celebrating 25 years of unity amongst pastors and leaders of ministries. We need to stop and reflect on what God has done in the Tri-State area. I grew up as a young ministerwatching all this happen before my eyes and be­ing in the meetings at Chris­tian Cultural Center, Allen AME, Redeemer, Be­thel Gospel Assembly and First Baptist of Flushing were planning meetings were held and I sat there saying, "Lord why am I learning all of this?" Never suspecting that one day I would be the president of Concerts of Prayer Greater New York writing a historical article like this today.
