The People, Land &
Future of Israel

Eileen Barr

Dr. Mitch Glaser & Cho­sen Ministries present The People, the Land & the Future of Israel Con­fe­rence, Thurs-Sat, Oct. 3rd-5th @ Calvary Baptist Church, 123 West 57th Street, New York, City. Focus: The Bible's ac­count of the role of Israel & the Jewish people - both now and in the future. The speakers are renowned scholars with profound insight into God's un­folding plan and purpose for the nation of Israel as the Scripture depicts them.

This event will also help you de­velop a deeper understanding of both current & future events in the Middle East as des­cribed in both the He­brew Scriptures and the New Testa­ment. The program also includes testimo­nies of both Jewish & Arab be­lievers serving the Lord in Israel and concluding with a dynamic Satur­day evening message by best-selling au­thor Joel Rosenberg and music by Marty Goetz. Speakers: Dr. Craig A. Blais­ing, Dr. Darrell L. Bock, Dr. Michael L. Brown, Dr. Robert B. Chis­holm, Jr., Pastor Da­vid Ep­stein, Professor Craig A. Evans, Dr. John S. Fein­berg, Dr. Mitch Glaser, Dr. Walter C. Kaiser, Jr., Dr. Bar­ry R. Le­venthal, Dr. Eugene H. Mer­rill, Joel C. Rosen­berg, Dr. Michael Rydelnik, Dr. Mark R. Saucy, Dr. Mi­chael G. Va­nlaningham, Dr. Michael Vl­ach, and Dr. Michael J. Wil­kins.

For more information contact Eileen Barr (212) 223-2252: visit www.chosen people.com.
