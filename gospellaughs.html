4th Annual Night of Gospel Laughs

Global Christian Event Network (GCEN) will host its  4th Annual Ethical Stand­up Night of Gospel Laughs at 5:30pm on Saturday, August 24th with comedians Akintunde, JFK, Big G, Chris Wil­li­ams, Glory Child and Sa­xophonist Eli Fola111 at the Kumble Theater–LIU Brooklyn on Flatbush Ave­nue between Dekalb Ave. & Wil­lough­by St. One Uni­versity Plaza, Brooklyn, NY 11201.

Returning for the fourth year, NOGL is a no holds barred standup comedy experience featuring some of the best Christian comedians of the new generation along with seasoned veterans performing their funniest routines. NOGL is suitable for everyone and creates entertainment for all age groups by using the available platforms of comedy, music, dance and other expressive forms of arts.

"There are fewer choices of safe and ethical entertainment available to families and Christians around us these days" said Gben­ga Omotayo, President GCEN -NY. "Moral values are slowly disappearing from our communities and from our lives, therefore NOGL is fully focused on showing the community and the general public that you don’t have to compromise your values to have a good time and to enjoy life’’.

Leading other acts at this year's edition is Co­me­dian Akintunde, a 20 Year Comedy Veteran. In addition to multiple appearan­ces on The Word Network, TBN, The Gospel Music Channel, and The Stellars Awards, Akintunde has been on 10 national tours in the past 5 years. He toured with Pastors Creflo and Taffi Dollar on the "Change Experience" Tour for 3 years and is signed with Arrow Global Enter­tainment as their only Co­medic Artist. Akintunde has a syndicated radio show that airs in more than 50 markets.

Supporting Akintunde is Chris Williams; the 2011 Kingdom Choice Awards host who has been blessed to grace the stage multiple times at Bahamas Faith Ministries’ Annual Youth Conference “Youth Alive”, Pastored by Dr. Myles Munroe. At the 2012 edition of NOGL, Chris captivated the audience so much that they simply asked that he should be brought back. There will also be other anointed performers to include Glory Child, JFK, Comedian Big G & Eli Fola 111 and more.

Advance regular and VIP tickets are available online at http://nogl4. eventbrite.com/, www. kumbletheater.org or at the box office. For more information call 917-826-3566, 718-488-1624 or vi­sit www.nightofgospel laughs.com. Tic­kets will also be sold at the event.
