Editorial

Edith Stranges

It is a blessing to be living in this time to see what God is doing and accomplishing. It’s a joy at 73 years of age to be seeing with my spiritual eyes all that God is putting toge­ther.

One of the cries of my heart has been ‘Lord, make us one as you said you would do.’ Many times I have cried and ask God how long will it be; what do we have to do; as Saul cried out to God in Acts 9:6, Lord, what will thou have me to do. Then it came after reading the account of the New York CityServe meeting in the Christian Post and having talked with many pastors and leaders, I’ve heard the same thing. The spiritual climate is reaping. The meeting together of the ‘Old Guard’ and the New Generation, coming under one banner, JESUS.

My eyes have seen the glory of the coming age; The devil cannot stop what God has begun in John 17 when Jesus prayed and said ‘Lord, make them one.’ John 17:21. Do not be afraid. The Lord is your helper and comforter. Many times I’ve been to events, concerts, conferen­ces, etc. where they have certain praise, worship, hip-hop that I’m not used to. I immediately turn it over to the Lord, and said, “Jesus take over; My heart be your heart.” So move ahead with God. Bless the Lord, oh my soul and all that is within me. Have a Holy day on Thanksgiving.
