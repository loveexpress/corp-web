Editorial: He is Risen

Edith Stranges

For God loved the world that He gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life. John 3:16. A familiar scripture, let it now be a truth in yur life, for God is Love.

“Edith, you will ceate your world by the power of my word. In your world is confusion, lack, disappointments but in my word is peace, truth, trust, protection, prosperity and unlimited resources. Allow my word to create my kingdom.”

Isn’t that awesome. I pray the you would be encouraged in your heart. As you read the many articles and visit the advertisers and their events in this month’s Love Express; keep them in prayer.

We continued to be encouraged by your prayer support and gifts to The Love Express each month. God has indeed called you to partner with us, and for that we are so grateful. Thank you so much for allowing us to come into your home via The Love Express. Your prayers for this ministry are so needed and are a tremendous encou­ragement.
