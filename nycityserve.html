NY CityServe & CityFest Launch

Save the Date. Join with Luis Palau, A.R. Bernard, Jim Cymbala, and hundreds of other pastors and church leaders from across New York City for the official launch of NY CityServe & CityFest. Mark your calendar for Tuesday, September 23rd at 10am at Calvary Baptist Church. NY CityServe & CityFest Launch Event. Location: Calvary Baptist Church, 123 West 57th Street, New York, NY 10019

Learn more about this exciting initiative. Hear from a handful of city leaders. And help launch a powerful movement involving hundreds of churches and organizations coming together to serve the city and share the Good News in a sustainable way.

This will be a time to gather as leaders and cast a vision for our city. Don't miss this historic gathering as we work together to impact all of New York City with the Good News of Jesus Christ.
