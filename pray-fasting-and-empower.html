Pray Fasting And Empower
What Who Where When and Why?

A Definition of Fasting:  To abstain from Food. To eat .very little or abstain from certain foods, to deny ones flesh. As religions discipline.

1. What is Fasting?

To deny the flesh in order to be more keenly aware of God’s presence, direction and power. It is a discipline that pleases God, which is to be accompanied by prayer and cleansing. Fasting alone, is like a hollow building. It looks good on the outside, but its missing something.

2. Types of Fasts

One meal fast; One day fast; Three-day fast; Seven-day fast

Ten-day fast; Forty-day fast; **Eight-day fast [Moses]**

3. Godly Fasts

a) Proclaimed

1. I Kings 21:9 ff-[Wicked fast] Jezebel, ahab and death of Naboth Moabites

2. II Chronicles 20:3-[War fast] Jehoshaphat, war with the Moabites

3. Ezra 8:21-[Fast for Guidance] Ezra needed God’s direction and help

4. Esther 4:16 - [Fast for deliverance] Esther would go to plead with the king for her people, without the king sending for her; which meant death

5. Jeremiah 36:9 - [Scheduled fast] Prophesy came forth through Jeremiah, written by Baruch, read on the fast day.

6. Jonah 3:5 - [Fast for Mercy] Ninevah fasted for forgiveness, and God forgave them.

b) Lent

1. This is a fast, still observed by some Christians, which lasts for 40 days, from Ash Wednesday, Through Resurrection Sunday [Easter]. In this fast, believers go with only one meal every day, usually in the evening, Monday through Saturday, for six and a half weeks. On Sundays, they eat normally. (So the fast is actually 47 days long, minus Sundays.)

2. The purpose of this fast is to mortify the flesh, by sacrificing food (and other things of pleasure), in a way that will allow the spirit-man to be more sensitive and aware of God’s presence. The 40 days is used to symbolize the 40-day wilderness fast Jesus Christ went through prior to the beginning of His public Ministry. The number 40, in Gods’s Word, stands for a time of testing, Which can result in success or failure.

B. Make it personal!

1. Who should fast?

Everyone! It can be done alone, or as a couple, group, assembly, or nation.

2. Duties of the faster

Sanctify yourself [Joel 1’ 14]  Turn to the Lord with all your heart [Joel 2:12] 

Recognize that this is essential for your spiritual health!

3. Failure to fast

This will result in not seeing the growth you need, and hopefully desire.

C. Timing is everything!

1. When should I fast?

When I need to repent; In the time of defeat; When in sorrow for the ill-treatment I or others have received; Before battle [natural of spiritual]; When in mourning; When you’re going through a time of shame or humiliation; When in pain, or suffering an illness; When receiving a skill from God; For understanding; For fruitfulness [Gal. 6:22-23]; To save a city and invoke God’s mercy [Jonah 3:5; Esther 4:16]

To obey God’s law [O.T.] [Jeremiah 36:9]; To discipline your will; For direction from God [Ezra 8:21]; To prepare for ministry/mission field/etc.; To renew your mind; To cleanse the body; For marital growth; For directions in dating.

2. Tips on fasting

The body can go 3 days without water. If you are new at this, build gradually to a long fast. You will feel light-headed, stomach pains, headaches, etc. DO NOT overdue while fasting. Take warm baths, if possible. Go back on food slowly, after a long fast.

3. Examples of fasts

MOSES: Exodus 34:28; Judges 20:26 [Total Fast]

ISRAEL: I Samuel 7:6; 20:34; 28:20; 31:13; II Samuel 1:12;3:35;12:16 [One day total fast for cleansing]

ELIJAH: I Kings 19:8;21:27; I Chronicles 10:12 [Total fast] 

EZRA: Ezra 10:6; Nehemiah 9:1 [One-day Total Fast Mourning for Sins of Others]

DANIEL: Daniel 10:3; Matthew 4:2 [21-day Total Fast]

CHRIST: Luke 4:1-2; 18:12 [Total Food Fast]

PAUL: Acts 9:9;10:30

D. Find your closet!

Where do I Fast

Proceed with your regular schedule, unless you are on a seclusion fast, where you can shut yourself in and be alone with God.  Otherwise, find a place to be alone with the Lord daily, as you fast, in order to wait before Him in prayer, meditation, and the reading of His Word.

2. To tell or not to tell

Though it is sin to tell someone you are fasting, it is to done in secret, that effects can be seen openly.

3. The public face

Wash your face and body, look bright and alert. No one should know that you are fasting. Do not BOAST about it, either. If men think well of you for fasting, you have gotten your reward from their praise.

E. The Challenge

1. Why should I bother?

You desire to grow, to be strengthened, get a breakthrough

2. Does fasting really work?

Yes

3. Reasons that ring a bell

In our walk with God we come to places where the things we are doing to maintain that walk no longer seem adequate, because they no longer are. It’s an indication  that the time has come to strongly consider fasting.

There are answers you are going to need from God that He just is NOT going to give you until seek Him with your whole heart. And sometimes, this will include fasting.

And when it comes to drawing from the power of Almighty God to tear down Satans’s strongholds, and set the captives free, you will NEVER be effective unless the Lord has you in the place

of TOTAL commitment, TOTAL surrender, TOTAL obedience. How will we EVER be able to consistently hear the Lord’s voice if our spiritual ear has not been trained? LET’S PRAY!
