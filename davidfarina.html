Northeast Worship Institute 2012

by Stephanie Esposito

Don’t let your talent take you where your integrity can’t keep you. “I didn’t create the phrase but it’s so deep in my heart that it had to be implemented into Northeast Worship Institute,” Said Pastor David E. Farina.

“For 9 years Calvary Full Gospel Church has hosted the Northeast Worship Institute and we’ve watched leaders and worshippers develop in each area of NWI training,” said Pastor Farina, who has been serving as Associate and Worship Pastor at Calvary in Fairless Hills, PA, for over 30 years. “The reality is that leaders struggle. Every leader at every level struggles, gets discouraged or faces challenges. And at some point every leader is attacked. This year we’re not just training worshippers, we’re training leaders.”

The Northeast Worship Institute is usually a 3 day event packed with daytime training sessions taught by the finest industry and ministry leaders, followed by evening services and free concerts. This year the Northeast Worship Institute has added a new component. Starting the afternoon of Thursday, April 19th, is the brand new Leadership Summit. The summit is designed to cultivate stronger leaders in the church through the word of God and mentoring. NWI is especially excited to welcome back Lamar Boschman who will share on God’s Grace for Leaders.

“The northeast worship style is unique,” said Pastor Farina. “NWI was originally founded in Dallas as an international event. We brought it home and it didn’t take long to develop a little northeastern style…but don’t be mistaken, the Holy Spirit is the same yesterday, today, forever, and across our nation.”

At NWI you can expect to get first class training in a small group setting in these specific areas.

1. Individual worshipper
2. Worship Leader
3. Worship administration: how to organize and administer a worship ministry.
4. Song writing
5. Vocal
6. Choir
7. Band Workshop: Guitar & Keyboard for worship,
8. Worship Arts: Flags, banners, and dance
9. Technical Arts: lighting, video, sound, visual–projection, recording for worship

“Not only are you getting training but you’re getting it from the best of the best. People who are pioneers, leaders, award winners and most importantly who use their talents to glorify God,” said Farina. NWI 2012 Welcomes keynote speakers, Dove Award winner Kari Jobe, Author and speaker Bob Sorge, Pastor of the multi-campus “Christ Church” and a Chaplin for the NY Giants, Jets and in the Pentagon Dr. David Ireland, and Former Director of the International Worship Institute and Founder of “Overwhelming Grace Ministries,” Lamar Boschman.

Over 85 churches were represented at NWI last year. It’s a quality practical and spiritual education that will benefit individuals and churches big and small.

“People have walked out of this conference and said that what they’ve taken has redirected and even revolutionized the worship ministry in their church. Yet you don’t need to be a worship leader to gain tremendous value,” said Pastor Farina.

If you’d like more information about the Northeast Worship Institute or to register online go to www.calvaryevents.org.
