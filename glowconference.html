GLOW Conference

James R. Mackey II

GLOW Conference Plan of Action: On Thursday-Saturday, August 16th-18th, we will be having our Student Leadership Conference called the GLOW Conference. Symbolically, GLOW is faith based initiative encouraging students, youth and young adults to “Shine in the Midst of Darkness,” and be leaders in the midst of followers for Christ. Our goal within the confines of the conference is to Gather, Engage, and Empower 1000 youth with the WORD, as well as topics of student leadership, and to present, and sign up students for consistent community service opportunities that will be available all throughout the upcoming school year, during our “Community Service High School Tour” We have discovered that the introduction of consistent community service opportunities

 1) Reduces idle time,

 2) Increases the awareness of the need to serve others, and

 3) Looks excellent on a Student’s Academic resume, while helping them to qualify for scholarships and other employment opportunities.

GLOW Conference Schedule: Thurs-Fri, August 16th-17th  will be held at Molloy College (1000 Hempstead Ave. Rockville Centre, NY) @ the BRAND NEW Madison Theater. (Doors will open @ 7pm sharp. Conference 5 minute Countdown will being @ 8pm Sharp) SEATS are LIMITED! Saturday, August 18th, 9am-5pm will be a day of Community Service @ Mirshel Park (90 Atlantic Avenue, Hempstead, NY, 11550). The day will include, free food, health/job fair, resources, basketball tournaments, music concerts and entertainment.

Conference Host: James R. Mackey II; Key­note Speakers: Bles­sed Sheppard & Jovita Sheppard. Featured Artists: Broken Chainz, C.H.R.I.S., T.R.U. Praise, YE Hip Hop, & many more special guests. Call (516)448-4942.
