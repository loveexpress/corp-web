4th Annual Jerusalem Prayer Banquet

Robert Stearns
John Hagee
Shlomo Riskin
Asaf Shariv

The Jerusalem Prayer Banquet 2008 will be taking place on Thursday, May 15 at the United Nations in New York City which is on the 60th Anniversary of the modern State of Israel, in an historic gathering at the United Nations. Christians and Jews will stand together and lift their voices in common prayer to bless Jerusalem.

The speakers are Dr. John Hagee, Ambassador Asaf Shariv, and Rabbi Shlomo Riskin from Israel, participating in this strategic gathering to be held at the Delegates Dinning Hall of the U.N. Together we will declare our belief in, and commitment to, God's eternal covenants with Israel. Together we will stand with Israel and pray for the peace of Jerusalem in the same dining room where the presidents and heads of state from the nations of the world gather for dinner every year.

Please come and join us for the premier Christian and Jewish leadership gathering of its kind, at the very seat of the nations, the U.N. and for more information about the Jerusalem Prayer Banquet, please contact Joel James at 716-759-1058 or via e-mail at joeljames@eagleswings.to or visit our website at www.daytopray.com.
