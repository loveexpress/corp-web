Eddy Farm Retreat & Conference Center Announces Biggest Loser Contest Winners

Eddy Farm Retreat & Conference Center held a Church Biggest Loser Contest, which ran May 1st to November 9th, 2010. Each church had a team consisting of three contestants. Members were encouraged to live a healthy lifestyle finding an exercise plan and nutritional program that would personally work for them.

Eddy Farm Retreat & Conference Center is pleased to announce the winners of the contest. The Praise Tabernacle of Massapequa, New York was our First Place Winner. they receive $15,000 in retreat credit to use at Eddy Farm in 2011. The members of the church lost an average of 52 pounds each. Bay Ridge Christian Center of Brooklyn, New York took Second Place winning $10,000 in retreat credit. The three members of the church lost an average of 15 pounds each.

The weight loss is definitely an accomplishment for all of the participants. Each journey begins with just one step. Each of the participants has begun their journey to a healthier lifestyle. Our two biggest losers Tom Lynch and Jamie Royce both of The Praise Tabernacle lost 97.2 pounds and 47.8 pounds respectively. While other participants may not have had such dramatic results they still did a great job! Yvette Padilla of Bay Ridge Christian Center said, “What a great idea this was. It gave us the push we needed to get ourselves on track and begin a process no one really likes or looks forward to, but to take care of our ‘temple’ is a must. Thank you Eddy Farm for inspiring us to live a healthier life.”

Eddy Farm Retreat & Conference also ran a Staff Biggest Loser Contest in 2010. The winner of that contest was Endrea Cobb who lost a total of 22 pounds!

Eddy Farm is a Christian Retreat & Conference Center is located in upstate New York. We are committed to excellence in providing programs and facilities for conference and retreat. For more information on Eddy Farm Retreat & Conference Center or our 2011 Individual Biggest Loser Contest visit www.eddyfarm.com or call 845-858-4300.
