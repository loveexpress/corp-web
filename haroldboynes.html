Dr. MLK Jr. - "Living the Dream 2008"

by Harold Boynes

On the third Monday of each year, our country celebrates a Federal Holiday named in honor of Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. In observance of the King Holiday, CORE, the Congress of Racial Equality, hosts an Annual Ambassadorial Reception and Awards that is one of the largest tributes to Dr. King in the country.

On these occasions we seek to bring together diverse positive thinking individuals and companies-both internationally and domestically to reflect on how far we have come and to commit to even greater progress. On these occasions CORE recognizes special people who, through their words and actions, have helped to promote the principles of peace, brotherhood, equality and justice that Dr. King lived and died for.

At last year's King celebration (Jan. 15, 2007) we honored Ayaan Hirshi Ali. Ms. Ali is a former member of the Dutch Parliament and an outspoken champion of women's rights. We also recognized Dr. Frederick K .C. Price. Senior Pastor of the Crenshaw Christian Center, for his leadership role in our community and we saluted the oldest and largest African-American Fraternity in America-Alpha Phi Alpha for their role in establishing the Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. National Memorial in Washington, DC. We also presented calypso music legend, The Mighty Sparrow, with the 2007 Martin Luther King award for "Lifetime Achievement,"

Martin Luther King Holiday celebration is set for Monday, Jan. 21, 2008 at the Sheraton New York Hotel and Towers, 52nd Street & 7th Avenue in New York City. At this event we will be recognizing the leadership and philanthropic activities of Bishop T.D. Jakes, Founder and Senior Pastor of "The Potter's House" Church in Texas; Mr. Dennis Bassford, President & CEO of Moneytree, Inc., and Retired General Ricardo S. Sanchez, United States Army. Reception - 6:30pm, New York Ballroom & Dinner 7:30pm, Metropolitan Ballroom. "Living the Dream." Proceeds to benefit CORE's "Project Independence" Welfare Reform/Job Training Program. For more information please call (212) 598-4000 or visit www. core-online. org.

Please keep in mind the King Federal Holiday Commission's simple theme. "If only for one day each of us serve as a drum major for brotherhood and peace, then we will bring to life the inspiring vision of freedom which Martin dreamed.
