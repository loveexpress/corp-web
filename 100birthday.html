Pastor Jackie McCullough Announces Mother’s 100 Birthday Celebration

Jamaica, West Indies native Evangelist Keturah Phillips celebrates 100 years of life.

“With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my salvation” (Psalm 91:16).

The Rev. Dr. Jacqueline E. McCullough is overjoyed to announce the 100th Birthday of her mother, the Evangelist Keturah Elizabeth Henry Phillips. Evangelist Phillips, a native of Jamaica, West Indies, was born on August 30, 1911.

Evangelist Phillips, affectionately known as “Mother Phillips,” enjoyed over 60 years of partnership in marriage and ministry to the late Reverend Percival Herron Graval Phillips, and stands as the joyful matriarch of her three children, Jacqueline, Rose and Charles, as well as a host of grandchildren, godchildren and adopted spiritual progeny. Known for her powerful ministry of psalms, hymns and spiritual songs, this preacher of the Gospel has ministered throughout Jamaica and in the United States at her husband’s side for over 60 years. As the Mother of Beth Rapha, she is much beloved for her hospitality, superlative cooking skills, and love of beauty. Even at age 100, this elegant handmaiden of God continues to stand in intercession for her beloved daughter as the Pastor of Beth Rapha, her family, the saints of Beth Rapha, and the Body of Christ throughout the world. Known for her bold, witty humor, her life is a living testimony of the benefits of a life fully surrendered to the Lord Jesus Christ and in the fear of the Lord.

This great lady will be honored in grand style at a Centennial “My Fair Lady” Birthday Celebration in her honor. We celebrate this anointed servant of the Lord for her tenacity, commitment and devotion to the Lord Jesus Christ. To forward a birthday gift, please write Evangelist Keturah Phillips, Beth Rapha, P.O. Box 684, Pomona, NY, 10970.
