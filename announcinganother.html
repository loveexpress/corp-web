Announcing Another Women’s
Prayer Summit - October 2010

The Women’s Prayer Summits of October 1997 & 1998 gathered over 6,000 women for prayer at the Madison Square Garden Theater to intercede for every aspect of our lives – our churches, marriages, families and culture.

After eleven years we are still asked, “When are you going to have another Women’s Prayer Summit.”

After months of fasting and prayer, we concluded God’s answer is “Yes!” It is time!

WE HAVE A NEW GENERATION of women to reach. We believe God wants us to again model and teach this new generation the great power of prayer and unity in the Name of Jesus with another Women’s Prayer Summit.

It happened this way. Last year after re-reading the book, What Can One Woman Do? (The story of the 1997 & 1998 prayer summits) one of the original organizing committee of faith and Spirit-led women from the prayer summits prompted the former WPS committee members to begin fasting and praying to ask our Lord what He wants to do and how we may join with Him. She wrote:

My heart is pounding with one single thought. We need to do this again, and we can’t wait much longer. Our city is under such strong spiritual attack, and I believe that God just spoke to me and said, “This is the remedy; this is what will work and change the spiritual climate over our city. You came once before. I’m asking you to come again.”

The Women’s Prayer Summit is not a seminar, workshop, or performance. Instead, it is a working meeting, called by God Himself, where thousands of women pray for God’s Spirit to prevail.

WE BELIEVE GOD wants to raise a tri-generational prayer force to transform individuals, families, local congregations, cities, and nations through the power of agreement. If we look only at today’s economy, we will retreat in fear. However, our God is not a God of fear and defeat. As God’s daughters of the Most High, we may call upon our heavenly Father and have the whole weight, power and authority of heaven behind our prayers.

The mission statement of the Women’s Prayer Summit is from Romans 15:4-7: ASSEMBLE women of Christ Jesus, so that through endurance and encouragement of the Scriptures we might have hope.

PRAY that God…will give us a spirit of unity among ourselves as we follow Christ Jesus.

PRAISE HIM, so that with one heart and mouth we may glorify the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ.

ACCEPT ONE ANOTHER, just as Christ accepted us, in order to bring praise to God.

Thursday night, March 11, a focus group/training meeting hosted at Manhattan Bible Church over-flowed with women -- young teens to mature women. God showered upon us an exhilarating spirit of unity and love as worship and prayer from this tri-generational prayer force ascended heavenward! At the conclusion of the evening we heard, “When is the next meeting?

The Executive Committee Director invited everyone present to join a forty-day rotational fasting and prayer network to pray for specifics of the October prayer summit.
