Sharpen Your Leadership Skills

ATTEND THE WILLOW CREEK LEADERSHIP SUMMIT

AUGUST 6 – 7, 2009

The Leadership Summit is designed to help leaders and their teams keep their leadership skills sharp. In the face of cultural upheaval, economic crisis, and persistent human need, our world calls out for leaders who do not back down, withdraw or compromise. You have been charged with a higher calling. On August 6-7, 2009, you and your leadership team are invited to be one of the thousands of global leaders who are choosing to take responsibility for their leadership development. Presented by the Willow Creek Association (WCA), a worldwide interdenominational network of more than 12,000 churches worldwide, The Leadership Summit is designed for pastors, business and community leaders, board members, elders, church staff, ministry leaders, volunteer leaders, students, emerging leaders, and those who would like to improve their leadership skills.

Some 100,000 church and business leaders are expected at conference sites in more than 200 locations around the world, where talks from world-class leadership experts will be beamed live via satellite. Nine churches will host The Leadership Summit in the Greater New York City region – in New York: Brooklyn, Bronx, Glendale (Queens), Manhattan & Syosett (Long Island); in New Jersey: Ocean Grove, Wyckoff and Norwood; and in Danbury, Connecticut. The Leadership Summit is facilitated locally by The New York City Leadership Center and Concerts of Prayer Greater New York. Register for the event online at www.willowcreek.com/summit, or by phone at (800) 570-9812. Rates vary between $75-$265 depending on group size and WCA Membership. Discount rates are available for students, faculty and active military personnel.

To ensure the best rates, please register by the Super Early Bird deadline of May 19. If you have questions regarding group rates, please call Peggy at 718-721-2626, x216.

The 2009 faculty of expert leaders and thinkers have been selected to deliver practical skills, stretch your thinking, and challenge you to reach the full measure of your potential as a leader.

This year's speakers include:

* Bill Hybels, Founder and senior pastor of Willow Creek Community Church, South Barrington, IL;
* Dr. Henry Cloud, Clinical psychologist, author, and business consultant;
* Carly Fiorina, Former chairman and CEO of Hewlett-Packard,business commentator, and strategic advisor;
* Dr. David Ireland, Founder and senior pastor of Christ Church, Montclair, NJ;
* Patrick Lencioni, Business consultant, author, and president of The Table Group, Inc.;
* Gary Hamel, Renowned business thinker and visiting professor at London School of Business;
* Dr. Tim Keller, Founder and senior pastor of Redeemer Presbyterian Church, Manhattan, NY;
* Jessica Jackley, Co-founder of Kiva.org, the world's first peer-to-peer online micro-lending website;
* Harvey Carey, Founder and senior pastor of Citadel of Faith Covenant Church, Detroit, MI;
* Dave Gibbons, Founder and lead pastor of Newsong Church, Irvine, CA;
* David Gergen, Political analyst for CNN and PBS and former White House adviser to four presidents;
* Dan Heath, Author, consultant, and co-founder of Thinkwell;
* Chip Heath, Author, consultant, and professor at Stanford's Graduate School of Business;
* Andrew Rugasira, Founder of Good African Coffee and African trade advocate;
* Bono, Lead singer of U2 and activist in the fight against AIDS and poverty in Africa; and
* Wess Stafford, President and CEO of Compassion International.

Founded in 1992, the WCA is a not-for-profit association of more than 12,000 churches from 90+ Christian denominations. International affiliates are located in Australia, Brazil, Canada, Denmark, Germany, Mexico, Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, South Africa, Sweden, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom. Member Churches range in size from very small to very large, and are located in urban and rural communities.

The New York City Leadership Center is an independent non-profit organization recently formed by Concerts of Prayer Greater New York. Our mission is to exponentially increase the individual and collaborative leadership effectiveness of Christian ministry and marketplace leaders. For more information, please visit our website at www.nycleadership.com.
