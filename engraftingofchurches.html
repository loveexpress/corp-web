Engrafting of Churches in NYC

Bishop H. Curtis Douglas
Overseer Rev. Stanley M. Kidd and Rev. Lenore Kidd

Bishop H. Curtis Douglas and Overseer Stanley M. Kidd confirmed in April 2010 that Dabar Worship Center of Brooklyn, NY and Bethlehem Missionary Church of Queens Village, NY, are merging under a new name and will be housed in the newly renovated, $5 million dollar facility, that now houses Bethlehem Missionary Church, located at 218-38 98th Avenue, Queens Village, New York 11429. The Services of Engrafting will be held on Saturday, October 2, 2010 at Noon. Please mark your calendar and plan to join the cloud of witnesses for this joyous and momentous occasion.

Overseer Stanley M. Kidd

Dabar Worship Center was founded by Rev. H. Curtis Douglas; an anointed and appointed vessel of God, ordained to the presbytery in 1990, and has been serving in ministry for over 35 years. Bishop Douglas will be the Senior pastor of the newly engrafted local church.

Bethlehem Missionary Church was founded by Rev. Stanley M. Kidd, Rev. Lenore Kidd, and Dr. Vernice Glaves. Overseer Stanley Kidd; an anointed and appointed vessel of God, was ordained to the presbytery in 1958, and has been serving in ministry for over 50 years.

For further information please contact Rev. Yvette Alphonsus, Episcopal Liaison, Dabar Worship Center, P. O. Box 30151, Brooklyn, NY 11203. Tel:718-758-0214. Email: dabaryal@aol.com www.dabarworship.org
