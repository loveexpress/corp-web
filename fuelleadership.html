Fuel Leadership

The Fuel Leadership Training which is an extension of the Fuel Movement that was birthed out of the Northeast region to equip, encourage, and refuel senior pastors, student pastors & youth pastors will be held on Sat, Feb. 22nd, 9am-2pm at Faith Fel­lowship Min., 2707 Main Street, Sayre­ville, NJ.

“It is our heart to unite for one purpose, one passion, and one movement to change the lives of this next generation of leaders. A 1-day, intensive training hosted with Ja­cob Burgei, Dan­ny Sana­bria & Chris Dur­so to bring forth a word to encourage,to connect, and to equipleaders for what Godhas called them to do.
