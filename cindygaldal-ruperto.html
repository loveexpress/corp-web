Oasis Singles

Hundreds of Singles to Attend the Annual Oasis Singles Valentine’s Event

by Cindy Galdal-Ruperto

When was the last time you saw a film so riveting it kept you on the edge of your seat? This year, at our ANNUAL OASIS SINGLES VA­LENTINE’S EVENT coming up on Saturday, February 15th at 6pm we will be showing just such a film. When I saw it I was struck by how profound it was and by how it bought me closer to the Lord. We are inviting singles not just to come and enjoy the film but to bring unsaved friends and family with them so they can be drawn to the Lord.

The biggest singles event of the year is our VA­LENTINE’S EVENT. These events always draw our lar­gest crowds. Many singles of all ages from different denominations and from all walks of life from throughout the tri-state area attend each year. We host these Christ-centered events at The First Evangelical Free Church located at 6501 Sixth Avenue in Bay Ridge, Brooklyn. We suggest that you arrive by 5:30pm because tickets cannot be reserved ahead of time.

This year the focus will not just be on the God who is Author of Love but on how we can share His love with others.

We will begin this very special evening at 6pm with a meal. Singles are used to eating alone, so it is wonderful to be able to enjoy a meal with fellowship. The dinner will feature a catered Italian buffet. The delicious menu consists of two choices: Rigatoni with meat sauce, salad and bread or Baked Ziti, salad and bread. A cozy café atmosphere with low lighting and lots of greenery makes it the perfect place for making new friends. You can come with friends or by yourself. Many singles come by themselves and travel a long distance to do so. Friendly greeters will make you feel right at home from the moment you arrive until the moment you leave. Church singles groups are welcome.

Our guest speaker will be Martin Aschner from Calvary Baptist Church in Manhattan. He is committed to the love ethic of Jesus. As such he is enthusiastic about sharing the Gospel with others. His presentation will include not just this short film but also practical tips for reach­ing out in love to anyone you know. There will also be a time of interaction with other singles as we break up in small groups to discuss the film.

Fellowship and dessert will complete this uplifting evening. There will be plenty of time to meet everyone who attends this event. We have a special way of helping you do that! OASIS is a very friendly place. We will also be presenting a new game room for everyone who wants to join in an activity as a group. This is a great way for singles to make new friends in an informal, fun way.

If you are tired of the singles scene, OASIS will be a refreshing change for you. There is no excuse to stay home. Come on out! This Valentine’s you’ve got a date with hundreds of Christian singles who are living for God and enjoying their inseparable union in Him. His eternal, unshakeable Love is worth celebrating and sharing!

Admission is $15 at the door. Tickets cannot be reserved ahead of time. Attire is casual, but neat. If you would like to volunteer to help out as we set up & decorate before the event call 718-836-0029 or e-mail Cindy@CompleteinChrist. com. To volunteer the day of the event you need to arrive at 4 pm. Visit www. CompleteinChrist.com for directions.
