50th Anniversary

Bishop Peter & Min. Rose Marie Bruno

The Rev. Doctor Bishop Peter Bruno & Minister Rose Marie Bruno of Nutley celebrated their golden anniversary year with a 13 day cruise to the South Caribbean Islands. A family dinner is planned, a road trip to several southern states and other activities to celebrate this event.

The former Rose Marie Butera and Bishop Peter Bruno were married June 14, 1958 at the Christian Pentecostal Church in Rochester, NY after Bishop Bruno finished Bible College in Lima, NY. The couple honeymooned in Miami Beach, FL. Bishop Peter Bruno has graduate degrees from Elim Bible College, Int'l Bible Seminary, Orlando, FL. & Evangelical Theological Seminary, Dixon, MO. Minister Rose Marie Bruno has a Ministerial degree from Logos Bible College. They traveled in the Evangelistic Ministry for 5 ½ years before pastoring the Bethel Full Gospel Assembly in Jamestown, NY. They came to Nutley August 10, 1970 to pastor the then, Nutley Assembly of God which is now the Nutley Abundant Life Worship Center. They have pastored this church for the last 38 years and have been in full time ministry for 50 years.

Bishop and Mrs. Peter Bruno have four children. Two sons, Rev. Peter Bruno, Jr. and his wife, Pamela, who pastor Metro Church in Saddlebrook, NJ; Rev. Paul Bruno and his wife, Suzanne, who is Senior Associate Pastor at the Nutley Abundant Life Worship Ctr., two daughters, Joyleen Mainiero and her husband. Louis and Jillaine Gingerelli & her husband Anthony.

The couple have 13 grandchildren, Peter, Taryn, DeAnna, Samantha, Alex, Louis, Vincent, Dominic, Salvatore, Jenna, Julia, Anthony and Jada.

Congratulations to Bishop Peter & Min. Rose Marie Bruno on your 50th Golden Anniversary from The Love Express.
