Benny Hinn

Comes To New York - May 24-25

Benny Hinn

On May 24–25, 2012, New York City will welcome Pastor Benny Hinn and his highly anticipated Miracle Crusade at the United Palace Theatre, 4140 Broadway, New York, NY 10033.

Pastor Benny Hinn says this about the upcoming events: “I know the glorious power of the Holy Spirit will descend upon us in New York during this Miracle Crusade. Come expecting a miracle from Jesus! And come bask in the awesome presence of God. I’m expecting the Lord to move mightily, healing His people, and I know His power will be glorious in these services. I will also have a very special word that the Lord has put on my heart. This word will stir your faith greatly to make 2012 your year of total recovery and renewal.”

The 7pm services on Thursday and Friday will be Miracle Services and the 10am Friday service will be an Impartation & Anointing Service. Doors open at 4pm for the evening services and 8am for the morning service.

A packed crowd is expected, so everyone is en­couraged to arrive early for seating.

In his 38th year of ministry, Pastor Benny Hinn, noted teacher, healing evangelist, and bestselling author, is well-known around the world for his massive Miracle Crusades, attended by millions each year. His three-day crusade in India, attended by over 7.3 million people, remains the largest healing and evangelistic crusade in recorded history. His daily television program, This Is Your Day, is telecast into 200 countries. Pastor Hinn’s bestselling books include Angels & Demons, Good Morning, Holy Spirit, Blood in the Sand, Lamb of God, and The Gift.

Worldwide mission outreaches of Benny Hinn Ministries include My Father’s House children’s homes in Mexico and the Philippines. Built from the ground up from the support of Benny Hinn Ministries partners and ministry friends, these homes provide year-round care for over 150 abandoned and parentless children in beautiful facilities. Partners of his ministry also support the operations of a remarkable hospital and pediatric center in Calcutta, India. In all, each year Pastor Hinn’s generous partners help provide food, clothing, shelter, education, and Christian nurture for tens of thousands of children around the globe through partnerships with missions and relief agencies.

Concerning the Miracle Crusade, Pastor Hinn says, “I believe the Lord has spoken to me that God’s people need a fresh move of His Holy Spirit. We are seeing this happen in service after service. I believe He is awakening people around the globe to all that He has prepared for them. God wants to do this in NY on May 24–25, and I have asked my partners and ministry to pray for a mighty move of God during all three services!”

Don’t miss this Miracle Crusade on Thursday and Friday, May 24–25. Come expecting the Lord Jesus to touch your life and make you whole. Bring your loved ones and friends and believe for the inevitable supernatural intervention of God.

For churches that wish to request reserved seating for your pastor and staff, be included in the Miracle Crusade Participating Church Booklet, or help spread the word about these services, call 1-877-777-7710 or go to BennyHinn.org/Events.

Crusade Dates and Times: Miracle Services, Thursday & Friday, May 24th–25th, 7pm. Anointing & Impartation Service, Friday, March 25th, at 10am. Doors open at 4pm for the evening services and 8am for the morning service. Crusade Location: United Palace Theatre, 4140 Broadway, NY, NY 10033.
