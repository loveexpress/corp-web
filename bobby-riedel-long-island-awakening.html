Long Island Awakening

Bobby Riedel

The Long Island Awakening returns on June 12, 2015 at the Deer Park High School 1 Falcon Place, Deer Park, New York 11729 with a 7pm start time! The Long Island Awakening has a goal of bringing the denominations together, the different cultures and ethni­cites together, and various church and ministry leaders; under one roof in the spirit of unity to pray for a plethora of topics facing the NY area.

At its debut event in March, over 650 people showed up to pray...Baptist with Pentecostal, Charisma­tic Episcopal with Non-Deno­minational, French, Chinese, Indian, Spanish, and Native American Christians praying together hand in hand!

The Police, Air Force, and Marines were also represented, as well as, the Christian Bikers, the tattooed preachers, women ministers, the old, the young, and even people with disabilities took the stage to pray. Deaf For Jesus Ministries was represented, and prayed in sign language.

Event Coordinator, Bob­by Riedel, is excited about the second event in Deer Park. When interviewed, he said, "The response was so overwhelming to have another one, and the support among the Pastors across the island from all different denominations, was so refreshing! Unity is vital to any future revival, and once we learn to forget what divides us in terms of doctrine or theology, but rather concentrate on the fact we all love Jesus and are called to being united in the bond of peace, then we can really be a force on the island"!

All are welcome to come to the June 12th, Long Island Awakening. It's free, it's powerful, and it's open to anyone who identifies as a Christian. Fifty ministers will be taking the stage to pray for 2 mi­nutes each, and there are always surprises.

For more information, please call Bobby Riedel at 631-940-1935 or visit us at www.integritybookings. com.
