Full Circle

by Pastor Anthony W. Gilyard

Bethlehem Judah Christian Fellowship formerly of East Orange, New Jersey has a place they can call home at 83 Galloping Hill Road in Elizabeth, NJ.

On April 13, 2008 Founder and Pastor Anthony W. Gilyard and his congregation had a ribbon cutting ceremony to commemorate a new beginning for their new church after renting in a couple of other spots over the last 5 years.

As the members slowly filed into the $1.4 million edifice, joyful optimism, praise and various choir singing filled the church as they held their first service in their new venue.

Some of the church’s features we were blessed with a facility that houses a main sanctuary, gym which will be used for Youth Church, Fellowship Hall, Dance Studio, Administrative Office space and multiple bathrooms.

In the latter part of the service Pastor Gilyard preached a powerful sermon entitled “A Trans-Generational Covenant.” Genesis 35:11

God had made a Transgenerational Covenant. God will cause us to revisit the place where it all started.

It’s amazing how God will bring us full circle, this is when you know you are moving in the completion and the next step in God and life because he often will take you in a loop and bring you back where you first started.
