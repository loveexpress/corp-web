The Raising Up of An Army of Prayers

Pastor Leroy Gripper, Jr.

God’s Army of Pra­yers Ministries (GAP) is a prayer ministry established and operating in Brooklyn, NY- founded by Pastor Leroy Gripper Jr. The mission of GAP is to promote prayer in the Body of Christ and to create a network of intercessors, prayer warriors, pro­phets and believers who will agree in prayer and pray the will of God - as outlined in the scriptures of the Old and New Testaments.

Inspired by the Word of God which proclaims: “For My house shall be called a house of prayer for all nations” Isaiah 56:7 (NKJV), Pastor Gripper passionately promotes prayer within the Body of Christ and believes that praying is taught and not caught. Pastor Gripper’s vision is to open a 24 hour prayer and learning center where people can pray in a safe environment and experience an atmosphere that enables confident and effective prayer.

“Since prayer is essential to our Christian life, there is an assumption that Christians are praying and praying effectively”- says Pastor Gripper. Some critical factors about the state of prayer in today’s church is the low attendance in many prayer services and a significantly low number of men engaged in corporate prayer activities. GAP Ministries, under the leadership of Pastor Gripper, is positioning itself to help change this.

GAP Ministries accepts the call to train an army of prayer warriors which extends beyond denominations, local assemblies/ministries, nationalities and cultural identities. GAP simply believes that intercessors, watchmen, prayer warriors and prophets alike pray more effectively when they are trained. For this reason, GAP is building bridges for pastors and prayer leaders to work together in harmony and unity in order to make serving and praying together easy and effectual.

“FELLOWSHIP OF PRA­YER” is GAP Ministries’ weekly service; held every Thursday evening at 121 New York Avenue, Brooklyn NY from 6:30pm–9pm where fellowship, teaching, breaking bread together and pra­yer takes place. “And they continued steadfastly in the apostles' doctrine and fellowship, in the breaking of bread, and in prayers.” Acts 2:42 (NKJV)

Register at Marriot LaGuar­dia Hotel. For information, Registration and Hotel Bookings, call Joy Brown 347-450-5159 or God’sarmyof prayersministries.org. Why the theme “Pre­paring For The Coming of the Lord”? “It is a God gi­ven focus for the workshop. Intercessors, watchmen, pro­phets and all of God’s people called to pray; saints of every rank and office should be part of the process of preparing for the returning of The Lord. Scripture clearly declares that Jesus will return and GAP Ministries believes that His return is eminent. Christians should be able to answer this question with a resounding yes: ‘When the Son of Man comes, will he find faith on the earth?" Luke 18:8 (NIV) says Pastor Gripper.
