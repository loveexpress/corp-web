Editorial: Persistent Prayers

Editor & Publisher: Edith Stranges

God will make a way when there seems to be no way. Pray and claim Psalm 91 daily.

Daily I read Psalm 145:2 “Everyday will I bless thee; and I will praise thy holy name for ever and ever.” Sometimes we are so busy, we forget to pray for our brothers and sisters in the Lord, but the Holy Spirit is here to remind us to keep them in prayer.

Continue to pray for:

 • The Love Express distributors each month; they faithfully give the papers to churches in the Tri-State area. What a Miracle.

 • The pastors who give out The Love Express in their churches.

 • For the Staff.

 • For those who owe The Love Express. Lord forgive them.

 • For continued growth of The Love Express. For Fa­vor, an abundance of Favor.
