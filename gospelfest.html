30th Annual McDonalds® Gospelfest

McDonald’s® Gospelfest will be held on Saturday, May 11th, 2013, 3pm. (Doors Will Open at 2pm) Celebrating 30 Years of God’s Amazing Grace! Leading this year’s Stellar line-up Donnie McClurkin along with Smokey Norful, John P. Kee and LeCrae. Legendary singer Aretha Franklin will return to her gospel roots to perform at the 30th Annual McDonald’s Gospelfest. A Powerful message on this historical Mother’s Day weekend by Bishop T. D. Jakes!

Produced & directed by Emmy Award winning Producer, A. Curtis Farrow. McDonald’s Gospelfest is a talent competition that features many abilities. Rising stars of all colors, ages, cultures & faiths will compete in a variety of categories including Soloists, Youth Choir, Adult Choir, Praise Dance, Step, Singing Groups & Gospel Rappers.

For further information visit www.mcdonalds gospelfest2013.com
