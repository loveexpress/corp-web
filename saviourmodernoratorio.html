Saviour - A Modern Oratorio

20th Anniversary Performance

On October 25, 2014, 6pm, Carnegie Hall will be the site of a historic sacred concert...the 20th Anniversary Performance of SAVIOUR: A Modern Oratorio, featuring the original recording artists, Steve Green, Larnelle Harris, Wayne Watson, Twila Paris and Keron Jackson, conductor and writer/creators of this unique sacred masterwork. 20 years ago, Christian mu­sic songwriters Greg Nelson and Bob Farrell wrote SAVIOUR, a modern oratorio masterwork unlike anything recorded or performed in our modern age of sacred Christian music. The oratorio musical format, which we’ve known through composers such as Felix Mendelssohn and G.F. Handel, has been beautifully expressed in true oratorio style, but with modern stylings and epic vocal solos performed by some of the most iconic Christian mu­sic artists ever to be assembled. These artists, who originally recorded Saviour, have been brought together once again for this 20th anni­versary performance at Car­negie Hall.

Saviour is truly the story of God’s love for those He created and the redemption provided through His son Jesus Christ, presented in a one-hour musical offering that will take the audience on a beautiful journey from Genesis through Revelation. Saviour is a unique work that deserves to be heard around the world.

We hope you will pray­erfully consider coming to this wonderful Christian masterwork at Carnegie Hall in NYC. Your sponsorship will be recognized and appreciated as a part of this entire 3-day NYC project of rehear­sals and performance activities. Our hosted group of singers, players and accompanying non-performers will number over 400 people and our anticipated audience for a Car­negie Hall sell out will be around 2800 people. Your contributions will help provide funding for many of the operational and personnel expenses required to stage a production of this scale.

Some of the areas fun­ded through your sponsorship are: venue rental, labor and production costs, equipment costs for chorus risers, chairs, musical instrument rentals, orchestra personnel payroll, conductor expenses, solo artists expenses, promotional expenses...along with other production-related needs.

For 20 years church choirs across America have performed Saviour, many of them having performed the work multiple times. Our 20th Anniversary performance chorus will be comprised of 250 voices from several church choirs from across America who have appreciated saviour for many years, traveled to NYC at their own expense and are eager to be a part of this historic evening at Carnegie Hall.

Our performance will be played live by the True North Symphony Orchestra which is comprised of professional instrumentalists from the greater New York area and some players from other parts of the United States. Our conductor, David Hamilton, was the original ar­ran­ger of Saviour and is, today, one of the most sought after arran­ger/composer/conductors in Christian music.

True North Presents works exclusively with top Christian music artists to stage high-qulity symphonic concerts in the greatest concert halls in the world.

This presentation will be the 12th concert event produced and presented by True North Presents at Carnegie Hall.
