Fit for the Kingdom Conference

Damascus Road Baptist Church

On March 19-22, 2015, The Damascus Road Baptist Church will be sponsoring their "Fit for the Kingdom Conference" Luke 9:62 at the Stony Point Resort in Stony Point NY.

This years theme was selected by our Pastor John Jamison as he went before the Lord in prayer concerning the conference "The Holy Spirit" revealed to him that the church must not keep starting,stopping and looking back. The church must move forward and do the works that Jesus has charged us (The body of Christ) to perform. Its not about denominations its all about JESUS. The world is looking for answers and the church has the answer for the world. Lets be about our Fathers Business for the night draws near. Lets get busy!

This conference will have many outstanding kingdom minded speakers (singles and youth included) with workshops designed to push, re­new, and transform your mind, body and soul. This conference is designed so that each person can be completely healed. In order to do the work for the Kingdom you must be "fit." No looking back!

Our Saturday night (7pm) speaker Dr. Jackie McCullough will be doing an exclusive Leaders and Pastors seminar on Saturday, March 21st from 1:30-3pm entitled "Taking the limits off of GOD."  This is a hands on seminar designed by Dr. Jac­kie McCullough to help leaders move forward. We wanted to have a special seminar whereby the woman of GOD could impart valuable Kingdom knowledge into Leaders / Pastors.

This is a special invitation to All Pastors and Leaders to join us at Stony Point for this event.  Please RSVP limited seating available.

Please clear your calendars and join us for this 3 day life changing conference.

To register contact us at www.drbc.info "DRBC Teaching Kingdom Principles" Valerie Montgomery Telephone: 516-717-5788
