With God All Things Are Possible

Apostle Lionel & Pastor Margaret Etwaru
by Kenise Etwaru-Singh

With God All Things Are Possible is the Song of Celebration coming from the Pastor, Leaders & Members of Global Christian Ministries.

This Multi-cultural Immigrant Church founded and being pastored by Guyanese-born Apostle Lionel Etwaru and his wife, Pastor Margaret Etwaru had its beginnings in a basement in Richmond Hill, Queens. After moving to a number of venues including the usual store-front, they acquired this Magnificent, Gothic and Architecturally Designed Stone Church Building which was built in 1900 AD (a landmark in Richmond Hill, NY), and formerly owned by the Union Congregational Church.

Many have been the hurdles over a two-year period but God enabled His people to Triumph. Our offer of a little more than half the asking price of $5 million, was accepted. This was against the back-drop of developers offering the full price, plus a few millions more. We were further confronted with the selling of our building. Two contracts were canceled and we were being pressed by the sellers to keep our contractual commitment. Finally, a new facetious offer was made cutting the price by another $250, 000.00, lowering the interest from 7-1/2 % to 5%, and instead of a Deposit of $1,000,000.00 only $490, 000.00 was accepted. The sellers are also holding the Mortgage for two years.

The Property consists of an Ornate Sanctuary which accommodates 500. There is a 6,000 square feet Banquet Hall with a Stage and a Balcony and seating capacity of 306. A 6,000 square feet Gymnasium with a Professional Basket Ball Court is in the Lower Level. There are also 11 other rooms for offices and other activities. One is a Board Room that can seat at least 60 persons with an attached Kitchenette.

The Banquet Hall was used as a Theatre for a number of decades at the beginning of the last Century.

Global Christian Ministries will continue to use the Sanctuary for its growing Congregation to reach this area with the Gospel. The Banquet Hall is available for Social and Religious Events. The Gymnasium with the Basket Ball Court and the other Rooms will be used for Programs like After School, Day-Care, Parenting, Sport Activities, Soup Kitchen and Distribution of clothes, for the Community with the Emphasis on Youths, Children, Family and the Needy.

Examples: A Valentine Party for Couples, a Family Easter Party, a Sweet-Sixteen & Bridal Showcase are Planned, distribution of over one hundred winter coats and a Praise-Up Gospel Concert, May 2nd, and a Pastors & Leaders Conference.

Apostle Lionel Etwaru & his wife, Pastor Margaret Etwaru have been the founders and pastors of Global Christian Ministries, 86-02 115th Street, Richmond Hill, NY 11418, for the past 19 years.

Saved from Hinduism in September 1963, Apostle Lionel has been involved in Ministry since 1964. He has served in Guyana, South America, the country of birth, as one of the pioneers of the Full Gospel Fellowship of Churches & Ministers that today has about 170 churches throughout the country. This group with an Apostolic mandate has raised and trained men and women in Ministry to serve in Guyana and in 26 different Nations.
