Viewpoint: AMERICA

By Rose Acker Lictro

THE PLEDGE

I pledge allegiance to the Flag of the United States of America and to the Republic for which it stands, one na­tion under God, indivisible with liberty and justice for all.

Folks, do you know the pledge? Do our children know it; Do we put our hands over our hearts for the pledge; Do we still salute when the flag passes by at a parade?      Al­ways keep this in mind.

People of America, when are we going to get serious about our nation and for those who gave their lives for our liberty and freedoms. Where are we as the “most outstanding Free Nation in this world.” Every American citizen, please let us take back what we so freely give away and hold it close to us! Our honor, our truths, our lives, jobs, homes, religions, and start speaking Right about America, USA. Start waving the flags on our homes, stores, parks, everywhere; be proud. I be­lieve in America and I be­lieve in the good work we Americans do every day. I love my country, America. Do you love her?

I was born August 3rd, 1939, World War II baby and I know what it felt like at the age of 4 to cover our windows at night with sheets, the coupon book for the bread lines, civil defense drills in schools, loved going to so many parades for soldiers coming home, visiting the great ships in New York harbors, listening to Bob Hope & the USO on the ra­dio; My uncles going to war and most of all, people praying for our nation and every adult and child carrying their Bibles.

THE FLAGS

I’m overwhelmed with gratitude for our Country our Flags and for the Sacrifice of Heroes who put their Lives on the Line to Defend our great Nation, our Family’s Life and Liberty. America Thank You! I’m Proud to Fly Flags on my Home. FYF
