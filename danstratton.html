Seizing Opportunities in Troubled Times

Three Words: Strategic / Practical / Urgent

by Dan Stratton

Strategic – When you look at the list of people that are coming to the ProVision Network Summit in Tenafly, New Jersey, June 11-13, you have to see the strategic team that is assembled. For example, on our roster of speakers you will see Gordon Robertson of the 700 Club. His work is strategic and global, and from the untrained vantage point, charitable and feel-good, but behind the scenes there is much more going on. You may also recognize Ed Rollins from CNN. His expertise in politics is recognized worldwide. The Securities and Exchange Commission, Fannie Mae, Worldwide Asset Gathering, Global Intellectual Property, Micro finance, Mexico, Funding, Military and Diplomacy. It is all there, as you look at who the speakers are, but what you can’t see is what will go on in small groups and with the others that are coming from across the nation and around the world to be part of the conversation. This conversation will begin to unify forces across the seven mountains of influence: government, family, business, arts/entertainment, education, media, and religion. Marketplace ministry leaders from across the nation are coming to bang out a common vocabulary so that we can begin to communicate clearly and execute quickly what the Holy Spirit is saying to all of us across the nation.

Practical– When are we going to stop talking? When are we going to start moving? I have been criticized by New Yorkers for being too connected outside the city. I have been criticized by those outside the city for being too New York. Criticize all you want, I am rolling out the red carpet for my best friends and making them available to you. Mi casa es su casa. Are you feeling like you want to do something? Nothing would thrill me more than for the New York/New Jersey/ New England crowd to show up to insist upon being a part of this dialogue. Richard Johnson, Gerry DiFiore, Ed Rollins, and Kevin McGovern are all New Yorkers. Google them. Decide that you want to run with some big dogs and get yourself to the meeting. Refuse to sit on the sidelines. Call and register. Write your bio and get it submitted and come to the table. Get your supply boiled down to a one-page presentation and submit it to the Book of Names for the weekend. Be counted. Come transparent, let people know what you need, but keep your ears open to see where you can help others.

Urgent – Mexico is boiling over and Luis Antonio Marquez Heine is coming to speak to that issue. Kevin McGovern will be fresh from the Global Forum on the Base of the Pyramid work that is coming out of our Universities worldwide. His work in the water space is cutting edge. Hear from Chuck Vollmer as he informs us as to the minds of those that live in the Middle East. The time is now and the only question at hand is this: Are you going to seize this opportunity to meet some of the best men and women that God is using today in the strategic, practical, and urgent places? I double dog dare you to come. It is past time to criticize. It is past time to have another discussion. It is time to hit your knees and ask the Lord whether or not you are called to this company. I hope to see you there.

Pastor Dan Stratton is the Senior Pastor of Faith Exchange Fellowship in New York City. For more info call (212) 430-6510.
