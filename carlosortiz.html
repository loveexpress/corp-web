18th Annual Pastor’s Prayer Summit

January 26-28, 2009

A Spiritual Highlight for 300 Pastors and Ministry Leaders Representing Multiple Races and Denominations

by Carlos Ortiz

A call is going out to pastors, ministry leaders, prayer coordinators, church planters and youth pastors to attend the 18th annual Pastors’ Prayer Summit sponsored by Concerts of Prayer Greater New York. Join us as we meditate on the Word and seek our Father’s face in prayer, individually and corporately. Experience the reality of Christ-like unity, across race and denomination, with pastors and leaders from New York, New Jersey and lower Connecticut. The Summit will be held in the Pocono Mountains at the Tuscarora Inn & Conference Center, Monday January 26 to Wednesday, January 28. Register online before December 6 for the best conference rates at www.COPGNY.org. Registration includes meals, lodging and conference materials.

“Our desire is to provide pastors and leaders with a program featuring devotional themes that will refresh, encourage and challenge pastors to grow in their spiritual formation, and to be more effective in their leadership,” says Rev. Carlos Ortiz, pastoral liaison for Concerts of Prayer. “Providing pastors with a time of personal renewal and time alone with God to rekindle their first love -- after serving on the frontlines at their various churches -- is a high priority for us as we develop the 48-hour program.”

The theme this year is “Consecrate Yourselves,” taken from the Book of Joshua, chapter 3, verse 5. The theme was chosen to help leaders to refocus their hearts and spirits on why and to whom we’re engaged in fulfilling our calling. The theme also lends itself to the launching of the 40 Days of Love national campaign being launched by Pastor Rick Warren and Saddleback Church in Lent of ‘09. “We see the value of this new collaborative to grow the spiritual and relational impact of the Church in our region,” continued Ortiz. “We invite pastors participating in the campaign to join us at the Pastors’ Prayer Summit for a special time of consecration.”

For more details regarding the Pastors’ Prayer Summit, go to www.COPGNY.org or call 718-721-2626, x211. To join the 40 Days of Love Campaign, go to www.saddlebackresources.com (click on “campaigns”), or call 1800-SADDLEBACK.

The vision of Concerts of Prayer Greater New York is to see God heal broken cities through passionate leaders and culturally relevant churches. Our mission is to mobilize leaders into movements of united prayer, leadership development, church growth and global compassion.
