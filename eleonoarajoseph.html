There Is Hope!

by Dr. Eleonora Joseph

Paul and Silas were thrown into prison for preaching truth. There was a slave girl possessed by a spirit of divination. She was exploited by some men to earn money by fortune-telling. The demon possessed slave girl followed Paul and his companions when they were on their way to prayer. She continually shouted out who they were until, Paul spoke to the demon in her to set her free. Her owner’s hope of making money was destroyed, and they dragged Paul and Silas before the judges in the market place. A mob was formed against them, and they were beaten and thrown in jail. Around midnight Paul and Silas were having a prayer meeting, praising and singing hymns unto the Lord. Suddenly there was a great earthquake, the prison doors were shaken to the foundation, and Paul and Silas were freed by the power of the living God. (Acts 16:16-40). Are you in a situation where divine intervention is needed? THERE IS HOPE!

Hold on to the Word, get it into your spirit. God says it, that settles it. Hebrew 13:6 states, “So that we may boldly say, The Lord is my helper, I will not fear what man shall do unto me.” The flowers will fade, the violets may not bloom, the birds may not chirp, but God’s Word stands forever! His Word is our collateral. He is our Bond’s-man. He is our security. John 14:13 tells us: “And whatsoever ye shall ask in my name, that I will do, that the Father may be glorified in the Son.”

Seasoned Saints:  Because of the lack of knowing and believing that God is omnipotent, we in ourselves produce seed of doubts. Doubt produces fear. II Timothy 1:7 declares: “For God hath not given us the spirit of fear, but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind.” Our help is in Him alone. When we stay united with Him, we can draw strength and courage to move on. He is the vine, we are the branches, thus, we are dependent on Him. We sang an old chorus that says: “Without Him I can do nothing. Without Him I’d surely fail; Without Him I’d soon be drifting. Like a ship, without a sail.” Do you have some situations confronting you today? THERE IS HOPE! You can pray and praise God and He will make a way. Instead of feeling defeated and sorry for yourself, get a praise on your lips and bless His holy name. At your midnight hour, you ought to bless Him. He is with you at all times. Songs and praises at the midnight hour will take you through the storm. There is victory in your praise. This is a glorious day to be an over-comer. This is my word for you today taken from Isaiah 43:2: “When thou passest through the waters, I will be with thee; and through the rivers, they shall not overflow thee: when thou walkest through the fire, thou shalt not be burned; neither shall the flame kindle upon thee.”
