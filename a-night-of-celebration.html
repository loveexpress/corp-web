A Night of Celebration in New York City
with David Jeremiah & Friends

David Jeremiah, Matthew West, Brooklyn Tabernacle Singers, Nichole Nordeman & Uriel Vega.

David Jeremiah & Friends, Thursday, Dec. 4th, 7:30pm (Doors open: 6:30pm) @ Beacon Theatre in New York City. Musical Guests: Mat­thew West, Brooklyn Tabernacle Sin­gers, Nichole Nordeman and Uriel Vega. Don’t miss the opportunity to celebrate in New York City with David Jeremiah, #1 New York Times bestselling author and one of the most esteemed pastors in America. Come and experience the power of worship as David Jeremiah teaches from the Word of God. Join with hundreds of other believers through dynamic music with special guests:

Matthew West - American Contemporary Christian musician and winner of the 2013 American Music Award for Best Contemporary Inspirational Artist

Brooklyn Tabernacle Singers – Six-time Grammy Award winning legendary ensemble

Nichole Nordeman - American contemporary Christian singer–songwriter and winner of multiple GMA Dove Awards, including two awards for Female Vocalist of the Year.

Uriel Vega - Gospel/devotional/spiritual artist

Celebrate Christmas in New York with Dr. David Jeremiah and friends. Register early! Visit www.davidjeremiah.org/newyork
