Glamour Decorating is a family owned and operated company with over 25 years of experience. Glamour Decorating began its business as a wholesaler, selling solely within the trade. In 2003, we began selling to the public, offering our own in-home service. Our main objective was and will continue to be to provide our clients with the highest quality products and services at affordable prices. Because we manufacture all of our products in our own state of the art 16,000 square foot manufacturing facility, we are able to keep consumer costs down. Absolutely no job is subcontracted, which allows us to pay close attention to every one of our projects. Whether you choose custom-made window treatments, bedspreads, re-upholstery, or fa­bric covers, Glamour Deco- rating has the staff and the tools to execute the job in a professional manner. We have an extremely knowled­geable and innovative staff of decorators who will add style and flair to your church and home. We assure you that you will receive the utmost personal service. The decorator that starts your project will be the one who finishes it. Being a client of Glamour Decorating gives you exclusive access to our thousands of fabrics from damasks, jacquards, plaids, prints, sheers, and silk. We have all of the proper materials to make your windows look beautiful and unique.
