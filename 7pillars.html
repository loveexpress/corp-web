7 Pillars of a Legacy Minded Man

by Joe Pellegrino & Jack Redmond

The statistics are staggering. Men and manhood are under attack. High divorce rates, substance abuse and pornography are rampant in our society and even in the church. The numbers are even worse for young men who grow up without a strong father in their lives. When you look at these realities, something must be done. Joe Pellegri­no and Jack Redmond are rea­dy to engage, equip and encourage men with The 7 Pillars of a Legacy Minded Man.

These statistics don’t even begin to capture many of the questions and issues that good men face in their daily lives. How do I live my purpose? How do I really live and achieve my priorities? How do I tap into God’s Po­wer to live a great life? The 7 Pillars of Legacy Minded Man tackle these and many other tough questions that men have.

The 7 Pillars of a Le­ga­cy Minded Man is a game plan to help men win in life! When men choose to follow Christ, He becomes their winning foundation. Then it’s time to build a great life focused on The 7 Pillars of a Legacy Minded Man and build a le­gacy for generations to come!

In the tradition of Promise Keepers and other Men’s Ministries, Legacy Minded Men strives to reach men and attack the issues that they face head on. Pellegrino and Redmond write in a clear, practical style that the average guy can relate to in a way that they can benefit both immediately, and for years to come.

Many men enter manhood without proper preparation from their biological fathers, society and even the church. This leaves many men struggling to survive either in their marriages, financially or as fathers. Even when men choose to follow Christ, this is no guarantee of them having the practical skills and spiritual strength for success.

In addition to this new book to help men grow, Le­gacy Minded Men offers ma­ny opportunities from men’s breakfasts, small group discipleship, prayer calls and regional conferences. You can learn more about getting connected at www.legacy mindedmen.org. Upcoming “Man Conferences” will be held on Saturday, May 17th at Calvary Temple in Wayne, NJ and on June 14th at Be­thel Gospel Tabernacle in Queens, NY.

In their previous writings, Joe Pellegrino co-authored Safe at Home which chronicled how personal faith in Christ helped many of Major League Baseball’s faithful to navigate life in the public spotlight. Jack Redmond in previous books, People Matter to God, Wounded Heart, Infusion and God Belongs in My City writes in a practical and powerful style designed to help people live victoriously through their faith in Jesus Christ.
