Viewpoint: I Am Better Off Today - Spiritually

By Rose Acker Lictro

Since Jesus came into my heart,

Since Jesus came into my heart,

Floods of joy O’er my soul

like the seal billows roll,

Since Jesus came into my heart.

Say this out loud with me “Spiritually, I am better off today since I first accepted Jesus as my personal Saviour. Do I remember the day, month, and year and where I was. What state of mind I was in; Did my body need healing. Did I need a job. Did I need a place to sleep. Did I finally recognize that I needed Jesus in my life! 1 John 1:8-9; John 3:17’

Am I better off in the church I attend today. Do I receive my Pastor as a gift from God and esteem him highly. Do I pray for my Pastor and his family. Do I receive my Pastors’ teaching and obey his word. Am I better off when I follow my Pastor’s example. Ephesians 4:11; 1 Thessalonians 5:13 & 25; 1 Corin- thians 11:2; 1 Corinthians 4:16 & 11:11; Hebrews 13:17

I am better off today when I pray without ceasing and with alertness exercising my spiritual authority, discerning and dealing with evil influences, consecrating and dedicating my home to God.

Establishing a Godly heritage, resolving family tensions and dealing with attitudes of resentment, anger and bitterness before the devil gets an opportunity to deal with me, confronting ungodly values and removing all desecration; filling my home with objects and activities that bring glory to God.

I am better off today when it is time to fight for the blessings of God for my nation, leaders, church, family and friends.

I thank you Father that as a watchman upon the walls of America and Isaiah 61:6-11 and as your servant, I am a holy servant, the redeemed of the Lord that America is not a nation forsaken.

How do you view the world’s future spiritually! Will our world ....stay the same? Get worse? Get better?

WHAT THE BIBLE SAYS: “God will wipe out every tear from their eyes and death will be no more, neither will mourning nor outcry, nor pain be any more. The former things are passed away. Revelations 21:3-4.

WHAT THAT CAN MEAN FOR YOU:

• Meaningful and satisfying work. Isaiah 65:21-23

• No more sickness or suffering, Isaiah 25:8; 33:24.

• A happy, unending life with family and friends. Psalm 37:11, 29.

CAN WE REALLY BELIEVE WHAT THE BIBLE SAYS? YES!

God has the ability to fulfill the promise. Revelations 15:3 and Matthew 19:26.

• God has the desire to fulfill the promise. Job 14:14-15; Mark 1:40-41; John 14:9.

So we can be sure that both the Father and Jesus want to help us to enjoy a happy future! Psalm 72:12-14; Psalm 145:16; 2 Peter 3:9

THINK ABOUT THIS: How will God change our world for the better? The answer Matthew 6:9-10 and Daniel 2:44.

GOD BLESS

ROSE
