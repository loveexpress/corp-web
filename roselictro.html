Viewpoint: March for Jesus!! ---worldwide

By Rose Acker Lictro

Believers can you ima­gine a Worldwide March for Jesus. Coming together from every nation, country, and across oceans, to march. Millions of believers carrying banners, flags of their country, sing­ing, playing bands and signs proclaiming JESUS is LORD. Do you think it can take off like a worldwide New Years Eve celebration?

So believers, would you MARCH for JESUS. Well I did and many others did years ago in New York City.

Remember now! Chur­ches, Pastors and believers all got together carrying their flags and banners, playing bands, T-shirts and hats (MFJ) and we marched up Madison Avenue to Central Park. What a “Spiritual March;” We all had smiles and tears on our faces. People said, “this is so Unbelievable, yes Believerable to Believers in Christ Jesus.

Pastor Stranges, Brother-Inlaw to Rose Acker Lictro

Jesus’ March into Jerusalem

Preparing to leave Be­thany, Jesus sent two disciples to Bethphage to fetch Him an ass, thus would His entry into Jerusalem fulfill the prophecy foretelling the coming of the King. He would be poor and riding on an ass. Surrounded by the people of Bethany, He began the Journey to Jerusalem, the excitement mounted rapidly as the procession approached the gate and crowds poured out to meet them. Palm tree branches were ripped down and throne on the road before Him and the crowds shouted their praise and cried Hosanna; Blessed is the King of Israel that cometh in the Name of the Lord. (Matt 21:1-11; Mark 11:1-11; Luke 19:28-40; John 12:12-19).

Love ones, let us reflect on “Jesus” march into Je­ru­salem.” This Palm Sunday leads to Jesus “Last Supper, Agony in the Garden, His Arrest, The Crucifixion, Dying on the Cross, His Resurrection, His Ascension”. Amen.
