Africa Prays For America

Mark your calendars now and plan to join us in prayers of repentance on behalf of America Saturday, October 16th from 10am-6pm in Washington DC!

Beginning at 10am on Sat, Oct. 16th, we will gather in prayer asking the Lord to move on behalf of America. Prayer sessions will be led by dynamic ministry leaders from many regions of Africa like Pastor (Dr.) William F. Kumuyi, Jackson Senyonga, Bishop Emmanuel Nohu Kure, Brother Enoch Agbozo, Bishop Isaac Ogbeta, Rev Francisca Zinyemba, Pastor J.O. Owoeye. American Ministry Leaders will include Rev. Billy Wilson, Dr. Bob Bakke, Dr. T. L. Lowery, Rev. Dr. Robert Stearns & many other special guests!

Strategically set at the US Capitol, this event is being led by the African Strategic Leadership Prayer Network (ASLPN), a non-profit Christian charitable organization dedicated to prayer and intercession. African Christian Leaders from around the world will come together in unity and in obedience to the Word of God to release the anointing of prayer and bring people and nations back towards righteousness.

This day is about gathering the united body of Christ to seek repentance and exalt His name over our families, communities, and nation. 50,000 intercessors are expected to unite in prayer for America. Please begin now to prepare your heart & join us in a 24 hour prayer calendar through October 16.

We will alternate speakers, prayer and worship NONSTOP on Saturday from 10am-6pm. The event is free and open to the public. Join us and bring your friends and family!

We are encouraging as many as possible to fast the three days prior (October 13th, 14th, 15th) as we are truly seeing this as a time to fulfill Esther’s call on behalf of America.

“For if thou altogether holdest thy peace at this time, then shall there enlargement and deliverance arise to the Jews from another place but thou and thy father’s house shall be destroyed; and who knoweth whether thou art come to the kingdom for such a time as this?” Esther 4:14

A video featuring the story behind ASLPN and Africa Prays for America can be seen on YouTube at http://bit.ly/aSVh1A and on Facebook at http://bit.ly/bI5p5z

There are many ways to get involved including: actively praying before, during and after the event; volunteering; organizing and mobilizing Christians to attend; sharing information to your Christian network; Encouraging people to wear an event t-shirt or texting a donation e.g.: “AFRICA PRAYS 10” to 27138

To register for the Free event, visit us at the ASLPN website at http://aslpn.org/ or call our offices at 1-877-29-ASLPN (877-292-7576). Email can also be sent to info@aslpn.org or donna@aslpn.org
