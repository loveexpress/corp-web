Day of Prayer
For the Peace of Jerusalem

Join with millions of people around the Globe to Pray for the Peace of Je­ru­salem on October 4, 2009, the First Sunday in October.

Over 1000 international Christian leaders are rallying millions of believers from many church traditions to unite to prayer, setting aside the first Sunday of every October as the "Day of Prayer for the Peace of Jerusalem," praying for God's promise of peace to be realized for all inhabitants of this historic city. Tens of thousands of pastors from every continent are mobilizing prayer for the peace of Jerusalem in their local churches, regions, and countries. You are invited to get connected by visiting www. daytopray.com.

Get Involved

1.Visit www.daytopray.com to connect to the online global community who are interceding "for Zion's sake." Be sure to sign up to receive the "Jerusalem's Watchmen Prayer Update" via email.

2. Become better informed to pray for and speak out about Israel, through the Watchmen on the Wall Training Resources. See www.kairosrersourcecenter .com to order.

3. Participate in a Watch­men Training Seminar near you. Visit www.daytopray. com.

4. Mobilize your church, and commit to becoming educated in your prayers for the peace of Jerusalem. Be sure to encourage your pastor to help co-Chairman Rev. Ro­bert Stearns and Dr. Jack Hayford trumpet this call to every local body on the pla­net.

You do not want to miss this strategic opportunity to join with believers around the globe to pray for God's peace for all who live in Je­rusalem!
