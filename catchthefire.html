Catch the Fire

Catch the Fire is a international family of churches & ministries that was birthed as a result of the incredible revival that began in Toronto in 1994.

Led by John & Carol Arnott, the church (formerly Toronto Airport Christian Fellowship) came to the world’s attention as a place where God was meeting His people. Seventeen years later He continues to pour out His blessing at Catch the FIre events held worldwide.

John & Carol and their team desire to reach the world with Jesus through the sharing of their values, equipping people and releasing them to bring revival to their nations. They are passionately committed to walking in God’s love and giving it away until the whole earth is filled with knowledge of the Glory of the Lord.

Special Guest Pastor Surprise Sithole has been a part of the world outreach team. He is experiencing extraordinary creative miracles in his meetings. People Have Been Raised Up From Their Death Beds!

This conference will emphasize the great love of God to change lives and experience miraculous signs and wonders.
