AllAroundTheTown

by Dr. D

What do you get when you cross a man with 50 years of preaching, singing, writing and producing song and a church with 40 years of community service? You get a 2 day anointing and spiritual awakening revival at Miracle Temple with Bishop “Mr. Clean” White as your evangelist. “The Bible doesn’t call us to be Baptist, Pentecostal, Catholic or any other denomination; it calls us to be Holy” states Bishop White at the start of his message. “We all need to take a blood bath” he continues.

His text was Isaiah 37:14-29, as he expounds on how Hezekiah, when receiving the threating letter on his life, family, and kingdom, spreads the letter on the altar of God and relied on God to take care of it… and He did by sending an angel to take care of Hezekiah’s enemy.

Miracle Temple was founded in June 1, 1969 by Bishop Caecer Gooding and Evangelist Josephine Gooding with 26 members. In the next 11 months, God blessed Miracle Temple to expand its ministry and move to its present location at 1555 Macombs Road in the Bronx. By 1991, then Elder Ceacer was ordained by the Late Bishop F.D. Washington.

Today, after 40 years of ministry, a Radio broadcast that lasted 20 years, and Bishop Ceacer taking ill, their youngest son, Superintendent Jay Allen Gooding, Sr. was installed as pastor, Evangelist Josephine Gooding continues as co-pastor. As Bishop Mr Clean expounded from God’s word, The blessing from God flowed on both Pastor Jay Gooding and Evange. Josephine Gooding of Fellowship Tabernacle COGIC and Miracle Temple.
