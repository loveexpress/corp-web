The Fuel Movement

Fuel Leadership Training 2015

The Fuel Movement is growing and expanding in 2015 with The Fuel Training in two locations this year in New Jersey and in New York. The Fuel Leadership Trainings are open for all Youth Pastors, Senior Pastors and Student Leaders across the Greater NY Region, and will be held on Saturday, February 21st in Paterson, New Jersey from 9am-2pm at Harvest Outreach Ministry and Saturday, May 2nd in Brooklyn, New York from 9am-2pm at Parkslope Christian Tabernacle.

This is a great opportunity to learn, grow and be empowered by some great voices to this generation; Guest Speakers Todd Crews from Brooklyn Tabernacle, Danny Sanabria from God Belongs in My City Movement, Jacob Burgei from The Fuel Movement and Alex McCormick from The Kingdom Alliance. We had over 200 in attendance last year and it is a great way for us to re-connect, celebrate and encourage other ministries in their growth and build lasting relationship with leaders for the Kingdom.

Seats are limited at each location and advanced registration is only $15 which includes all sessions and lunch as well. You can purchase tickets online at www.fuelconf.com.
