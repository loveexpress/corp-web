Men’s Discipleship Network

by Rev. A. R. Bernard, Sr.

Never before in the history of our nation has the church & family been so threatened. The culture's assault on Biblical Manhood has confused God's original design. We have reached epidemic proportions where society is now having a greater effect on the Christian than the Christian is having on society.

The stats confirm this diagnosis: 90% of Christian men surveyed do not be­lieve in the totality of the Bible. A 45% Christian di­vorce rate affects one million children each year, producing countless emotional widows and spiritual or­phans.

There is good news though, we have a God who is still in control and specializes in turning darkness into light. This same God is calling men back to Himself. Men who say we've had enough, we're ready for change...

Join the NY Churches and their Men at This Year's Men's Discipleship Boot Camp entitled "The Seven Marks of Maximized Man­hood," Saturday, March 29th, 8am-1pm (Continental Breakfast included-Re­gis­tration) with special guests including Keynote Speaker, Dr. A. R. Bernard, Pastor Steve Milazzo, Pastor Jim­my Jack, Pas­tor Rich An­derson, Pastor Craig Hol­liday & Coach Scott Cae­sar. Registration Required. Tickets:$20; $25 at the door.

Venue: Smithtown Gospel Tabernacle, 1 Higbie Drive, Smithtown, NY 11787.
