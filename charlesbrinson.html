A Macedonia Call

by Bishop Charles B. Brinson

As we stand in the night of despair, we the Brinson Memorial Church family and Pastor are making a Macedonia call (please, come help us.)

On Dec. 9, 2008, 11pm, the adversary launched an attack of arson, against the church, resulting in heavy damage throughout the entire structure. Insurance coverage for the building had lapsed, rendering us in the place of need. From the ashes we shall rise! The Work of the Lord must continue.

Pursuant to Acts 16:9-10, a man from Macedonia appeared to the Apostle Paul in a vision with the plea; "Come over in Macedonia and help us." It is with the spirit of humility that we solicit your prayers and financial support in this dark hour.

Please continue to pray for our congregation and consider donating a monetary contribution. Please send all correspondence to the attention of Brinson Memorial Church, Inc., P.O. Box 2, Florence, NJ 08518.

All charitable donations are tax deductible. Thanking you I am, Bishop Charles B. Brinson, Pastor, Brinson Memorial Church, 200-204 Brinton Avenue, Trenton, New Jersey 08618. Telephone: 609-847-0001.
