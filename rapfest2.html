Rap Fest 2014

Rap Fest has released the confirmed artist list for Rap Fest 2014. Rap Fest 2014 will take place at VIDALIA PARK located at E. 180th Street and Daly Avenue in The Bronx, NY, from 12:00PM to 8:00PM on Saturday August 9th.

Confirmed artists include:

  • The Storytellas (NY)
  • 412 Brothers (TX)
  • Ali Rozet (WA)
  • Angie Rose (NY)
  • Bridge B (CO)
  • Cellus (GA)
  • Cypha Ixous (NY)
  • D-Will (NY)
  • DAY (CT)
  • Heesun Lee (NY)
  • Infinite Automatik (NY)
  • K-Drama (OH)
  • Red Dott (MD)
  • Righteouz Knight (NJ)
  • Rkitect (FL)
  • Swinn Da Example (NJ)
  • Th3 Saga (NY)
  • The Few (LIVE BAND) (DE)
  • Tragic Hero (NJ)
  • Whuteva (NY)
  • Young Joshua (PA)
  • DJ BreakBeat Lou (NY)
  • DJ NuMan (NY)
  • DJ Tranzformed (NY)
  • Dynamic Rockers (NY)

Rap Fest 2014 features 21 rap ministries, break-dancers, multiple DJs, and 8 Hours of non-stop Gospel hip hop music, all for 1 God, Jesus Christ. The goal of Rap Fest is to evangelize communities through hip hop music and offer them an opportunity to trust in God as a solution to problems.

Rap Fest 2014 sponsors include: The Sanctuary Fellowship, New Birth New York, God Belongs In My City, Sphere of Hip Hop, Grateful Apparel, Nyack College, Adorando Con Flow, Jam the Hype, C1 Radio & Holy Culture.
