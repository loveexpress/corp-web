Hillsong Conference

Pastor Brian Houston

For the first time ever Hillsong Confe­rence is coming to the Uni­ted States of America in 2013. Pastors Brian and Bobbie Hous­ton will be hosting this conference to­gether with Christine Caine, Carl Lentz, Joel Houston, Reuben Mor­gan, Hillsong UNITED and Hillsong Live in New York. Hillsong Con­ference exists to champion the cause of local churches everywhere. Friday night and all day Saturday, Hill­song Conference will be in New York City Oct. 4th & 5th at Radio City Music Hall located in the heart of Roc­kefeller Ctr., 1260 6th Ave­nue (Avenue of the Ame­ricas) (Betw W. 50th St. & W. 51st St.), NY, NY 10020.

For more information about visit www.hillsong conference.com/usa.
