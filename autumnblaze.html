Autumn Blaze 2009

Back To Our Roots

by Raphael Giglio

I am pleased to announce that Zarephath Christian Church is welcoming the return of Autumn Blaze on Saturday, Oct. 31st, 1pm-8pm, this year to the Zarephath Fields. After a long run as one of the area's premier arena concert-outreaches, Autumn Blaze is returning to the fields of Zarephath where it all started. Rob Cruver and I began this annual event while we were still teenagers in the early 80's. At that time it did not have a name or reputation. It consisted of me with my guitar, Rob with his Bible, some donuts and cider and a few dozen of our friends for a hayride and bonfire with some worship and the Word. Over time it developed into a multi-faceted, large-scale event that grew to over 7,000 participants before it moved to the Meadowlands Arena where it topped off at nearly 14,000.

Through the years it featured bands like; The Newsboys, Third Day, Jars of Clay, Toby Mac & many others along with popular speakers and sports celebrities. The event saw approximately 10,000 young people make commitments to Christ since it began and has paved the way for many other large-scale concert outreaches in the NY/NJ area.

When I became Executive Director in 2006 it had already become a major arena outreach-concert and involved over 1,200 churches in our region. Our ministry partner STAR99.1-FM has featured it as the premier autumn event for Christian music in their listening area and it has been mentioned in many major newspapers, magazines and internet sites. An event of this magnitude required much planning, hundreds of volunteers and a tremendous budget to present each year.

This year's Autumn Blaze is not about numbers or big names. We'll most likely have neither. What excites me is that we have the opportunity to return to our roots and present a Harvest Event that features; the sorely-missed hayride and bonfire, children's attractions and activities, food, and vendors. It will be a perfect Halloween alternative and will present all-day live worship music from area church bands and a special message by Pastor Rob Cruver. One of the best parts about Autumn Blaze '09 is that we will offer all of this for Free. There is no admission charge, just a $5 per carload parking fee. Our goal is to welcome back youth groups, past Autumn Blaze attendees, and new guests who want to experience the excitement of this beloved event. We also hope to invite many friends and families from around New Jersey who we could potentially minister to all year round at Zarephath Christian Church. While we don't have the big-time persona of an arena-size concert that we've grown accustomed to over the past few years, we are thrilled to offer a Free Outdoor Worship and Outreach Event complete with a bonfire and hayride along with the fun and excitement of a Christian family Harvest Festival.

Autumn Blaze, Saturday, Oct. 31st, 1-8pm, Free Admission. Zarephath Christian Church, 10 Chapel Drive, Zarephath, NJ. Call 1-800-GO-BLAZE; visit www.autumnblaze.org.
