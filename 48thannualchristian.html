48th Annual Christian Ministries Convention

Our slogan “Empowered By Christ to Help Others Finish Well” rightly describes New Jersey Christian Ministries (NJCM). Our purpose is to equip Christians for bringing people to a saving knowledge of Jesus Christ and then to help them mature in Christ by learning to apply God’s Word. This is done through an annual Convention, which for the last 25 years has been hosted by Hawthorne Gospel Church in Hawthorne, NJ. This year the Convention is October 24-25.

One hundred workshops and 6 panel discussions are presented by 80+ instructors who are mostly clergy, have PhD degrees, or lead their own ministries. Almost every area of Christian ministry is covered. Over the last three years attendance has ranged between 800-900 adults and between 300-400 teens at our Free Teen Rally on Friday night. When our exhibitors, ranging between 60-70 different organizations, and our 80+ instructors are included, we host about 1500 people.

The Convention is a real unique Christian event! We like to say it is “a little bit of heaven.” We are interdenominational so we have churches and ministries represented from all over the Body of Christ. Christians get to learn, worship, and fellowship with people with whom they would not normally interact. We’ve had between 140-160 different churches or ministries represented - mostly from NJ but usually about 40 churches from NY attend. Many senior pastors and associates attend as well.

There’s also a General Session with a keynote speaker and worship leader. This year we have Jennifer Sands, a gifted and inspiritional author and speaker, a 9/11 widow, who has appeared on the 700 Club, the Home Life Magazine Cover Story, Aspiring Women, The Coral Ridge Hour, and Strength For Living. Our worship leader is Carl Brister. He’s what you get when you combine a modern day minister, with modern day music, and the word of God delivered in modern day language? His latest CD, Focus, comes on the heels of his 2000 debut album, The Journey, which received national attention, receiving both radio and television airplay.

And it’s affordable for EVERYONE. Comparable conventions cost from $100 up. But for us the price for attending both days has stayed at $37 for years ($32 for the Early Bird). The Lord continues to bless us as we minister to others!

To register or get more information call 1-800-363-4410 or go to www.ministriesconvention.com
