AllAroundTown

by Ms. Gee

The Praise ship has landed on the new and spacious TV station owned by TBN. It is so wonderful to have a Christian TV station in NYC. This will give churches the opportunity to spread the Gospel on television, where we will be able to hear and look and be a part of the bigger picture.

The new 200 seat studio at Union Square is in an area of little theatres and studios. The hand of Jan Crouch the wife of Paul Crouch, founder of TBN was seen throughout the studio. The elegance and detail are a trade mark of her outstanding work on every studio throughout the world. Its been a long time coming almost 25 years, but the time is just right. It's Gods timing, a mighty harvest has been declared and proclaimed over the tri-state area. The Christian media will always be right there bringing you the Good News. The studio plans to be running at the end of January and the beginning of February with live programs of area pastors. Chris Elia station manager declared, "New York City has always been on the heart of Jan & Paul.”

TBN hosted the opening night by presenting their latest movie called Noelle. A story of forgiveness and repentance and to remind us that Jesus still forgives and forgets. Edith Stranges and I had the pleasure to sit down with Matt Crouch on the opening night, who happens to be one of the sons of the founder of TBN.

Matt is the CEO of Gener8Xion Entertainment has produced in the past such films, as “China Cry,” “The Omega Code,” “The Champion,” “Megiddo” and most recently “One Night With The King.” Noelle is the first film that they acquired after it completion, then re-edited and made ready for theatrical released. “Noelle is the most faith-filled yet non-churchy, non-Christian-ese film we’ve ever seen. Matt expressed the importance of good programming where the family can sit and enjoy. He was asked how TBN came to be located in NY. He said "it was a long time coming with problems that come with trying to open up a tv studio." He is grateful that we now have this station in NYC.

Matt talked about a dream he had about a child his wife and him had lost. When he read the play written by David Wall it brought to his remembrance this dream and how God gives you another child not to replace the one you lost, but to take their rightful place in your lives.

FROM THE CHRISTIAN COMMUNITY,
WELCOME TBN TO THE NORTHEAST.
