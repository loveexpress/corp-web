Bizarro World

A Student Ministries Conference

If statistics are truly a mirror to the condition of our world today, then the reflection is simply terrifying. But as the culture continues to decline, arising is a remnant generation with audacious passion to see their world return to its intended position. They are a peculiar people who look, think, speak, love, & live distinctly different, in fact, you could say they are down right bizarre...and they're all coming together for two days in NYC. Will you be among them?

Join youth leaders and students from the Tri-State area on Jan. 29-30th, 2010 to encourage and learn from one another through conversation, interactive learning experiences, ministry training and worship. Featuring Harvey Carey, Adam Durso, & Greg Steir and 16 other seasoned leaders on how to make the maximum impact with this generation through leadership, discipleship, worship and outreach.

Guest Worship Leaders Hezekiah Walker, Canton Jones & Kari Jobe. Hear from local and national leaders such as Dr. A.R. Bernard, Ron Luce, Bo Boshers @ Christian Cultural Center, 120-20 Flatlands Avenue, Brooklyn, NY. Register Today (Only $30/ $40 at the Door). For more information visit www.upsidedowninc.com. Limited Spaces Available.

The Bizarro World Conference is presented by Upsidedown Inc. & Willow Creek Association in partnership with BattleCry / Teen Mania.
