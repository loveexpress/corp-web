Vision For New York City

by Jay Fordice

It started with one small group of Latino leaders and a simple invitation. They were eager to have Luis Palau and his team help lead an evangelistic effort reaching the Tri-State area. Within a few more meetings, the group of interested leaders grew exponentially spanning all five boroughs along with parts of New Jersey, Connecticut, and Long Island.

At the same time, Kevin Palau, LPA’s President, took part in ongoing discussions with leaders in New York Ci­ty. They discussed ways to accelerate the ongoing Gos­pel Movement that was already underway.

Their vision? To see a greater level of unity,colla­boration, and partnership among churches and Gospel or organizations while incorporating service, prayer, church planting, and evangelism.

By God’s grace, and the prompting of several local leaders, these two streams came together. Latino, An­glo, Korean, Caribbean, and African-American leaders actively considered ways to bring their ideas together and unite in one large-scale effort. With that, NY CityServe & CityFest was born.

God is on the Move in New York City

There is a movement underway in NYC. It has gained traction and produced enduring results. Because of this movement, Church planting has become a major priority, with 100 new church plants in Manhattan alone!

Other healthy signs of this Gospel Movement continue to reveal God’s work in NYC. For instance, the evangelical population is growing in the urban core. This is due, in part, to millions of new immigrants who are bringing a strong Christian faith to the city. Collaboration among denominations and ethnic groups is also growing in strength and participation. As a result, civic and corporate leaders are working with churches to actively build bridges of cooperation in their communities, renewing their commitment to service in unprecedented ways.

Much groundwork has already been laid in prayer, faith, and service. Today, New York’s spiritual environment is ripe for a new movement of God’s people. And many see NY CityServe & CityFest as the catalyst. Elements of the Campaign include:

NY CityServe —A long-term, citywide movement that identifies, develops, and nurtures kingdom collaborations around the areas of justice, mercy, and education throughout the Tri-State area. These church-connected, neighborhood-focused ser­vice initiatives will mobilize thousands of individuals and accelerate the impact of what God is doing. We’re excited to have the leadership of Chris Troy (Director of NY CityServe), Robert Guerrero (Redeemer City to City) and Althea Taylor (Bronx Betha­ny Church of the Nazarene) overseeing this long-term vision.

Regional Outreaches — A three-month-long evangelistic initiative focused on saturating the Tri-State area with the Good News of Jesus Christ. Including more than 150 evangelistic gatherings, every borough, region, and neighborhood will have the opportunity to prepare and plan their own localized outreaches, large and small. Luis Palau, Andrew Palau, and other partner evangelists will be available to help share the Good News at each event.

CityFest with Luis Palau — A free, multicultural festival (in Spanish and English) in June/July 2015. This fun, family friendly festival will be focused on worship, clear Gospel proclamation, and celebrating the work accomplished through CityServe.

Imagine the Impact

Hundreds of churches and nonprofits from across the New York City metro area are already coming together to love and serve their area’s in a historic and sustainable way. Imagine what will be accomplished as we help them to increase collaboration among civic, corporate, and church leaders, which span across ethnic, economic, and denominational lines.

LPA is helping to establish sustainable partnerships, unique to each borough, region, and neighborhood, as we push toward greater levels of action and community involvement for the greater good of their region. Imagine all the regional and neighborhood efforts to share the Good News through multiple evangelistic gatherings, and culminating in one large multicultural evangelistic festival in the heart of the city with world evangelist Luis Palau. Just think thousands of lives, families, and communities can be transformed by the power of the Gospel.

This is the vision of the New York City Initiative...and your opportunity to change lives for eternity...in the most influential city in the world.

A City of Influence

New York City is among the most influential cities in the world. History has shown that great spiritual and social movements birthed in New York City have long-term influence on the rest of the nation and the world.

NY CityServe & CityFest is our opportunity to energize the Christian community, lift up the name of Jesus Christ, and mobilize tens of thousands of individuals to serve the city and proclaim the Good News. LPA will help hundreds of local churches as they demonstrate Christ’s unconditional love and compassion for the city through an ongoing, sustained Gos­pel Movement.
