City Action Coalition

Presents A Night of Prayer, Worship & Empowerment to Transform NYC & Beyond

Tuesday, Sept. 18 from 7:30-9:30pm at Bethel Gospel Tabernacle, 110-25 Guy R. Brewer Blvd., Jamaica, NY.

The evangelical church is being ignited like never before to pray, to speak truth to power, nurture the next generation of world-changers and to influence the political and social spheres of the marketplace. The evangelical church for too long has been divided and unable to influence the political arena despite the fact that we have 1.5 million Bible-believing Christians in NYC. Finally a strong multi-ethnic and cross-denominational voice is arising. Come be a part of this historic event!

This gathering is for pastors, marketplace leaders and emerging leaders who want to unite in prayer for our nation and learn how they can be salt and light to their communities.

For more information go to www.cityactioncoalitioninternational.org
