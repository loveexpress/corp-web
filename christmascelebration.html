Christmas Celebration - Dec. 1st-16th

Evangel Multi Media & Arts Center presents The most Spectacular Broadway-Style Musical Production in the Washington DC, Maryland, Virginia, Pennsylvania and Delaware Metroplex Christmas Celebration 2012 December 1st-16th featuring eight of the top gospel artists in the world. Mary Mary, BeBe & CeCe Winans, Marvin Sapp, Wess Morgan, Tye Tribbett & Kiki Sheard. Cast of over 200, Live Animals, Laser Light Show, Amazing Special Effects, and Brand New Scenes. Venue: Evangel Cathedral, 13901 Central Avenue, Upper Marlboro, Maryland 20774. Host: Senior Pastor Bishop Don Meares.

For more information please call 301-249-9400 or you can visit us at online at www.evangelchristmascelebration.com
