FEATURES

Helen Wilk Ministries

Prayer & Prophetic Conference

Prophetess Helen Wilk

Greetings Brethren in the name of the Lord, the name which is above every name in heaven and on earth! May the grace and peace of God be toward you this day and may His blessings and mercy overflow.

I believe the Lord is calling us together in this hour as we have not quite heard in this manner in times past. The urgency is a little different than we have experienced and the call to come together is much stronger. This is precisely what this July 2010 Kingdom Connection Prayer and Prophetic Conference at “The Word of Life Ministries” in Freeport, Long Island, NY is all about. God is and has begun to connect the Kingdom in a very profound and supernatural way and at a rate of speed which is almost breathtaking, by which our natural eye is unable to discern.

During this very timely gathering, I believe the Lord will speak to us corporately and individually about times and seasons, strategies and plans and give us clear concise guidance for the days, months and years that we are facing ahead of us.

This is an hour in which the church should neither be afraid nor retreat but press in ‘together.’ Eph. 4:15-16 “But speaking the truth in love, may grow up into Him in all things, which is the head, even Christ: from whom “the whole body fitly joined together and compacted by that which every joint supplieth, according to the effectual working in the measure of every part, maketh increase of the body unto the edifying of itself in love.”

God is calling us to build the kingdom, to be connected (united), to have a sure prophetic foundation, exercising prophetic understanding and cautions, and dominion. We are on the brink of a prophetic breakthrough that will bring us into our prophetic “destiny.“ Sons and daughters of the King, this is all part of His great and glorious plan. There’s never been a better time to be saved and be in the kingdom than now. Oh, to those that have eyes to see and ears to hear what the sprit is saying… won’t you come and join us Friday, July 30th, 7pm & Saturday, July 31st, 9am-4:30pm, at Word of Life Ministry, 80 West Merrick Road, Freeport, NY.

For conference registration information call 1-631-588-7704 / 4672 Fax or email hcwilk@optonline.net. Address: Helen Wilk Ministries, 440 Furrows Road, Holbrook, NY 11741.
