Arise & Walk Ministries Foundation

by Dr. Mayra Suero-Wade

Thanksgiving Benefit Awards Dinner with Rev. Dr. Lester Taylor. Minister of Music Alvin Slaughter. Master of Ceremony Rev. Clifton Davis. Honorees Bishop Joel & Doris Jeune, Stan Neron & Dr. David Ireland.

The 2010 Thanksgiving Benefit Awards Dinner is just around the corner! Join us as we welcome Dove & Stellar Award Nominee, Recording Artist Alvin Slaughter and Rev. Clifton Davis, who will be our Master of Ceremony. The program also features an elegant, gourmet three-course dinner and awards program.

This very special event will be held on Friday, November 19, 2010. The banquet will take place from 7pm-12Midnight at the Marriott Glenpointe Hotel, 100 Frank W. Burr Blvd. in Teaneck, NJ. Ticket prices are $150 for adults and $100 for children under 16. The Founder Rev. Dr. Mark J. Wade, President/CEO of AWMF along with his wife Mayra Suero-Wade, D.D.S. are the hosts.

Arise & Walk Ministries Foundation was born as a result of extraordinary experience, of witnessing a drastic need and perceiving it not as an insurmountable problem, but as an opportunity to serve. The vision to fulfill that opportunity and the mission to serve are the subjects of this profile.

Mark J. Wade, MD, Wade Healthcare Systems, Inc. (WHS) is a physician owned & operated healthcare management and Consultation Company. In 1996, WHS was founded by Mark J. Wade, M.D. after more than 13 years of healthcare leadership and management service to inner city healthcare institutions. WHS’ philosophy is that quality comprehensive healthcare can be delivered to every segment of our population in a cost-effective manner, regardless of the economic or health status of the target group.

Over the past 9 years, WHS has established a number of healthcare contracts including a pediatric primary care delivery contract with New York City’s foster care agencies and a contract to provide Medical Issues Training Services to social service, legal and medical personnel in the New York child welfare systems. We have also established healthcare management consultative contracts with New York & New Jersey healthcare providers.

Dr. Mayra Suero-Wade D.D.S., M.P.H. In addition to her dental career in New York Dr. Suero-Wade travels to third-world countries providing free dental care through the Arise and Walk Ministries Foundation she established with her husband. She also writes an oral health column.
