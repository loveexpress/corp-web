Misfit Conference 2015

August 21st- 22nd

Misfit Conference Friday-Saturday, August 21st-22nd, (Fri:4pm; Sat:9am) with Jentezen Franklin, Chad Veach, Rich Wilkerson Jr. and Chris Durso. Voices: Be­thel Music, Social Club, Misfit NYC Republic, Mis­fit Rap Society. Live W/O Fear. Location: Salvation Army Centen­nial Memo­rial Theatre, 120 West 14th Street, NY, NY 10011. Pas­tor Michael & Maria Dur­so. This a gathering of all people who identify themselves as Mis­fits. Those who are uncomfortable with their surroundings and who seem to be disturbingly different from others. This conference is a meeting place for all leaders, young and old, to help affirm and discover their calling.

For more information call (718)386-8882 or visit www.misfit.nyc/misfitthe conference2015.
