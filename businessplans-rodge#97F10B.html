"Business Plans to Prosper with God"

by Gary Scarano
www.ReachOUT.tv

"Yielding Big Dividends in Business and in Your Spiritual Life”

Rodger Parker (rparker58@optonline.net) is a senior level executive with over thirty years experience managing multiple operations on a worldwide basis. He has managed 20 different general administrative departments responsible for operating budgets over $300 million. He presently heads up a consulting firm (www.VanHoutenParker.com), and is on the Board of Concerts of Prayer, NY; on the Advisory Board of ReachOUT.tv, and has advised other Christian organizations.

Some are saying that dividends are a thing of the past. But I do not believe that. In Jeremiah 29:11-12 God says “For I know the plans I have for you, plans to prosper and not to harm you…then you will call upon me and pray to me, and I will listen to you.” We need a plan for life. By seeking God and His plans for our life and then developing a strategic business plan based upon God’s direction we can be successful. We need to understand: Where We Are; Where We Want To Be; and How We Can Get There. This is accomplished by developing the Strategic Plan Process Wheel that consists of five phases that should be evaluated before a decision is made to enter into any new business deal.

Phase One – Analyze:

Gather the facts from the past three years for all the key business drivers: People; Space; equipment; IT requirements; operating expenses; balance sheet impacts; and etc.

Phase Two – Plan:

With create a plan of where these key business drivers will be over the next three to five years. Create options and models as to how these business drivers will function within various business environments.

Phase Three – Forecast:

Taking the data created in the phase two planning process, forecast the impact of these business drivers in today’s enivirnoment and make assumptions as to how they will function in the coming years. Forecasting should include growth, contraction and or rightsizing and any other known issues.

Phase Four – Recommendations:

Based upon the business drivers, specific assumptions and your business experience create several recommendations with various options and models including a roadmap for implementing the recommendation. Then make a final recommendation based upon data that supports the decision which will include a detail five year operating plan and a ten year overview strategic plan.

Phase Five – Implementation:

With the approved recommendation and roadmap, implement the project immediately.

Business opportunities will yield dividends to someone in every deal. The magnitude of the yield is based upon the preparing process. Developing a Strategic Plan is the best source to ensure that business has been evaluated and that all aspects of the planning process has been completed and thoroughly analyzed. Decisions are made based upon facts, assumptions, models and utilizing the planning process. Prayer should be a major ingredient into the Strategic Planning Process.

In the kingdom God we read in Jeremiah 29: 13 & 14: “You will seek me and find me when you seek me with your whole heart. I will be found by you.” As we seek God he will provide the answers that we need in life. Spiritual and business dividends will be developed in a way that we can glorify God.
