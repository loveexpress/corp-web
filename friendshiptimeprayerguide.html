Friendship Time Prayer Guide

1. Our Father Which Art In Heaven, Hallo­wed Be Thy Name.

a. Thank God you can call Him Father because of the blood of Jesus.

Praise Him That He Is:

Jehovah-Tsidkenu

(Jehovah my righteousness)

Jehovah-M’Kaddesh

(Jehovah my sanctifier)

Jehovah-Shalom

(Jehovah,y Peace)

Jehovah-Shammah

(Jehovah os present)

Jehovah-Rophe

(Jehovah my healer)

Jehovah-Jireh

(Jehovah my provider)

Jehovah-Nissi

(Jehovah my protector)

Jehovah-Rohi

(Jehovah my shepherd)

Declare with your mouth:

You are my righteousness.

You are working in me both to will and to do your good pleasure.

You are my peace.

You are the Holy Spirit in my life who will never leave or forsake me.

You are my healer.

You are my provider

You are my protector.

You are my guide, leading me daily to do your will.

2. Thy Kingdom come thy will be done.

a. Yourself

b.Your family (name each-member

c. Your church (Pastor and staff, teachers, harvest).

d. Your nation (President, leaders, city, school, teachers.)

3. Give us this day our daily bread

a. Your needs.

b.Your family’s needs.

c. Your church needs.

4. And forgive us our debts as we forgive our debtors.

a. Ask God to forgive you.

b. Forgive others who have sinned against you.

c. Choose to forgive (ahead of time, at this moment) anyone who may sin against you today.

5. And lead us not in­to temptation, but deliver us from evil.

a. Put on the whole armor of God.

1. Belt of Truth buckled around -- “Jesus You are my truth -- I will not let any lie be in my heart.”

2. Breastplate of Righteousness -- “Jesus You are my righteousness- Your blood cleanses me from sin, and makes me right with God.”

3. Feet fitted with the readiness of the Gospel of Peace “I am not ashamed of the power of Gospel of Christ, for it is the power of God unto salvation to everyone who will believe. I am ready!”

4. Shield of Faith -- “Jesus, You are my faith -- I will believe what you say, not what the devil says, or my friends, or circumstances.”

5. Helmet of Salvation-- “Jesus, Your are my salvation, You are my Savior, my Redeemer, my Deliverer.”

6. Sword of the Spirit which is the Word of God --“Jesus, You are the Word of God which lives in me. Fill my mouth today with your word.

b. Put a hedge of protection around you and your family.

1. “Jesus, I set;my love on you. You are my home, my fortress, I trust in you. Thank you for letting me know your name.”

6. FOR THINE IS THE KINGDOM, AND THE POWER,
0AND THE GLORY, Forever

a. Praise God that He wants to share His kingdom, power, and glory with you.

b. Thank God that He will work in you so powerfully that other people will be able to see His glory on you.
