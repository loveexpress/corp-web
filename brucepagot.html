New Jersey Christian Ministries

Convention Offers 70 Workshops

By Rev. Bruce Pajot
President New Jersey Christian Ministries

New Jersey Christian Ministries will host its 52nd Annual Christian Ministries and Spiritual Life Convention, on October 26th and 27th, at Hawthorne Gospel Church, 2000 State Route 208 North, Hawthorne, New Jersey 07506. We will offer both Adult Ministry and Youth Ministry.

The convention features: 73 workshops spread over Friday night and Saturday. This includes 6 Teen Workshops on Saturday. The convention also includes a Friday night "youth rally" for teens, led by Rev. Dave Hayman and the youth band: "Less is More." TEENS ATTEND THE CONVENTION FREE. "Less is More" will also lead worship at the General Session. This year’s keynote speaker is Rev. Dr. David Schroeder, President of Somerset Christian College, and author of 10 books. Additionally, there will be 60 exhibitors representing a wide range of ministries and vendors. Three curriculum publishers will also be communicating their Christian Education curriculum offerings.

The theme for this year’s Convention is “Finding Our Treasure, True Riches and Our Destiny in God.” "Where your treasure is, there will your heart be also." (Mat 6:22). In difficult times such as these, it has never been more important to consider what our treasure is, and what is truly valuable. I believe the answer for us as Christians is: God and His Kingdom is Our Treasure! This year our convention will explore this theme in its application to our lives, our churches, and our ministries. The quality and depth of experience in our workshops, panel discussions, and our exhibitors is unequaled.
