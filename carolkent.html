Calvary Baptist Church
Women’s Conference

Carol Kent
http://www.carolkent.org

Saturday, October 26th, 2013, 9:30am-4pm with Carol Kent - “A New Kind of Normal” Nationally celebra­ted author and speaker.

At our Annual Women’s Conference this year we are blessed to have Carol Kent as our speaker. She is a best-selling author of over twenty books and Bible studies and her theme is “A New Kind of Normal” based on her own life experience. Ca­rol weaves humor, hope and faith into every message.

Carol Kent's life was turned upside-down when her only child, a graduate of the U.S. Naval Academy in Annapolis, was arrested for murder. Life hands most of us unexpected challenges that mean life as we once thought it would be, turns out differently from the "normal life" we anticipated. The conference will be filled with en­cou­raging reminders that in every difficult experience we encounter, if we are looking for Divine Surprises, we’ll find them, and when we embrace those challenges in the tight spots of life, we will discover an adventurous journey that produces unexpected joy, un­anticipated favor, a streng­thened faith, and a meaningful adventure. We'll laugh out loud and wipe a few tears as we discover how to lean into "the grace place." "See, I am doing a new thing! Now it springs up; do you not perceive it? I am making a way in the desert and streams in the wasteland.” Isaiah 43: 19

Carol’s messages will lift our hearts to a place of renewed hope and brand new courage. Ladies, please mark your calendars and bring a friend!

She is a former radio show co-host and her messages have been featured on Focus on the Family. She truly speaks from the heart and from her own journey as she follows Him. Venue: Calvary Baptist Church, 123 West 57th Street (between 6th & 7th Aves.), New York, NY 10019. Cost: $20 (lunch not included).

For more information call (718) 975-0170 or visit www.cbcnyc.org.
