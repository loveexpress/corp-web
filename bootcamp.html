Men’s Discipleship Boot Camp

By Melissa Alcorn, Executive Director, and
James Winans, Manager of Institutional Giving.

What is a Boot Camp? God4Me Men’s Network is a National Alliance of Chur­ches and Men’s groups systematically training and as- sisting men in their pursuit of significance and purpose. “Discipleship Boot Camps” are forums to provide en­cou­­ragement, instruction and fellowship for the transformed man. Anointed speakers help men replace the lies of the world with Biblical truth. All Men, Leaders and Pastors are invited. Events usually fill to 50% of capacity upon announcement. Attended by 300-1000 Men per location. A continental Breakfast at Round Tables creates a “Cup of Coffee Listener” en­vironment so men are free. Recognize-Resist-Retaliate to the given message.

Speakers include Au­thor and Pastor Jim Cym­bala, Head Coach Scott Caesar. There will be Music by The Brooklyn Taberna­cle Men’s Choir.

The Theme this year is “A Man of Order in a World of  Chaos.” Balancing Your Life Radically. Over 500 Chur­ches - Interactive Work shops - Multimedia Pa­ra­bles - Release of the W.E.A. P.O.N.S. - Follow Up Curri­culum for Men.

Come alongside your Pastor and support him by edifying his Leadership and encourage men to be in­volved with Church Male fellowship. This establishes cre- dibility and empowers the Leadership to coach their men effectively and disciple and encourage men to de­velop a relationship with Je­sus Christ and other godly men. Saturday, October 16, 2010 @ Freedom Cha­pel, 641 Broadway, Amity­ville, New York 11701 & Saturday, October 30th, 2010 @ The Brooklyn Ta­ber­nacle, 17 Smith Street, Brooklyn, New York 11201. The schedule for both meetings is from 8am-12:45pm. Tickets:$12; At Door $15. Team Pack 10 Men:$100. (This Includes a continental breakfast). Call now at 631-753-1244 or go to the web and visit www.discipleship bootcamp.com. Sponsored by God4Me Network.
