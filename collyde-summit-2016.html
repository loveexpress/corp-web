Collyde Summit 2016

Francis Chan

Our greatest fear should not be of failure but of succeeding at things in life that don't really matter" says, Francis Chan, best-selling author of Crazy Love, Forgotten God and many other videos and books. As leaders, pastors and believers, our constant struggle is to turn away from the noise-cluttered busy ministry world we live in and quieten our hearts to hear from the Holy Spirit. If this describes your challenge, then Collyde Summit 2016 is for you.

Collyde Summit is a ga­thering of passionate leaders, pastors and believers in the NJ, NY and PA area, who love Jesus and are convicted to become world changers. Collyde Summit is quickly becoming the premier two day conference of the Northeast for believers and leaders alike. What sets Collyde Summit apart is the combination of amazing worship, practical and relevant break-out sessions and inspiring Spirit-led speakers. The four break-out sessions are Lea­dership, Justice, Life & Community and Worship & Creative Arts. There will be 30 or more global organizations like World Vision, International Justice Mission and many others. A great bonus is the opportunity to meet like-minded believers, pastors and leaders from the Northeast area.

Some of Collyde Summit 2016 speakers include Francis Chan, Lysa Ter­keurst, Jennie Allen, Ryan Baitzel and Richard Sterns. This event will sell-out. Don't miss this inspiring, transformational and impactful local conference. Register today at www.collydesummit.com. The Love Express readers can get an additional 10% off by using the special Code: TLECS16 until February 15, 2016. Limited Seats available with the above code. Register today!
