American Bible Society

Embodying Hope

American Bible Society is pleased to announce the Embodying Hope Conference Series 2011 Saturday, May 7th, 9am-3pm at 1865 Broadway, New York, NY. This is to REFRESH and ENCOURAGE pastors and ministry leaders with God’s Word.

It will provide practical tips and biblical insight for those who serve the least, the last and the lost in their communities.

With more than 50 years of experience guest speakers will cover the following topics.

• God’s Promises for You.
• Passionate Pursuit of God’s Plan.
• Maximizing your Ministry Gift.
• Advocating Scripture Engagement.

General information Free Registration for the first 150 people who register by April 22, 2011. Also provided Continental Breakfast & Lunch. Complimentary tour of the Bible Society’s Museum of Biblical Art.

Guest Speakers:

Senior Pastor Carter Conlon, Times Square Church.

Brian R. Johansson, Vice President of The Bowery Mission and Kids with a Promise.

Brandy Shiloh Esq., Author. Brandy Shiloh Ministries is established as a vehicle that empowers individuals everywhere to live their best life now.

Minister Sheree E. Williams, Christian Recording Artist will lead praise and worship.

Register by visiting EH.AmericanBible.org or call Delores Burnett at 212-408-1416.
