Gracepoint Gospel Fellowship

Babbie Mason Conference / Concert

Gracepoint Gospel Fellowship located at 384 New Hempstead Road, New City NY will be hosting Babbie Mason as a Keynote Speaker at their Women’s Conference on Saturday Oct. 20th at 9am and Live In Concert on Sunday Oct. 21st at 6pm. Ticket registration via www.gracepointgospel.com are $10 for the Women’s Conference. Tickets for the concert via (www.itickets.com) are $20 (in advance) & $25 (at the door).

Babbie Mason is a Grammy Award Nominee, a Dove Award Winning Singer/Songwriter of Contemporary Christian/Gospel Music. Growing up a ‘P.K.’ (Preacher’s Kid) in the traditional Baptist Church, Babbie demonstrated exceptional musical skill by the tender age of 9. She was playing piano and choir directing, accompanying her Dad (the late Rev. George W. Wade) as he shared the Gospel message across the Great Lakes region. Babbie would gain experience in Inspirational Music Ministry. She has ministered in venues such as Billy Graham Crusades, various Women’s Conferences and in musical auditoriums worldwide.

Out of Babbie Mason’s deep commitment to faith in Christ she spreads the Gospel message via teachings, hosting a T.V. Talk Show, and most recently she has written a book “Embraced By God…Celebrating Who & Whose You Are” (Abingdon). A companion to the book is Babbie’s 24th musical project. The CD entitled “Embrace”.

A powerful, awe inspiring speaker, Babbie utilizes a blend of sharing, teaching, preaching, singing and humor to encourage all to embrace God’s unique plan for us utilizing the gifts that He placed in us. She tirelessly encourages all to understand acceptance, forgiveness and love from God’s perspective.

Several of Babbie Mason’s songs are recognized as ‘Christian Classics’. “All Rise”, “Each One Reach One”, “All In Favor”, “With All My Heart” are but a sampling of the yielded talents that resulted in Babbie being inducted into the Christian Music Hall Of Fame in 2010. Vibrant, witty, yet gracious and approachable, Babbie loves to encourage aspiring musicians and songwriters who want to respond to the call to enter Music Ministry. More recently she has partnered with her husband Charles to mentor musicians in their “Inner Circle” music conferences and workshops.

Babbie is making herself available for phone / radio/interviews to help promote her engagements at Gracepoint. (770)258-8863 Gracepoint Gospel anticipates no less than a wonderful time.
