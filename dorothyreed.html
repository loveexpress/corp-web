A Church With Vision

by Pastor Dorothy Reed

After a fourteen-year battled with cancer, Bishop Charles J. Reed was called home to be with the Lord, and a 38 years friendship came to an end “…and who would have thought, that the death of Bishop Reed would have thrusted Dorothy Reed into the purpose of God as it relates to her life in God”, Bishop Ralph L. Dennis.

On Feb. 18, 2007, Dr. Dorothy Reed, the wife of the late Bishop Charles Joseph Reed, with the blessing of ArchBishop Roy E. Brown opened the doors to Fresh Beginnings, “A Church with A Vision” Inc. and 39 people became the founding members. Since that day God has continued to add to the church such as should be saved! The vision of, Fresh Beginnings “A Church With A Vision” was birth out of what God deposited into the late Bishop Charles J. Reed. Several months prior to his demise, he shared several things that had become again very important to him and as a result of this Fresh Beginnings A Church With A Vision’s mission statement is to Reach up in Worship, Reach in as we seek to mature and Reach out impacting the community in which we worship.

Pastor Dorothy Reed received her call to the ministry as a teenager at The New Jerusalem Holiness Church, where the late Bishop Johnny Lee Harris pastored. Years later, as newly weds they transferred their membership to the Pilgrim Church for approximately eleven years after which Bishop Reed was assigned to the Pilgrim Church of Harlem and for the next fourteen years, she served faithfully beside her Pastor/husband. Pastor Reed’s duties and assignment was specifically to President of the Women's Dept., Head of the Fine Arts Dept., the Audio Dept. nd where ever needed. Serving in ministry along with them were their three children, Charles A. Reed, Corey A. Reed and Chemdiya A. Reed.

On July 14, 2007, her spiritual father Bishop Ralph L. Dennis, Chief Prelate of Kingdom Fellowship Covenant Ministries ordained and installed her as Pastor. Pastor Dorothy Reed holds a Masters Degree and a Doctoral Degree and is presently in Regent University where she is working towards a degree in religious studies. She continues to wear many hats mother, administrator, and student.

On Dec. 9, 2007 at 4:30pm Fresh Beginnings, ‘A Church With A Vision’ will host its 2nd Annual Bishop Charles Joseph Reed Educational Scholarship Fund. The fund is committed to educational development in primary education, secondary education and higher education. We look forward to another year of expanding this portion of our dream.
