Higher Ground Record Pool

By Melissa Alcorn, Executive Director, and
James Winans, Manager of Institutional Giving.

On Sept. 21-22, 2009, The Higher Ground
Record Pool (HGRP)/DJ Alliance is hosting the 1st DJ Conference geared to educate, empower, and mobilize God’s Mobile Soldiers... “The Gospel/Christian DJ”. This conference is geared to educate, empower, and mobilize Gospel/Christian DJs while equipping them in grassroots promotions and business stabilization. It all takes place in the city where DJs (Hip Hop and Gospel) were birthed: New York City.

Today’s digital music paradigm has radically changed how people discover, hear, interact and purchase music. Where they hear music is just as important as where they buy music. According to the August 2008 Black Enterprise Magazine, major radio stations are aiming to cut expenses while boosting ratings. They are doing this by creating more syndication shows thus eliminating as much as 1,520 jobs on the once indispensable staple of urban radio; the local radio programmer. Radio is no longer the primary outlet for new music, new artists, and a variety of genres.

Furthermore, in a recent email blast from “Radio Hit on Demand”, it stated that 4 out of 10 Radio listeners get little or no satisfaction from local radio programming and that radio does not capture the youth market. IPods and CDs have cut into radio appeal and very little or zero music heard from local based artists or bands. Gone are the days of breaking music, right... Wrong.

Although radio has the ability to reach more people, breaking music was never their niche. Ask any clubbers or true party goers and they will tell you that the superstars for “Breaking” new music while “Rocking the Crowd” were, and still are, the “Mobile/Club DJs”.

The Crown Plaza Hotel-La Guardia Airport, Monday and Tuesday, September 21-22, 2009. 2 Full Days of workshops, Seminars, DJ exhibits, Awards, Artists showcasing, and “Real” Holy Ghost parties.
Contact Dr.D@highergroundrecordpool.org.
