About us

The Love Express

Mrs. Edith Stranges, Editor & Publisher

The Love Express is the news media of Expressions of Love, a Christian Media Company that has served the Christian community for over 25 years in the tri-state, New York , New Jersey and Long Island area. The primary purpose of The Love Express is to obey God, and with that goal in mind we endeavor to share the Good News of what God is doing on the East Coast, as well as around the world. We at the Love Express have our finger on the pulse of what the Holy Spirit is saying. "A voice in the midst of a voice, a watchman at the gates of the city." God is calling us to be vigilant with a strong desire to be constantly watching, praying and being alert.

The Love Express is a reflection of what the Body of Christ will be like in heaven, where every tribe, every tongue and every culture will be represented praising the Lord.

Be apart of the great move of the Holy Spirit that is sweeping across this nation. For further information call, email or write concerning the print edition or the web.
