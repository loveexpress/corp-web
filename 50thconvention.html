Update:

50th Annual NJ Christian
Ministries Convention

On October 22-23, 2010 at Hawthorne Gospel Church, Hawthorne, NJ 07506 we will sponsor our 50th Annual Christian Ministries Convention. It’s been our privilege to be used by the Lord for 50 years to equip Christians for service in His Kingdom.

Name Change: Over the years, we have primarily equipped believers for being more effective in their ministry. Over the last few years, however, we’ve included workshops specifically aimed at helping us live the Christian life. So we are now calling the Convention: The Spiritual Life and Christian Ministries Convention.

Keynote Speaker: Rev. Dr David Ireland. He is the founder and senior pastor of Christ Church (with about 5000 members) in Montclair, NJ and Rockaway, NJ. A former civil and mechanical engineer, Dr. David couples his keen sense of humor with a precise, analytical style in translating complex biblical concepts into practical applications. He has an earned Ph.D. in Organizational Leadership and is a noted author.

Convention Theme: “Empowered By Christ We Keep Pressing Toward The Goal.”

For further information call 1-800-363-4410 or email us: info@ministriesconvention.com or visit www.ministriesconvention.com
