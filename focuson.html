Focus On The Children: Silent No More

Jesus Is The Reason for The Season

by Elder Charlotte Hall

HELLO BELOVED! We thank God for this glorious season that is upon us. The season that celebrates the birth of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ! Less we forget, JESUS IS THE REAL REASON FOR THE SEASON! Imagine my surprise as I walked into the sanctuary one Sunday morning in early October only to find the congregation up and greeting each other with a happy and warm MERRY CHRISTMAS! All I could do was join in with the biggest smile ever, wishing all those in close proximity a very MERRY CHRISTMAS! How awesome to be on the offensive as we prepare to wage war against the onslaught of “happy holiday”

CHRISTMAS is for CHRISTIANS and for all those who wish to participate in our celebration. Yet the world and its merchants seek to over shadow the reason for our celebration by their pronouncement of “happy holiday”. How about a “HAPPY HOLIDAY”, In response. My challenge to you is to not be swept away by what seems to be a gracious salutation. But is only a bold attempt to eliminate christ out of our Christmas salutation. In fact rearranging our Christmas greeting altogether. The adversary roams about seeking whom he may devour. And we will not be ignorant of his devises. No, No, No! JESUS IS THE ONLY REASON FOR THE CHRISTMAS SEASON! If we think its just a small thing I say, “WAKE UP THOU THAT SLEEPEST”. IT IS A DIRECT ATTACK AGAINST CHRISTIANITY. Just another attempt to ebb away the true meaning of this holy season. According to the word of God. It is the little foxes that spoil the vine, Song of Solomon 2:15, and a little leaven leaveneth the whole lump. Gal. 5:9 It only took one woman to initiate taking prayer out of schools. Did we forget?

We are the light of the world and Christmas is our time.    Think about how many Christian dollars light cash registers around the country and the world. Let your light shine brighter at the cash register by boldly and kindly asking the cashier “Do you know    Jesus? ”Be prepared to leave the cashier with a tract perhaps, and whatever else God leads you to say or do. But do minister the love of Jesus Christ. This is the time when our light should shine intentionally brighter, our voices boldly and confidently expressing “MERRY CHRISTMAS”. Please let the greeting cards we send reflect the reason for our celebration of the season, “JESUS!” Daily remind our children and grandchildren why the schools will be closed for close to ten whole days, ”JESUS”. As we run around shopping and preparing to express our love and excitement let us keep the reason in the forefront of our minds, “JESUS”! The only reason that many will not be working on December 25th, Christmas Day, is because of “JESUS,” THIS IS OUR SEASON! YES, AND SOMETHINGS BEAR REPEATING, over and over and over again.

Let us not conform to this world, Rom. 12:2, as it continues to spiral out of control, attempting to do away with every remembrance of Christianity and the all powerful name of JESUS CHRIST. God has given us power and authority we must use it intentionally at every turn and on purpose. Yes, your voice counts. ONE MAN SHALL CHASE A THOUSAND, AND TWO TEN THOUSAND, Joshua 23:10. EVIL PREVAILS WHEN THE RIGHTEOUS DO NOTHING.  Selah. Do it afraid, but just do it and do it NOW!

By God’s leading I have designed a “JESUS IS THE REASON” lapel button. To be worn as a constant reminder and witness to the world as I travel about during our glorious season. They have been well received for several years. And they over came him by the blood of the lamb and the word of their testimony, Rev. 12:11. MERRY CHRISTMAS TO ALL! I would be most happy to bless you with complimentary”JESUS IS THE REASON” lapel button.

Charlotte A.Hall, Pastor/ Founder, Emanuel Pra­yer Ministry Post Office Box 210-054, Brooklyn, NY 11221
