Focus On The Children:

Silent No More! - part 2

by Elder Charlotte Hall

Hello Beloved! JESUS IS THE REASON FOR THIS SEASON, and every season. Christmas is a glorious season of celebrating the birth of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ! Yet even in the midst of this holy and exciting time for Christians, the world attempts to over shadow the reason for the season by their pronouncement of "happy holiday."

My challenge to you is don't be swept in by what seems like a gracious salutation. But is really a bold elimination, taking Christ out of our Christmas greetings. In fact removing the name and the greeting all together, I think not! Jesus is the ONLY reason for the Christmas season and holiday celebrations. Is it really a small thing? No, it is just another ploy of the enemy to ebb away the true meaning of this holiday season and a direct attack on Christianity. According to the Word of God its the little foxes that spoil the vine, Song. 2:15, and a little leaven leaveneth the whole lump.  Gal. 5:9, It only took one women to initiate taking prayer out of our public schools. Did we forget?

We are the light of the world and Christmas time is our time, (and many Christian dollars light up cash registers all around the country at this time too). This is the time when our light should intentionally shine bright, our voi­ces boldly and confidently expressing and sometimes shouting, "MERRY CHRISTMAS"!! Let the greeting cards we send reflect the reason for our celebration of the season, "JESUS." Remind our children why schools are closed for ten whole days, "JESUS." As we run around shopping and preparing to express our love and excitement let us keep the reason in the forefront of our minds, "JESUS." The only reason that many of us will not be working on December 25th, Christmas Day, is because of "JESUS." THIS IS OUR SEASON!

Let us not conform to this world, Rom. 12: 2a, as it continues to spiral out of control attempting to do away with every remembrance of Christianity and the name Jesus Christ. We have the power and the authority that God has given us, we must use it intentionally, at every turn. Yes, your voice counts, use it on purpose. One man of you shall chase a thousand, Joshua 23:10. Evil prevails when the righteous do nothing and say nothing!  Selah.

Complimentary "Jesus is the reason for the season" button available. Wear it as a witness and constant reminder to the world as you travel about during this glorious season. And they overcame him by the blood of the Lamb and the word of their testimony, Rev.12:11

MERRY CHRISTMAS TO ALL!!!
