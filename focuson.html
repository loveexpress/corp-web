Focus On The Children:

Silent No More!

by Elder Charlotte Hall

Hello Beloved! Greetings in the last quarter of the year of 2014. Yes, time is fleeting and here we are in the last quarter already. In the midst of these dark days as the world continues to spiral out of control. Are we sitting on the couch in the land of complacency, luke warm discouraged, depressed?

Just where are we and just what are we doing in Lo Debar? Where a baby is aborted every 30 seconds. Where a young girl is being used and sexually abused, sometimes as many as 20 times a day. Being held captive by a human slave trafficker. Wake up thou that sleepiest. The devil is on a rampage. He’s after you and me without exception. He’s after the weak, the strong, the venerable, the invincible(?), the young, the older, the rich, the not so rich. Who cares? Certainly not the enemy, relentless in his quest to steal, kill and destroy. John 10:10

Distractions are a scheme of the enemy to take you off course. We must find our way back to our first love, on fire for the things of God. Oh yes, trials and tribulations, the cares of this life have come, so what else is new? Just take a quick look in your rear view mirror and see the trail of victories, the times of deliverance, healing and the supernatural power of the Holy Ghost. Did we forget? There’s nothing the enemy can throw at us that God has not equipped us to handle. But when the pressure is on I have to constantly remind myself, who’s really in charge. What about you? Oh but maybe, just maybe you gradually stop­ped telling yourself or you slowly went silent or were caught unaware and just shut down in weariness. Oh the subtle wiles of the enemy. None of us are immured.

Is God speaking to you as He is to me? If so then we must get up, stand up and stir up ourselves and those around us like nobody else can. Get back in the game. Return to our first works with the fervor for our first love. Get back on course. This is no time to quit. Saints don’t retire, we just get “re-fired”! Some of us have just been waiting and asking. Well, now God is speaking to those who have ears, let us hear what the Spirit of the Lord is saying. Rise up, pursue, recover all and occupy doing what God has called each of us to do individually and winning souls for the kingdom collectively. His return is eminent. We must fight the good fight of faith like never before and remember who wins. We want to be found faithful and obedient to the end. What is faith without works? A distraction to keep you from doing what God has gifted you to do and called you to do.

Yes, we continue to use the mighty weapon of prayer, as we praise Him like never before. Praise a most lethal weapon. Don’t do it because you feel like it. Do it by faith, especially when you don’t feel like it. Speak life giving words, declare and decree with faith and boldness. God has given us that authority and the power of the tongue. Silent no more, we can’t allow the enemy to steal our voice. The Bible is the source of our strength so open it or re-open it and find the word for the situations and problematic circumstan­ces that may be in your midst. Remember that favor surrounds us as a shield. Grace and new mercies are present every morning.

Goodness and mercy follows us all the days of our lives. So rise up and shake off the works of darkness; despair, confusion, distress, discomfort and on and on. The entrance of the word brings light and life. We must take courage and choose life so that darkness will be dispelled and the world around us won’t be devoured by the enemy.

Conflict is inevitable, you can count on it. But our victory is assured through our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ! We fight ‘til we win and the fight is fixed! God always causes us to triumph. We are victorious in Him! Finish strong. Don’t Be Distracted.
