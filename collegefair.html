Free Christian College Fair

The 12th Annual National Christian College Fair is being held at Hawthorne Christian Academy in Hawthorne, NJ on Friday, October 1st from 7-9pm.

Over 30 Christian Colleges and Universities are expected to be represented and a FREE financial aid seminar will be held at 7:30pm to help parents and students navigate the financial commitments of a college education.

Hawthorne Christian Academy is located at 2000 Route 208 North in Hawthorne, NJ. For more information call 973-423-3331, ext 270 or visit www.hca.org.

Both the College Fair and Financial Aid Seminar are Free of Charge.
