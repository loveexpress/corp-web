50 Couples Renewed Vows

By Miss Gee

The LE Praise ship landed in Freeport, LI for the “Reaffirming of Marriage” at Zion Cathedral where Bishop Frank Otha White presides and Bishop Frank Anthone White is Pastor. This monumental event had over fifty couples reaffirming their vows to each other according to the Word and God. Marriage was instituted by God for a man and a woman. Even if our society tries to re-define what they feel marriage should be, but the word of God never changes.

It was the most powerful wedding ceremony I have ever been to. When you walked into the church, the set up so beautiful, you felt like being in a fairy tale. You felt as if you were in a garden full of flowers; just you, your man, and God. My hat goes off to event and wedding planner extraordinaire, Ms Keisha Wilson.

The couples were married anywhere from three years to 65 years and faced each other as if they were married for the first time. Bishop Anthone White presided and did such an excellent job with the vows he created for the couples. If Bishop A. White ever needed another career, writing vows would be the route for him to take. For the reception we went to Verdi to celebrate the couple’s reaffirmation. It is a beautiful venue to eat, laugh and be merry. Each couple received a certificate to remember this day. Also the jurisdiction honored people for their dedication to the Jurisdiction, which I loved, because it’s so wonderful to be recognized for the work that you do for the kingdom.

Ms. Gee
