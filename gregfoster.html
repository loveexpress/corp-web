Shifting Into Destiny

by Bishop Greg Foster

It is not by chance that the precious metal used to mark 25 years is silver; a metal that is purified, and made complete for use, through fire and refinement. It is no coincidence that Bible Faith Tabernacle is celebrating its 25th anniversary in the year 2007, representing completion, and ushering the year 2008, which is the year of new beginnings. It is GOD’S DIVINE PURPOSE that our theme marking 25 years of ministry is “Shifting Into Destiny”.

This 25th anniversary celebration marks the accomplishments of the ministry, and recognizes and honors the Shepherd of the house, Bishop Gregory A. Foster. Bishop Foster’s faithfulness to God, love of God and his people, and endurance and dedication in the time of adversity have proven him to be as refined silver, set apart and precious for the Father’s work. We celebrate Bishop Gregory A. Foster’s 25th anniversary of preaching, teaching and reaching for the Kingdom’s work. Through the years, Bishop Foster has ministered to thousands of souls, touching many lives through the gospel of Jesus Christ.

As we embark on this celebration we invite you to join us in our services on Friday November 9th with Bishop Clarence McClendon, Sunday November 11th with Pastor Jackie McCullough and Tuesday November 13th with Bishop George Bloomer. Our banquet feast will be held on Monday November 12th @ 6:00pm at Russo’s on The Bay Queens, NY.

Call for further information 1-718-467-3040 ask for Deacon William McKinney. Services are held weekly: Sunday Morning 11:00am and Tuesday, Friday and Sunday night 7:30pm @ The Bible Faith Ministry 1368 St. Johns Place (between Utica Ave and Schenectady Ave) Brooklyn NY 11213
