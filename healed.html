"Healed @ Work" Conference

by Drew Campbell

Every workplace in America has "issues." On October 18-20, 2007, the first-ever "Healed @ Work Conference" will be held at the Sturbridge Worship Center in historic Sturbridge, Massachusetts to bring healing to workplace problems, relationships, and issues. The conference will feature top-notch speakers from across the United States, who have decades of experience in bringing emotional healing to wounded and broken people. Presenters will share through general sessions, workshops, and personal sessions. Breakout groups will be held for top management, middle management, employees, and entrepreneurs.

The Healed @ Work Conference is being orga­nized by a variety of chur­ches, ministries and businesses that share a desire to reduce workplace violence and dysfunction. Dysfunction affects every business, school, church, non-profit, institution, and government agency in the world.  Billions of dollars in productivity are lost each year through workplace stress, sexual harassment, lack of integrity, illness, interpersonal conflicts, violence and theft.

Keynote speakers will include (in alphabetical or­der) Pat Baldridge, President & Co-Founder of Women’s Initiative Network in Charlotte, NC; the Rev. Dan Lee, Director of Lion's Den Min. in Sturbridge, MA; the Rev. H.B. London, Vice President of Church & Clergy for Focus on the Family in Colorado Springs, CO; Dr. Ed Smith, Founder of Theophostic Prayer Min. in Campbells­ville, KY; and the Rev. Ken Unger, Founder of Sunrise Retreats in Rancho Palos Verdes, CA. Music by sin­ger Michele Wagner of Cleveland, OH and the Sturbridge Worship Ctr. Praise & Worship Team.

The organizers deliberately selected Sturbridge during beautiful peak New England fall foliage season. The facility is near historic Old Sturbridge Village and the Massachusetts Turnpike, and within a 90-minute drive of major airports in Boston, Providence, and Hartford. Discounted early registration is just $199 for individuals and $299 for married couples.

For details, please call toll-free 1-800-JOY-9075 or visit us at www.HealedAtWork.org.
