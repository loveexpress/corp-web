Take a Stand for Merry Christmas

Prophecies from the Old Testament were fulfilled in Jesus’ life, death and resurrection. Nearly 300 predictions from hundreds of years before his birth were acknowledged by rabbis as having been made in reference to a coming to a coming deliverer they called the Messiah.

These predictions included Bethlehem as the place of his birth (Micah 5:2), that he would be born of a virgin (Isaiah 7:14 in the Septuagint Greek translation c.a. 250 B.C..E.) and that the time of his birth would be just before Israel lost their sovereign power as a nation (Genesis 49:10) - this took place just after the beginning of the First Century C.E. when Archelaus took the throne. This Christmas season receive the greatest gift that can ever be given. Open your heart and let the One who was born in a stable in humility, died on a cross in love, and rose from the dead in power, enter in!

Have you accepted the most precious gift he has for you? You can right now.
