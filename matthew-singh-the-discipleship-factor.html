The Discipleship Factor

Pastor Matthew & Ka­thy Singh.

Mark 1:17 says “And Jesus said to them, come follow me and be my disciples, and I will make you to become fishers of men.”

When Jesus says, “Come follow me”, He is not referring to some well- thought out church program or event, He means come follow him, come walk with him, come establish a relationship with him, come be his disciple. Discipleship is all about the relationship. It is the concept of Heaven.

Four steps of the Discipleship process

 1. Come and seek- When you come to church, look for Jesus. Disciples do daily devotionals, grow closer in their walk with God by building a relationship with Him through His word? When we understand His words, then we may do His works. When you enter the Kingdom of God, aim to sit next to the King and recognize when He is there, do not be distracted by mundane things that are of no importance.

 2. Come and follow-Being ready to follow means your eyes will no longer be seeking, your heart is fixed on following. Pursue Him with a goal of being a disciple. Come to church on time, bring your Bi­ble, take notes and tithe every week, a true disciple of Christ is mindful of these habits.

 3. Come and become the moment right after you come and follow, you will become, there will be transformation, from that of a believer to a disciple. You cannot attract people to Jesus if people cannot see changes in you.

 4. Come and go and make disciples- Become equipped with the knowledge and skills. Put all that you have learned into practice. Come and become eventually turns into come and go make disciples of others.
