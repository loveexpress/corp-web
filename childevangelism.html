Proclaiming His Name

from Generation to Generation

15th Annual Child Evangelism Fellowship

Presented by Child Evangelism Fellowship of Greater New York City

Saturday, April 9, 2011, 8:15am-4pm; First Baptist Church, 142-10 Sanford Avenue (at Union Street) Flushing, NY 11355.

Why “Proclaiming His Name from Generation to Generation”? The need to reach the next generation with the Gospel of Christ has never been greater! You know the Lord’s heart for the children! And you know of the challenges these children face. It’s our responsibility to make sure each one had the opportunity to come to the One who gives hope —eternal hope!

If the children of Generation Z—the children of the current 4-14 Window—are going to hear the Good News, the generations that came before need to come together with a common, uniting goal! Jesus’ words found in Matthew 18:14 make it clear that it’s not the Father’s will that one of these children should be lost. Let’s come together and proclaim His name to the children of the next generation! Let’s help them know how to pass that truth on to the generation that follows!

Conference Cost: $70/person. Register early!

Lunch, conference notebook (with notes for all workshops) and a gift are included in the registration fee! Checks or money orders may be made payable to CEF and mailed with completed registration form to: CEF, PO Box 140220, Staten Island, NY 10314-0220.

Attention Pastors! Bring 5 or more people from your congregation, and one senior or youth pastor’s registration is free!

300 registrations are available! Please register early! Additional registration forms may be found at www.cefnyc.com
