DJ Centered…Industry Based:
THE FIRST ANNUAL CHRISTIAN DJ CONFERENCE
 Comes to New York

On Sept. 21-22, 2009, The Higher Ground Record Pool (HGRP)/1 Accord DJ Alliance will host its 1st Annual DJ Conference. This milestone marks the creation of the 1st National DJ Conference for the Faith-based community. The Higher Ground Record Pool (HGRP)/1 Accord DJ Alliance Conference is geared to educate, empower and mobilize Gospel/Christian DJs while equipping them in grassroots promotions and business stabilization. This conference will be hosted in the city where DJs (Hip Hop and Gospel) were birthed--New York City. Open to DJs, artists, record labels and radio personalities, the conference will feature a combination of workshops, seminars, DJ exhibits, artists’ showcases and some “Holy Ghost” partying. Attendees will leave with a comprehensive understanding of the united role they play for those in the Faith-based music industry. NY based HGRP President Dr. D says, “With the rise of artists driven monolithic syndicated urban radio shows and radio no longer an key source of music assimilation, it’s time for an organized Christian DJ driven movement”.

Confirmed for Tuesday’s “Feeding of the 5000” morning worship service is Bishop Kenneth Moales, Senior Pastor of Prayer Tabernacle of Love, Inc in Bridgeport, Connecticut. Workshop speakers include award winning author DJ Ray Mar, professional sound engineer Tim GilChrist, touring DJ- DJ Robbo (The Truth), Christian Record Pool chapter Presidents DJ Lace (HGRP) and DJ D-Lite (Kingdom Affiliates), artists Marketing specialists Eboni Funderbunk-Grimes and Don and Stephanie Wilkerson, and Jason Hollis on Distribution. Confirmed industry panelists include DJ Times Magazine editor Jim Tremayne (DJ Expo), George Flores (Starr 99.1 FM), Liz Black (1190 AM WLIB), Darren Greggs (1240 AM WTHE) and guest moderator will be Dwayne Haskins (CEO, Soul Brothers).

Higher Ground and OADA are proud to welcome its official sponsors: IDJNow DJ store, Chauvet Lighting and Dzigns by Faith of New York serves as the HGRP T-shirt sponsor. The official “Welcome to New York Party” will be held Sunday night at the new Gospel Uptown restaurant in Harlem. Artists sponsorship slots are available.

Recently, Higher Ground welcomed aboard National Association of Mobile Entertainers (N.A.M.E) the largest Mobile Entertainer / DJ association. NAME continually seeks to develop strategic relationships that aid not only the industry but also each respective member’s company. As the date draws closer, more and more DJs are registering to attend the 1st “DJ Centered and Industry Based” Christian Conference designed to educate, empower, and mobilize Gospel/Christian DJs while equipping them in grassroots promotions and business stabilization.

HGRP continues to make noticeable strives and set new industry standards as it announced last month that it will serve as the Official DJ’s of the 2010 Xist Gospel Cruise. This cruise is designed to be the hottest, craziest, for real Holy Ghost party on a ship that one could ever imagine! Not only will the cruise have musical guests, live contest, panel discussions, award-winning speakers but Xist has partnered with the HGRP DJs to spin at all the many after-parties each night! DJ Lace- HGRP Atlanta Chapter- President stated, “Many companies overlook this part of the cruise itinerary. On the 2010 Xist Urban Gospel Cruise, after you have heard some of the best musical guest this industry has to offer, you can come party with them as we cruise to the Bahamas.”

This past April, HGRP DJ’s had a massive presence at this year GMA Week in Nashville, TN. The release of their brand new digital downloadable “Positive DJ Mixtape Vol. 5” was received with an overwhelming positive response. Highlighting new releases from 17 artists, this mode of artists promotions created by the Atlanta Chapter President, DJ Lace, was distributed to industry reps, radio personalities, celebrities, artists, TSU college students and GMA/Dove Awards attendees. Mixtapes are a primary tool of artist promotions for DJs. The “Positive DJ Mixtape Vol. 5” was also the Official DJ Mixtape for the Urban Soul Café series and the Urban Soul Café was the Official Pre-Dove Awards show event this year.

About HGRP
HGRP was established in 2001 as an international liaison between Gospel music and its consumer base composed of over 17 million church members. With chapters currently in New York, Atlanta, Chicago and Philadelphia, they have become the first Gospel DJ Pool to report and chart to STS in 2008. HGRP was created to provide a network for the niche ministry work of God’s mobile soldiers.
