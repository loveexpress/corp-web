Editorial: We Are All Mothers

Edith Stranges

Women are natural Mothers. As in World War II, the women rose to the occasion and stepped up to the plate, to take care of the homefront and working in jobs usually given to men.

In this season, when the winds are changing the spiritual scene on earth, Go­d is breathing encouragement and inspiration into many women of faith and valor. As the enemy en­ters their domain, they will rise to the occasion and attack as a lioness protecting her babies. They will act with cou­rage and strength discerning the opportunity God provides them and slay their foes.

3,500 years ago there was a brave judge and pro­phet named Deborah who held court under a palm tree between two cities. She was the leader of the nation of Israel. She handled both civil and spiritual matters, handling disputes and speaking prophetic words; a Mo­ther in Israel, (Judges 5:7). I believe God is releasing a special anointing on women, because many women have gone AWOL (Absence without Leave) or else they are imprisoned within their culture, and a religious system. The Lord wants to set wo­men free, and He wants to raise up a company of end-time Deborahs. Women who ga­ther in War Rooms around their homes to learn to move strategically as His government invades the earth.

Judges 5:9 ...into the hand of a women, God gave Deborah revelation of how to do battle and then a pro­phe­tic word when it was time to fight the enemy (Judges 4:6,7-14.

Blessings, Editor
Edith M. Stranges
