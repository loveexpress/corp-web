Grace Chapel &
Pillar College

Announce New Internship Program

Grace Chapel, a Christian Missionary Alliance church located in Bedminster, New Jersey and Pillar College are announcing a unique new mentored internship program designed to provide selected students of Pillar College with opportunities to develop their talents and abilities in real world ministry experiences while receiving up to $7,500 in tuition assistance and $2,500 cash per year. The program is open to students studying in any major, and will include opportunities to work with youth, young adults, children and mothers, and even technology and media.

The program was born from the creative thinking of the Grace Chapel’s Pastoral Staff and Elders who have a desire to develop a new generation of servant leaders committed to building the Kingdom of Christ. Bryce Baldwin, Pastor of Student Ministries, described the approach saying, “We loved the idea of a holistic approach to developing ministry leaders academically, spiritually, and vocationally. So we designed a program that combines financial support for academic growth, one-on-one mentoring support for personal spiritual formation, and hands-on experiences to hone the skills ne­cessary for successful mi­nistry in the real world.”

“Pillar College is so honored to work with Grace Chapel in this incredible new program” said Dan Wright, Executive Vice President.  “Talk about the ultimate educational experience for students. They can learn, serve, and earn all at the same time!  As the only accredited evangelical Christian college in New Jersey, we are always looking for new ways to partner with New Jersey chur­ches to build the Kingdom of Christ, and we are thrilled to be involved with Grace Cha­pel in a program that I’m sure will have a tremendous impact on all involved.”

The program will launch this fall with four mentored internships. Find out more by visiting www. pillar.edu. Those interested in providing financial support for the program should contact Grace Chapel at info@gfcnj.com.
Grace Chapel of Bedminster is a Christian and Missionary Alliance church located in Bedminster, NJ. For more information visit www.gfcnj.org or call 908-719-1093.

For more information about Pillar College contact (973)803-5000 or visit www.pillar.edu.
