The Easter Mission

Bringing a Revolution through Resurrection. God’s concept of community is to bring all of us together under one roof. It’s called the Plan of Salvation. This is His vision for us. Proverbs 29:18, “Where there is no vision, the people perish.”

The Easter Mission is that none would perish. The enemy of our soul has done a huge job on removing vision from peoples lives. This easter we should accept the mission of God to put vision back into the lives of people.

Start believing God’s dream for Your life. It all starts with a dream. It all starts with vision, an idea. What does God want to do in my life? First, Ephesians 3:20. “God, by His mighty power at work with­in us, is able to do far more than we would ever dare to ask or even dream of - infinitely beyond our highest prayers, desires, thoughts, or hopes”.

God says “think of the biggest dream you can think of for your life. Guess what? I can top that. I can do something bigger in your life that the biggest thing you could dream of. God bles­ses people who dare to ask. That’s the vision of God... to complete you. He says: “I am the Lord, the God of all people of the world. Is anything too hard for me?” The Easter Mission to share this big God with people and put new vision into there lives... His death, burial and resurrection, His vision is that none would perish ...but all would live their dream, and enjoy his resources and inheritances not only in heaven but even here on earth.

Pastor Matthew & Ka­thy Singh.
