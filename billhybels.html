Coaching Opportunity
Bill Hybels & Senior Leaders of Greater NY

by Bill Hybel

For Pastors, Marketplace & Mission Agency Leaders

The spiritual growth of individuals is more than just counting the numbers that participate in church programs or services. It involves a change in attitudes, thoughts and emotions-a change of heart.

Come hear Bill Hybels, founder and senior pastor of Willow Creek Community Church, speak about the startling findings of a major study of more than 30 churches, including Willow Creek, their successes and failures in making disciples, and new strategies they are considering to bring about this change of heart.

Participate in roundtable discussions with leaders in your area. Find out where your church fits into the grand scheme of moving congregants forward in their relationship with Christ. Leave with practical steps you can implement in
your church immediately.

Meeting Locations:

Thursday, May 8, 2008 @ 10:00am @ American Bible Society 1865 Broadway, New York, NY10023.

1:00pm @ Gateway City Church 257 Bay Ridge Avenue, Brooklyn, NY 11220.

Friday, May 9, 2008 @ 12:30pm @ Christ Church, 68 Church Street, Montclair New Jersey 07042.

Admission: $15 in advance $20 at the door includes meal and a complimentary book.
