Oasis Singles

Fall Celebration

by Cindy Galdal-Ruperto

Hey singles, are you ready to take a break from everything and enjoy a much needed fun night out? You are invited to join hundreds of Christian singles of all ages, from all walks of life and from all denominations throughout the tri-state area for an invigorating evening featuring a delicious dinner and dessert, powerful ministry and meaningful fellowship. Our OASIS SINGLES FALL CELEBRATION will take place on Saturday, October 25th at 6 pm at the First Evangelical Free Church at 6501 Sixth Avenue at 66th Street in Bay Ridge, Brooklyn. We suggest that you arrive at 5:45 pm.       

The dinner hour will begin at 6 pm sharp with a catered hot Italian buffet. The delicious menu consists of two choices: Baked ziti with cheese (no meat), salad and bread or Rigatoni with meat sauce, salad and bread. Singles eat alone most of the time so it is special to be able to fellowship with others as you enjoy your meal.

Our interactive program will begin at 7 pm. Let’s connect with God and each other. Guest speaker Diane Petrosino will share a liberating message entitled “Knowing Your Position in Christ.” You’ll be strengthened in your faith as you learn new ways to experience victory in the midst of the battle. Do you know how to recognize the enemy’s lies in your life?  Diane has been serving God for over 20 years in various ministries in her home church Faith Community Church in Staten Island. She married her husband Michael when she was 36 and has three step children.

After the program a scrumptious dessert assortment and fellowship will be enjoyed. OASIS is a very friendly place. 

Admission is $15 at the door. Tickets cannot be reserved ahead of time. The event ends at 10 p.m. Free parking is available in the church parking lot. Take the R train to 69th Street and 4th Avenue which is also called Bay Ridge Avenue. For directions visit: www.CompleteinChrist.com. OASISevents are held just three times a year.
