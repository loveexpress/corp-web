Oasis Singles Fall Celebration

by Cindy Galdal-Ruperto

Discover the power of praise! This year the theme of our OASIS Fall Celebration will be “A Night of Worship RAISE THE PRAISE!” You are invited to join hundreds of Christian singles of all ages, from all walks of life and from all denominations throughout the tri-state area for this uplifting evening featuring dinner, dessert, and fellowship.  It’s all happening on Saturday, October 24th at 6 pm at the First Evangelical Free Church located at 6501 Sixth Avenue at 66th Street in Bay Ridge, Brooklyn. We suggest that you arrive at 5:45 pm.              

The dinner hour will begin at 6 pm sharp with a catered Italian buffet. The delicious menu consists of two choices: Baked ziti with cheese (no meat), salad and bread or Rigatoni with meat sauce, salad and bread. Singles eat alone most of the time so it is special to gather with others to share a meal.

Our program begins at 7 pm. Let’s raise the praise and knock down our Jericho walls! Do you realize that worship is a powerful weapon for spiritual warfare? The Bible tells us to put on the Spirit of praise for the spirit of heaviness. The enemy hates when we worship God! We’ll join our voices and hearts as we sing some of          the most popular praise and worship favorites of all time. Musical guest Aaron Louie is worship leader at Grace Christian Church in NJ. Soloist Deborah Antignano recently recorded her first CD of contemporary Christian music.

After the program a scrumptious dessert assortment and fellowship will be enjoyed. OASIS is a very friendly place. A café atmosphere with low lighting and lots of greenery give it a very cozy feeling. You will have the chance to meet each and every person. Feel free to come by yourself or with friends. Our greeters will make you feel right at home from the moment you arrive until the moment you leave. Church singles groups are welcome. OASIS is not just another singles event. The focus is on Christ, not on yourself. The evening is all about HIM! Please come prayed up and expecting to receive from Jesus. All our fountains are in Him. You will be refreshed and renewed.

Admission is $15 at the door. Tickets cannot be reserved ahead of time. The event ends at 10 p.m. Free parking is available in the church parking lot. Take the R train to 69th Street and 4th Avenue which is also called Bay Ridge Avenue. For directions visit: www.CompleteinChrist.com.

Volunteer opportunities are available for the night of the event as a greeter, food server, prayer partner or as part of the clean-up crew. Volunteers should arrive at 4 pm. Volunteers are also needed the night before for decorating and set-up from 7 pm to 9 pm. Audio and visual volunteers are needed also. Being a volunteer is a great way to make new friends! If you are interested please e-   mail:Cindy@Completein Christ.com or call 718-836-0029.OASIS events are held just three times a year. This is our last event of the year. Don’t miss it!
