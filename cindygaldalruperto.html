Oasis Singles

Hundreds of Singles to Attend
the Annual Oasis Singles Valentine's Event

by Cindy Galdal-Ruperto

The biggest singles event of the year is our ANNUAL OASIS SINGLES VALENTINE’S on Sa­turday, February 9th at 6 pm. Our Valentine’s events always draw our largest crowds! More than 300 singles of all ages from different denominations and from all walks of life from throughout the tri-state area attend each year. We host these Christ-centered events at the First Evangelical Free Church located at 6501 Sixth Avenue in Bay Ridge, Brooklyn. We suggest that you arrive by 5:30pm because tickets cannot be reserved ahead of time.

Many Christian singles feel forgotten by their chur­ches. There are ministries for children, youth, married couples, men and women but few events specifically geared for the singles community. For over 15 years OASIS has ministered to the spiritual and social needs of Christian singles. Our main focus has always been to teach singles about their new identity in Christ and to provide an opportunity for much needed fellowship.

Our Valentine’s event will not focus on the worldly view of love, but on the One who is the Author of love. As Christians we are called to have Christ dwelling in our hearts through faith, so that we, being rooted and groun­ded in love may be able to understand the width, length, depth and height —to know the love of Christ which passes knowledge; so that we may be filled with all the fullness of God (Eph. 3:17-19). Yes, the Christian singles life is not to be emp­ty, but full and overflowing with the love of God.

We will begin our celebration of God’s love at 6 pm. with a catered meal. We have designed our seating arrangement in our café so that singles can easily talk to one another. You can come by yourself or with others. Many singles come alone and travel a long distance to do so. Friendly greeters will make you feel right at home from the moment you arrive until the moment you leave. Church singles groups are welcome.

The musical guests will be the dynamic band REV 7. This gifted group of musicians delivers a high energy worship experience combining the sounds of bongos, sax and organs. The band features Allen Cuffey, an on air personality for Christian radio stations. He is also a Choir Director, Worship Lea­der, Associate Minister, and Music Producer who worked with Motown legends at the height of their career. The concert will end with an altar call for singles who would like to receive prayer.

Fellowship and dessert will complete this uplifting evening. There will be plenty of time to meet everyone who attends this event. We have a special way of helping you do that! OASIS is a very friendly place. If you are tired of the singles scene, OASIS will be a refreshing change for you. There is no excuse to stay home. Come on out and make some new friends! This Valentine’s you’ve got a date with hundreds of Christian singles who are living for God and enjoying their inseparable union in Him. His eternal, unshakeable Love is worth celebrating!

Admission is $15 at the door. Tickets cannot be reserved a head of time. Attire is casual, but neat.

If you would like to volunteer please call 718-836-0029 or e-mail me at: Cindy@Completein Christ. com. For directions visit: www.CompleteinChrist. com. Free parking is available in the church parking lot, or take the R train to 69th Street and 4th Ave, Bay Ridge Avenue. Don’t miss this special event!
