Healing Through The Word

by Rev. Ellen Comis

 IT IS FINISHED

All the blessings and promises that are in the Bible were crea­ted by the love of God. He loved us so much that He sent His Son Jesus so we could be Saved, Healed and Delivered. How do we receive these blessings and promises? Many times we are waiting on God to send them. We pray “God I need healing and deliverance”  and we wait for God to send those promises to us. But God does not control the timing for miracles – our faith does. It is much easier to blame God for not responding to us and cry “God don’t you hear me?” then to take the guilt of realizing that the responsibility of believing and receiving is on our part.

Jesus did all that He could do for your Salvation, Healing and Deliverance.  In Gal. 3:13 it says, “Christ redeemed us from the curse of the law, having become a curse for us so that the blessings of Abraham might come upon the Gentiles in Christ Jesus, that we might receive the promise of the Spirit through faith.” The Cross provided the way for Jew and Gentile to be justified and made righteous through His Blood.  All satanic powers were defeated at Calvary.  The devil does not have power over you unless you give it to him.

When Jesus cried out “It is finished” that meant:

 1. Finished – was the payment for our sin.

 2. Finished – was satan’s control over mankind.

 3. Finished – was the ability of sickness and disease to control and destroy.

 4. Finished - was the hopelessness of mankind.

 5. Finished –was the control of addictions over our loved ones.

 6. Finished –was the spirit of fear and anxiety controlling our lives.

 7. Finished–was the destruction of marriages.

 8. Finished–was the demo­nic control of our minds.

YOU MUST BELIEVE THIS – AND ACTIVATE IT BY YOUR FAITH

Then He gave all power and authority to His church and said in Mark 16:15-18 “Go into all the world and preach the Gospel to every creature, he who believes and is baptized will be saved: he who does not will be condemned, and these signs will follow those who believe, in My Name they will speak with new tongues; they will take up serpents, and if they drink anything deadly it will by no means hurt them they will lay hands on the sick and they shall recover. 

We must learn to release our faith to receive the promises and blessings of God.  Remember the woman that had an issue of blood for 12 years? Well, as Jesus walked the cities many were healed but there were many that he never prayed for.  Matt. 9:18 says, He was in Capernaum and a ruler came to Him and asked that He pray for his daughter. As He arose and followed him a woman who had an issue of blood for twelve years came from behind and touched the hem of His garment, for she said to herself, “If I can touch the hem of His garment I will be healed.” She was! Jesus turned around and said, “Who touched me?” It seemed as if He had no plans to pray for her – but she released her faith, because she was determined to be healed.

What God makes available to us through His blessings are not necessarily pre- destined, it depends on our faith. Numbers 23:19 says, “God is not a man that He should lie nor a Son of man that He should repent, has He said and will He not do, or has He spoken and will He not make it good? That is one of the most powerful scriptures in the Bible.  GOD DESIRES TO BE BELIEVED AND TRUSTED COMPLETELY. Faith is simply confidence in God and what His Word says, His Word must come out of your mouth not what you see, feel or hear. God impregnates His Words with confidence and as you read them they become a reality to your spirit.  When you read the Word of God and God’s healing scriptures read them out loud – faith comes by hearing and hearing by the Word of God.

Testimony:

Mary McCabe Sea Cliff, NY

For about 6 months I was in constant pain in my feet, legs and back so that I could hardly walk. I could not turn over in bed without screaming in pain. Doctors could not diagnose what was wrong. On May 30th I went to Ellen’s Healing Service. When Ellen touched me the wonderful power of God went through me. When I went to sleep that night I didn’t want to turn over because I was expecting that terrible pain in my back – but when I did, there was no more pain in my back, feet or legs. I have been pain free ever since.  Thank you Jesus!
