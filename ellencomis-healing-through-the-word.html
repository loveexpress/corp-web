Healing Through The Word

by Rev. Ellen Comis

Christmas is the most precious time of the year- because it is the day we celebrate the birth of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. On this holiday something supernaturally permeates the atmosphere, its God’s love do you feel it? It is supernatural because some of the strangest people are touched and changed by it, absolutely amazing!

At this season we seem to sense more of a wonderful move of God’s presence and glory.You feel something different inside and if you dare you can enter into a place where you can all but touch the very heart of God and experience Him as never before. Paul said “I want to know Him and the power of His resurrection.” Dear Jesus, “I want to know You in that way also” Do you know what is so exciting, He desires us to grow in a deeper knowledge of Him and be drawn closer and closer to His presence every day. This isn’t something that should only happen at Christmas but it should become a lifestyle.

WE WERE CREATED TO WORSHIP GOD

Because God created man to worship Him, in the heart of every person is a longing to worship and we will all worship something.  What we worship is a priority in our lives.  Some worship their independence by doing what they desire and living life according to their decisions.  Some worship money. It doesn’t always mean that they are wealthy but it controls them and drives them. Others worship their abilities, talents, and so on. Jesus said in John 4:23 “The hour is coming, and now is when the true worshipers will worship the Father in spirit and truth, for the Father is seeking such to worship Him.” How absolutely wonderful it would be if we would fulfill what He desires for us and become a worshiper.

The Bible says in Acts 17:28 “for in Him we live and move and have our being.”  You are in Him and He is in you.   Praise God, His glory is already within us.  Jesus said in John 17:22 “..And the glory which You gave Me I have given them, that they may be one just as We are one..” If His glory is already in us then we are to be carriers of that glory, distributors of that glory.  So you have the ability already within you to worship.  1 Cor. 6:19 says, “...Or do you not know that your body is the temple of the Holy Spirit…” We were created to worship the Lord.

IT’S ALL ABOUT JESUS

My brothers and sisters it’s not about our ministries flourishing, our churches growing, or even our personal lives.  It’s about Jesus, it’s all about Him.  When we make worshiping Him our priority something happens.  Your life will change – you will draw closer to Him then you have ever experienced. You may say, but I can’t sing or play an instrument.  It’s not about singing or playing an instrument. It’s about a heart change, a priority change. 

Perhaps you can begin by getting quiet before the Lord, putting on a worship CD and just pouring out your heart to Him telling Him how wonderful, how powerful and how glorious He is.  Try worshiping with the Psalms “I will praise You, O Lord, with my whole heart, I will tell of all Your marvelous works.  I will be glad and rejoice in You; I will sing praise to Your name, O Most High.” (Ps. 9:1,2)  Tell Him how much you love Him above everything else in your life and how you desire to be transformed into His image.   It’s not about being perfect or what you do or don’t do. It’s not about your talent or gifting that gets God’s attention it’s your passionate love for Him in your worship.  What a wonderful Christmas gift for Jesus this year for you to become a worshiper not only at this wonderful Christmas season but every day of your life.

 HAVE A BLESSED AND MERRY CHRISTMAS
