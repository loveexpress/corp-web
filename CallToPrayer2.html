A Call To Prayer

Great Convocation at City Hall

The church has been given a historic opportunity to gather on Sunday, September 7, 2008, at 1:00pm.

There is an urgency to come together as one body, and as the body of Christ we must take the opportunity to place a mantle of intercession and bring forth a prophetic message over our city and our nation during these vital and critical times.

We want to communicate the concern of our heart regarding the gravity of this hour.

It will require us to stretch out of the comfort zones of the four walls of our sanctuaries, in order to participate in a mass gathering at a most prominent place, CITY HALL, NYC

If not now, when? If not us, who?

In ancient times when a nation was at risk or peril, gate keepers led by the king would gather the people and cry out to God for mercy, justice and His hand to save and spare the city. Politics will not save, charismatic or experienced politicians will not save us no amount of money or human reasoning can save us.

¡For such a time as this the church has been called! If not now, when; if not us, who? The King has called his people. Let the gatekeepers and intercessors, and all those that love the Lord and stand and live by His Word. ARISE. The time is now to cry out on behalf of our city. Join us on Sunday, Sept. 7th at 1pm in front of City Hall, Broadway & Murray Street in Manhattan.

Various renowned church leaders of our region are calling the church to participate with in focused prayer, praise and exhortation on behalf of this great city and nation.

¡We call you to action!

• PROMOTE
• PRAY
• PRESENT YOURSELF

This is not a typical event. It is a call to the church to rise up against the forces of darkness that come through laws and legislations that will effect and impact our biblical foundations. Let all that we do be to the glory of God. Let our voices glorify God and speak an on time word to this generation.

Bishop Dr. Hector Luis Bonano, CONLICO, Bishop Dr. Joseph Mattera, Christ Covenant Coalition, Rev Dr. Washington L. Lundy, Empire Baptist Convention.
