Men's Discipleship Boot Camp

Prepare. Train. Live.

What is a Boot Camp? God4Me Men’s Network is a National Alliance of Churches and Men’s groups systematically training and assisting men in their pursuit of significance and purpose. “Discipleship Boot Camps” are forums to provide encouragement, instruction and fellowship for the transformed man. Anointed speakers help men replace the lies of the world with Biblical truth. All Men, Leaders and Pastors are invited. Events usually fill to 50% of capacity upon announcement. Attended by 300-1000 Men per location. A continental Breakfast at Round Tables creates a “Cup of Coffee Listener” environment so men are free. Recognize-Resist-Retaliate to the given message.

Speakers include Author and Pastor Jim Cymbala, Head Coach Scott Caesar. There will be Music by The Brooklyn Tabernacle Men’s Choir.

The Theme this year is “A Man of Order in a World of Chaos.” Balancing Your Life Radically. Over 500 Churches - Interactive Workshops - Multimedia Parables - Release of the W.E.A. P.O.N.S. - Follow Up Curriculum for Men.

Come alongside your Pastor and support him by edifying his Leadership and encourage men to be involved with Church Male fellowship. This establishes credibility and empowers the Leadership to coach their men effectively and disciple and encourage men to develop a relationship with Jesus Christ and other godly men. Saturday, October 16, 2010 @ Freedom Chapel, 641 Broadway, Amityville, New York 11701 & Saturday, October 30th, 2010 @ The Brooklyn Tabernacle, 17 Smith Street, Brooklyn, New York 11201. The schedule for both meetings is from 8am-12:45pm. Tickets:$12; At Door $15. Team Pack 10 Men:$100. (This Includes a continental breakfast). Call now at 631-753-1244 or go to the web and visit www.discipleshipbootcamp.com. Sponsored by God4Me Network.
