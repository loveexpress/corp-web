47th Annual Christian
Ministries Convention?

Our 47th annual interdenominational Christian Ministries Convention at Hawthorne Gospel Church, Hawthorne, NJ is October 26-27, 2007.  Our theme is STAND UP! STAND TALL! STAND STRONG!

This may be your only time to worship, learn, and fellowship with Christians from all over the Body of Christ. It’s a little bit of heaven! Last year about 850 people from 173 churches attended. Our instructors, panel members, and exhibi­tors also come from many different churches and geographic areas. Almost half of our instructors/panel members are clergy or have PhD degrees, and most of the others lead their own ministry.  They (along with the exhibitors) will greatly enhance your knowledge and usefulness in ministry.

Besides having 100 workshops in all areas of Christian ministry and panels of experts to answer all your questions, we also have a General Session at 10am on Saturday with a GREAT keynote speaker, Pastor David Epstein, senior pastor of Calvary Baptist Church in NYC and ONE-OF-A-KIND worship leaders Sean & Troy Isaacs (identical twins).

Because of the amount of pastors that have attended each year, Pastor Epstein, our keynote speaker, will present a special workshop FOR PASTORS ON­LY on Saturday, Oct 27th at 9am. Special refreshments will be served. We know that a pastor’s work is difficult and hope that this will be a time of challenge, encouragement, and refreshment for them.

We also have 70 + exhibitors with the latest resources. The exhibitors include:Bible Colleges, Christian Publishers, Retreat Centers, Music/Worship & Drama Ministries, Family Ministries, Bookstores, Missionary Organizations, Counseling, Creation Science, and many other creative ministries that reach out to believers and non-believers alike.  To get a past list of exhibitors and some links to their Web sites go to http://www.ministriesconvention.com/index.php?op=18

Also, on Friday night, 7:30-10pm, we have a FREE TEEN RALLY presented by Jericho Falls. Last year we had 400+ teens and about 40 rededicated their lives to Christ or received Him as their Lord and  Savior for the first time.  This year the Rally will be held in the new gymnasium in the Hawthorne Youth Center.
