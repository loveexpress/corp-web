Businessmen of Faith:

"Anointed For Business"

by Scott Caesar

Businessmen of Faith, a businessmen's ministry of the God4me Men's Network, conducted another of their monthly luncheon meetings where over 50 local businessmen assembled at the Blue Lagoon Restaurant on Route 109 in West Babylon to discuss living their faith in the workplace.

Dennis Labriola, Director of Businessmen of Faith, hosted the spirited-minded gathering whereby he introduced Mr. Bill White, Entrepreneur and Chairman of God4me Ministries. Bill introduced keynote speaker, Head Coach God4me Men's Network Scott Caesar.

Scott welcomed the group, not only as the CEO of God4me and its related ministries, but also a successful social Entrepreneur with past successes in the garment industry as well as marketing. Mr. Caesar shared his experiences and training on his journey from success to significance, while interviewing a new found faith in Jesus Christ.

Scott shared a 10 year struggle of trying to live the life outlined in the Bible, while applying "his" business ways and knowledge to build business. Contrary to a "businessman of faith", Scott operated as a "businessman of SELF." As he attempted to meet the ever increasing burden of growing business, he found he needed to steal time away from his God and family, constantly re-craving the "time-management" clock.

Scott then opened his heart to the group and spoke of the challenge he and his family faced when illness struck his wife Debbie. As he sought God and mentors like Dr. Ed Cole and Coach Bill McCarthy who influenced his life he learned to adjust his ambition and efforts through his relationship with God, not around it. He spoke of employing "Priority Management" instead of time management through the Word of God. He was able to bring the "Art of Balance" into his life and find true success, peace and renewed energy.

Next month's Businessmen of Faith luncheon meeting's keynote speaker will be Dennis Labriola, President and CEO of IMPACT, a highly regarded technology firm. Plan to attend January 16th (same location) to hear a dynamic discussion as to how God brought balance and peace as Dennis maneuvered his firm through the infamous tragedies of 9/11 and through a personal family misfortune as God brought restoration and elevated his business to great success.

Businessmen of Faith is a discipleship and resource ministry of God4me Men's Network created to assist the local church in equipping, educating, and encouraging men with present day examples of God's work in the marketplace. We would like to invite you as we hear mighty men of God who journeyed the business valleys and mountain tops teaching that "There is no difference between the Sacred and The Secular" Businessmen of Faith also provides a means to network among businessmen and there professions.
