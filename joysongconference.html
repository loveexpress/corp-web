Joysongs Conference

Jared Anderson

Abundant Life Wor­ship Ctr. presents Joysongs Con­fe­rence, Friday, (Worship Night)-Saturday, (All Day Wor­ship Training), Octo­ber 10th-11th, with worship lea­der & songwriter Jared An­derson, Aimee Sych, She­ri Gould, Doug Gould @ 43 South Jefferson Road, Whippany, New Jer­sey 07981. Theme: “We are a people custom made to praise Him!”

Let’s gather this year with a heart to worship Him just as we were designed to. Isaiah 43:21 [MSG]

This is all about helping your church encounter God. Joysongs exists to educate, equip & em­po­wer with a passion to ad­vance the local church and to impart principles and technical advice that you can implement right away to make a difference in your home church or worship team.

For additional information call (973)463-9455 or you can register on­line: joysongsmusic.com
