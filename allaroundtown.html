AllAroundTown

The Praise ship has ta­ken off again to here and there spreading the good news of Jesus Christ. We will still be going from place to place to the end of the month, then the ship will be landing for an overhaul, so we can continue to do the good work of the Lord. I would like to take this time and thank Edith Stranges for this awesome opportunity she has given me, Bill who has the biggest heart, Rose and rest of the staff, Happy New Year to you all.

First stop was in Brooklyn at Oil of Joy Ministries for the birthday celebration for Apostle P. Wiley. I truly love this woman of God, because when you celebrate with her you can rest assure that God is going to show up and show out. The preacher for the evening was Prophet Marcus Johnson of Orlando Fla., who preached about travailing and messed us up. We were torn up from the floor up, but in a real good way. It is always a blessing to celebrate someone while they can yet smell the coffee. Blessing be upon you woman of God. 

Next stop was in Queens, NY at St. John CME church for the birthday celebration for Pastor Karen Connor-Wilson, also my BFF. The Lord moved in that place, it was a crazy worship, where you’re just about to lose your mind. Pastor Connor-Wilson’s, Preacher for the evening was none other than Pastor Charlena Crouch, who slew us with the word, “After this.” There are times when you feel so disconnected from everybo­dy and everything and God sends you a word to remind it’s not over until God says it over. The MC for the evening was Overseer Robert Nelson a mighty man of God all by himself. It was a wonderful time in the Lord after having a feast from the living bread, we had feast from the natural bread. Happy birthday Pastor Karen. May God’s fa­vor always follow you and be upon you.

Next we landed in Brooklyn at Pleasant Grove Tabernacle for a celebration for life fundraiser for Tunesha Cris­pell, who has battled with that demon cancer since 2008. Tunesha has a voice like an angel, so sweet and anointed. No one can really understand how she continues on day to day with a smile on her face, with cancer spreading on all parts of her body. She gave her testimony of how even the doctors don’t understand how she handles it. Tunesha re­plies nobody but God. Psal­mists came through to lend their support, such as Gratitude, Laos in Harmony, Bria­na Drayton, James Hall & Worship and Praise, David Bratton and God’s blueprint for life. But the highlight for the night was watching her son sing, “thank you” and showing his gratitude for his mom’s life. We honor God for this mother and son, and we thank Monique Walker Davenport and her crew, Devon and Patricia for the not finding it robbery to want to help someone in their time of need, that’s what friends are for, can I get an amen. Tunesha,  we will continue to pray for you, I still believe God is healer.

Last stop was in Brooklyn, at Christ Fellowship Baptist Church for the CD re- lease concert and birthday celebration for our very own James Hall & Worship and Praise. If you ever came to anything James Hall is involved, well you need to be dressed to the nines and be ready for some singing folks. People came from near and far to celebrate James. Psal­mists that came through were, Liv’re, Bubby Fann & Praise Beyond, Joshua Ro­gers, and of course James Hall and Worship & Praise who do what they do as only they can do. In 2016 please let us learn to celebrate one another. It is really not about us, but about the savior we sing about. Happy New Year and love to you all.

Dr. Gee
