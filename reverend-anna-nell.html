Reverend Anna Nell Wyatt, lmsw

First Anniversary as Founding Executive Pastor

Churchill Baptist Church Of Christ, Inc.
Harlem, NY, NY
90th Birthday on November 11, 2015

Anna Nell Wyatt, Founding Executive Pastor, Pastor of Mu­sic, Licensed Master Social Worker, Guidance Counse­lor, Psychologist, Early Childhood Educator, and Cosme- tologist is a woman of many vocations and accomplishments. She is also Founder of Churchill House of Har­lem, NY.

God called Anna Nell to the Gospel Ministry in her young adulthood. However, it wasn’t until after seventy years of prayer that she answered God’s call. She is now licensed, ordained, and installed as Executive Pastor. Hallelujah!

Widowed for forty-two years, the remnant of Anna Nell’s marriage to The Late Rev. John Willie Wyatt, is their daughter, The Rev. Theresa Ora Wyatt, JD, her Founding Senior Pastor.

Anna Nell has helped build seven Churches, including Christian Universal Temple Baptist Church, Founded and Pastored by her Late Brother The Rev. Dr. Curtis Rumph. She is the youngest daughter of twenty-one children of The Late Mr. and Mrs. Caesar and Beatrice Rumph of Branchville, SC.

Anna Nell’s Accolades include the following:

79 year Music Legend 2010, National Baptist Convention, USA, Inc.

Master of Social Work, Adelphi Graduate School of Social Work

Bachelor of Arts, College of New Rochelle

First NYC Board Of Education/DC 37 Career Ladder Program Graduate

Condemning the old PS 90, Man. and Building C.S. 200 Man. and I.S. 10 Man.
