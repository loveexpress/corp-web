Eagles’ Wings

72 Hours Non-Stop Rosh Hashanah At The Eastern Gate, Thursday-Saturday, September 18th-20th, 2014  (Thurs:12pm, 7pm & 10pm; Fri:12am, 9:30am, 12pm, 2pm, 4pm, 7pm & 10pm; Sat:12am 9:30am, 12pm, 2pm, 4pm  6pm) Calvary Tabernacle, 69 Myrtle Street, Cranford, NJ 07016 www.ctnj.org. Robert Stearns, David Hertzog, Matt Gilman, Clem Sa­ler­no, Aimee Sych, Jamie Fitt, Jonathan Fitt and Fran Mangano.

In 2005, the Lord gave Robert Stearns a vision to see the greater Body of Christ come together across denominational, generatio­nal, and racial lines during the season of Rosh Hasha­nah, to bring in the New Year on God's calendar with 72 hours of nonstop worship and prayer.

Call 716-759-1058 or Toll-Free:800-51-WINGS or visit rheg2014.com.
