Fuel Conference-Oct. 10th-12th

by Jacob Burgei

The three-day, power-packed Fuel Conference will be held October 10th-12th at Faith Fellowship Ministries in Sayreville, New Jersey.

Fuel conference is an extension of The Fuel Movement that was birthed out of the Northeast region to equip, encourage and refuel students, leaders, and pastors.

It is our heart to unite for one purpose, one passion, and one movement with thousands to change the lives of this next generation,” said Jacob Burgei, the founder of Fuel. “We want to see lives full of vision and full of purpose fulfill their destiny for the kingdom. Fuel will ignite in your purpose (uniting cultures, churches, and people), passion (connecting you to others both big and small), and movement (working together to Fuel up this generation to create a revolution of change).”

One Fuel attendee, Alex McCormick, said, “The Fuel Movement will, without a doubt, be a catalyst for a next-generation awakening in the church.”

Shekinah Powlett exclaimed, “seeing so many teenagers being transformed by the power of God was such an awesome experience! Teens were using their gifts to glorify God: it was amazing!”

Speakers and guests for Fuel 2014 include: Chad Veach, Ralph Castillo, Robert Madu, David T. Demola, Jacob Burgei, Rich Perez, Alex McCormick, David Ham, Danny Sa­na­bria, Aaron Gillespie, DJ YNot, Social Club, Hee-Sun Lee, Misfit Republic & Rap Society, and the Fuel Band.

“Voices from across the United States and around the world will converge around a revolution of ideas, as well as artists from all genres, all cultures, and all sounds coming together to reach this next generation of leaders,” Burgei said. “This is more than just a conference; it’s more than just an event, but the beginning of a movement.”

“Jesus is the Fuel for the journey, once you fill up on Him you will never run on ”E” again!”
