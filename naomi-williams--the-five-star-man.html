The Five Star Man

by: T. Naomi Williams
Released on Wednesday, April 8, 2015.

The Five Star Man” is a dating handbook written for women and discusses the hazards of dating while at the same time examines the overall stability of today’s man. It encouragaes women to use the dating period as a time of investigation and not just infatuation and suggest that choices be based on self-worth and value rahtaer than on pure attraction and desire.

Unlike so many other books on this topic “The Five Star Man,” does not instruct women in the method of getting and keeping a man, instead it focuses on men and discusses some of the reasons many are not suitable for dating and marriage.

While dating is not a biblical practice it must nevertheless be practiced sensibly & responsibly.

To assist women in the evaluation process “The Five Star Man” reveals (5) major areas of stabillity and uses a rating system to determine eligibility. This rating system has proven to be very effective by those who have used it.

The primary goal of this book is to give women a new sense of direction, purpose, and self worth, while equipping them with the tools needed to identify the man who is truly deserving.

Published by High Bridge Books, the book is available through bookstores nationwide, from the publisher at www.Highbridgebooks. com, or by visiting barnes andnoble.com or amazon.com.
