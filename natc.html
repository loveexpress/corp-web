Don’t Walk By Campaign

Imagine walking every block on the island of Manhattan. Your feet can hurt just thinking about it. 6,000 blocks may seem daunting, but “Don’t Walk By” accompli­shes this feat each winter over four weekends, all the while serving Manhattan’s vulnerable homeless population. An estimated 3,000 homeless people spend each night in New York City’s streets and subways. Don’t Walk By has addressed the community with food, clothing, shelter, and prayer successfully for the past six years. Get involved. NY CityServe & CityFest, 1865 Broadway, New York, NY 10023. Call (917) 475-1799.
