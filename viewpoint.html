Viewpoint: Dads

Around 1953 - Rose, Edith, Dad Henry, Mom Genevieve, Richard and Henry, Jr.

On any given Sunday after church at Van Nest AOG in the Bronx, and after our pasta dinner, our Dad Henry would take us kids to either Street Witnessing in Coney Island or to other churches in the city, New Jersey and West­chester County.

Mom sometimes stayed home with Henry Jr. until he got older. Imagine going to big and small churches, storefronts, even pastors’ homes. We were so blessed to meet so many wonderful people listening to sermons and my Dad playing the piano and also learning how to pray together and witness to others.

We celebrate our Dad Henry who passed away Jan. 27, 1984. A devoted christian Dad and Husband (when he traveled he sent us postcards addressed to his sweetheart Gen). A Dad who taught us “godly principles.”      

Love You Always,

Rose, Edith & Henry, Jr.

Appointed Dad - Joseph

Joseph and Mary were planning to be married. They were betrothed. Before they came together, Mary was found to be with child from the Holy Spirit. Joseph was a just man. He thought he would divorce Mary quietly. But as he thought about these things, an angel of the Lord came to him in a dream. The angel said, “Joseph, son of David, do not be afraid. You can take Mary as your wife; the baby is from the Holy Spirit. She will have a son. You shall call his name Jesus. He will save his people from their sins.” Joseph woke up from his sleep. He did what the angel comman­ded him to do. He took Mary as his wife. Later when the baby was born, Joseph called His name “Jesus.”

Handyman’s Prayer

Dear Lord

Please give me the tools

I need to nail the job

You’ve given me to do on

this earth -

a good measure of patience,

so I can stay level-headed

when I get into a fix...

the power of prayer,

because life is not

a do-it-yourself project...

the perseverance

to follow Your directions,

even when a wrench

gets thrown in the works.

Help me to remember

the nuts & bolts of my faith;

The blueprint for my life

is in Your hands,

and all will work out,

according to Your master plan.

HAPPY FATHER’s DAY
