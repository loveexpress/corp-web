Collyde One Day

New Leadership Event for all Leaders & Believers

By Jinu Thomas

Are you a Leader? Do you consider yourself a leader? Now, I could sense, as we have asked this question before in groups, that some of you, would immediately say “No”. If we probe further, you might, just like me many years ago, say, “I’m not a leader, I am just a regular guy”, or, “I’m not a leader, I am just a mom”. The truth is, God has blessed you with gifts and He has already placed you in a sphere of influence. Whe­ther you are a mom, a dad, a pastor, a believer, church staff, volunteer leader, lay leader or employee, you are a "called" leader. This means no matter where you are and in whatever season in your life, you are influencing your kids, your teams, your workplace and the people around you. So the question really is not if you are a leader or not, the question really is "How well do you lead?"

As Andy Stanley recently shared, there is an inexplicable relationship be- tween Leadership and Change. Great leaders do four things: #1. great leaders make things better, #2: great leaders love progress, #3. great leaders love to fix or solve things that are broken or need a solution and finally, #4, great leaders take initiative to lead themselves well.  

Collyde, a local Christ-centered ministry, is launching a new event focused on Leadership called "Collyde One Day." Collyde One Day will be held on Saturday, March 14th, at Emergence Church. The theme is "Leading yourself well". If you have a heart for leadership principles and want to invest into learning from other great lea­ders that lead effective lives and ministries, please register at www.collydeoneday. com. One Day will only have 250 seats by design. So register right away and secure a seat today. Lunch and coffee will be provided.

Venue:Emergence Church, 930 Riverview Drive #300, Totowa, New Jersey 07512. Visit www. collydeoneday.com
