Christ for all Nations
Reinhard Bonnke Gospel Crusade

Nov. 14th-15th, 2014 @ Nassau Veterans Memorial Stadium

Internationally known evangelist and founder of Christ for all Nations (CfaN) Reinhard Bonnke announced today that he will host his first-ever Reinhard Bonnke Gospel Crusade in Long Island, New York, on November 14th-15th, 2014.  The crusade will be held at Nassau Veterans Memorial Coliseum and will begin at 7PM both nights. Local chur­ches are urged to help promote the event and bring the lost and hurting.

“We’re winning millions to Christ, one soul at a time,” said Evangelist Bonnke. “Every single, solitary soul is precious.”

Evangelist Bonnke has preached face-to-face to multiple millions of people in Afri­ca. To date, more than 74 million people have respon­ded to the call of salvation through Bonnke’s preaching. 

“God spoke to me that the time has come for a mighty wave of salvation to sweep the USA from city to city, from state to state, and from coast to coast,” said Evangelist Bonnke. “America shall be saved.”

CfaN’s founder said that evangelism on any continent must always lead into the church. Evangelist Bonnke tells local churches, “I, the evangelist, bring my nets and would like to borrow the boats of the local churches.  Together we will cast the nets and bring in the haul for Jesus. I promise not to take one single fish for myself.  I will just dry and mend my nets and move on to the next station.”

Local churches that would like to help promote the upcoming crusade in Long Island may sign up at ww.gospelcrusade.org. 

Visit jmcmahel@cfan. org Christ for all Nations (CfaN) is based in Orlando, Florida, with offices in Ca­nada, Germany, United Kingdom, Nigeria, South Africa, Singapore, Austra­lia, Hong Kong and Bra­zil. www.gospelcrusade.org
