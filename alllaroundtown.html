FEATURES

AllAroundTown

by Ms Gee
myprayereagle@aol.com

Next we slid into Gospel Lighthouse at Canticles for my birthday celebration and 12 years as columnist for the Love Express. I can honestly say this was one of the greatest times in my life. My brothers Alvin & Calvin know how to treat their sister and I love them so much. It is an honor and a privilege to have family and friends celebrate your life and accomplishments. I had the greatest artists come to celebrate with me, Carl Brister, Queen of spoken word, Sis Desiree, and Minister Steve, with these three it was more than enough. My DJ for the evening, DJ Real there are no words to express my gratitude for what you have done for me, you wrecked the place. We ate, we laughed, we were ministered to and we celebrated my life. Myhusband gave me triple honor by presenting me a beautiful portrait with people whom I have interviewed. It gave my guests the opportunity to see some of the people I have taken pictures with and they were given the opportunity to sign it. Next I surprised by First Lady Delicia Garnes, wife of Bishop Garnes and members from her church, my first cousin First Lady Jackie Webb, and wife of Bishop Derrick Webb. Lastly friends I have not seen in a long time, I know for sure Dr. D is a keeper. It was a night I shall never forget, and I love you all that found it not robbery to celebrate with me.

Last stop was in Bronx, NY at Van Nest Assembly of where, Rev. David H. Hernquist is pastor for a concert with Carman. Carman gives a very interesting, but powerful concert. Carman uses theatric props, sounds and great visualization tools to help you see what the natural eye sometimes cannot see. It was packed house and absolutely no room to spare. Carman is ageless and if anything he just gets better and better. This man of God dropped some heavy wisdom of God in our hearts and minds. The audience rejoiced and participated in every song, they were definitely fans of Carman.

One particular person kept me in stitches with his antics. He knew every song and move of Carman and that person is none other than the youth Pastor Jesus Goyco otherwise known as “Pastor G.” This man of God has such a passion and love of God that emanates from the inside to the outside. Carman is the type of artist that likes the one on one approach with his audience and they loved it, signing t-shirts and CDs and even taking pictures. Carman all this makes me understand why you are still selling out to sold concerts and you really deserve it all.

By Ms. Gee
