1st Art of Church Photography Exhibit and Sale

By Duane Knight

The 1st Art of Church Photography Exhibit and Sale will be held from Sunday, Nov. 11th to Tuesday, Nov.13th with the official opening reception on Monday, Nov. 12th. This three day evening photo exhibit (Sunday 2pm- 6pm, Monday (5pm-9pm) and Tuesday (6pm-10pm) is an illuminating experience on church worship and those who make up its body of believers. Church photography creates images of tremendous inspiration. The work of four church photographers will be spotlighted in this 1st Art of Church Photographers Exhibit. The exhibit will be held at the First Calvary Baptist Church, located at 953 Putnam Avenue in Brooklyn, NY.

This year the exhibit spotlights the work of four church photographers who, like other church photographers, dedicate their talents to documenting Church Life and Christian Worship, from an insider’s point of view. Duane Knight, Allan Harris, Marc Thompson, and Jeff McWilliams, by virtue of their activity, create images of worship that have become a symbolic form of visual artistry preserving the rich history of the faith-based community. Our vision is to shine the spotlight on photographers documenting the rich life and history of the church everywhere.

Another major benefit is that the Media Arts program set up by their church teaches young people and adults, like Jeff McWilliams, a lifelong professional craft in photography, videography, and graphics design; while taking kids off the streets. Also to have Pastors consider implementing media art programs like them to education their members and the community as well.

For more information call Dr. D. at 917-520-7704.
