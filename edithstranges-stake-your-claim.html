Editorial:

Edith Stranges

May the Lord bless you with all of the blessings that He has stored for you in heavenly places. May you come into a new intimate place with Him, and overcome and press through into a new identity.

As you get ready to enter 2016, expect the Holy Spirit to move. This coming season is a time of a great move.

Do not fear as we enter a War Season. Let us go up to the high places that many resisted in 2015. Watch the high places. This is not the season for partial victory. There are certain things that the Lord is refusing to allow us to bring into 2016. Choose to let go of all that the Lord is requiring you to leave behind.

2016 will be a year to drive a tent peg through the enemy’s plans. Be like Ja’el (Read Judges 4 and 5). When the enemy comes near your territory, drive a tent peg through his efforts. Shout out like Jeremiah 29: 11-13 Stake your ground and decree the Lord will ma­nifest miracles in your life.

Until next month. Remember to go to church.

God bless, Edith
