Gateway City Church

John & Carol Arnott

Senior Pastor Roger McPhail of the Gateway City Church invites you to join us as we celebrate our 20th Anniversary Weekend on March 26th-29th 2010 with Special guests John and Carol Arnott.

They are the Founding Pastors of the Toronto Airport Christian Fellowship (TACF) and Presidents of Catch the Fire World (CTF). As international speakers, John and Carol have become known for their ministry on the Father's Love, where they have seen millions of lives touched and changed through encountering the Father's embrace and His incredible Love for them. The Lord then called them to Toronto in 1988, where TACF was started, and in January 1994, through a sovereign outpouring of the Holy Spirit, Revival started with nightly meetings and TACF came to the world's attention as a place where God chose to meet with His people. John is known for his teachings on the Father's Love, Grace and Forgiveness and the Holy Spirit.

He continues to impart wise counsel and provides a strong framework for those who want to see the power of God manifest in their church. Carol has embraced the message of Intimacy and Soaking. Her heart is to see those in the Kingdom become lovers of Jesus Christ and come into that place of intimacy. John and Carol live in the Greater Toronto Area and travel extensively while continuing to oversee TACF, CTF and Partners in Harvest, an affiliation of churches pursing renewal and revival.
