FEATURES

Global Christian Events Network

Global Christian Event Network prepares to unveil its Annual Ministry Expo & Awards slated for March 17th, 2012. Businesses, ministries and professionals will converge for a day of Networking, Exhibition and Conference entwined with an Award Gala to highlight trailblazing individuals and organizations in the Christian community.

This event will offer a networking platform for Christians Resource suppliers, Ministry workers, Business Owners. Entrepreneurs and Young Christian professionals to connect, exchange ideas and be empowered for personal ministry fulfillment. This event will offer participants the platform and opportunity to be empowered through relevant seminars, meet potential customers and supports as well as create opportunity for collaborations and networking for growth.

“We are really excited about this event’s offering which set it apart from the usual ones,” said Gbenga Omotayo, President GCEN - NY. “This event offers one stop shop for Christians to get connected, be empowered and also be celebrated. “This year’s CMA categories will have nominees who have impacted our lives through the act of selfless services be engaging their God given talents “They also shed light on serious social concerns within our society through their individual actions.”

The day begins at 12pm with an exhibition of Christian resource suppliers, ministries and not-for-profits organizations as well as relevant businesses, which services the Christian community. Running concurrently will be class seminars and panels covering areas like the role and implementation of Social Media, Team Building for Effective Growth, Starting a Ministry/Business, Ministry Branding & Marketing and Purpose Discovery.

The Christian Ministry Awards black-tie event ceremony follows at 3pm where awards will be given to recognize the year’s exceptional in different areas of ministry. The CMA Awards cover 8 categories which includes Pastor of the Year, Church Choir of the Year, Gospel Artist of the Year, Christian Media, Changing Lives, Ministry of the Year, Youth Group and Young Entrepreneur and will feature a lively mix of entertainment, celebrity presenters and comedic talents to celebrate Christian ministries’ best of the best. All events will be held at Brooklyn College Student Center.

About GCEN: GCEN is a unique social enterprise and event marketing organization that facilitates and hosts events which appeals to all classes of people within different age groups. Based in the metro New York area with global reach, GCEN caters primarily to the Christian communities through hosting of ethical and life changing events, such as the highly acclaimed “Night of Gospel Laughs” which brings over 500 attendees each year for an ethical comedy experience spiced with quality entertainment suitable for all ages.

For up-to-the minute information on Expo, seminars & Awards, visit www.gcenexpoawards.com and for information on GCEN, Please visit www.GCENMedia.com
