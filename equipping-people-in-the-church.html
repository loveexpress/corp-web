EQUIPPING PEOPLE IN THE CHURCH

Pastor Matthew Singh

Most people attend services on a Sunday morning with a need for God to meet, with an issue to be resolved, with a dilemma or with something that they want God to fix. They hope that something in the service will address this problem and by the time they leave it will be over.

While Nothing is wrong with this it this Gods people in the “I need Help” position.

God does not want his people to remain in a state of need…he desires that we we become EQUIPPED ….AND ARMED WITH THE KNOWLEDGE, WISDOM AND SPIRITUAL ARMOR TO VICTORIOUSLY FIGHT OUR BATTLES, DISCERN THE ENEMY STRATEGIES, BE IN GOOD HEALTH AND PROSPER…..

The book of Ephesians 4:12-16 states that God gave gifts to the church for the equipping of the church for the work of the ministry.

SO WHATS YOUR GROWTH PLAN…. WHAT PLANS DO YOU HAVE TO GROW YOU? TO GROW YOUR SPIRITUAL LIFE?

WHATS YOUR GROWTH PLAN TO GROW YOUR MARRIAGE, WHATS YOUR GROWTH PLAN TO GROW YOUR CHILDREN? TO GROW YOUR LEGACY, YOUR RETIREMENT? AS A CHURCH, GOD HAS BEEN DESIGNING A GROWTH PLAN FOR US TO FOLLOW. HE HAS CREATED THE CHURCH….AS A CONDUIT TO GROW…US…

The church is an environment that promotes growth. It Provides a growth diet, a growth plan. It's a place that pushes people forward. A place that Develops and equip others to serve in Gods kingdom.

Here are a few areas that we can develop to grow us into a healthy church:

Treat one another with a First Class mentality...

We all would love to fly in first class and be treated as ....first of the class

How we treat others should be reflected this way…..Put others in first class.

Give people a first class value .....a person of worth…..let them ride up front with you….Value people. Let them know they are Extra good.

Think Success.

Success is what everyone wants….the key to success is determine by what we do everyday….our daily agenda.

What you and I do eve­ryday is either making us or braking us.

Everyday we are either going forward or backwards.

Everyday we are either pre­paring or repairing. Where do we begin on our journey to success….we start with today. The secret to our success is determined by what we do everyday of our life.

Change your Work Ethic:

Pay now and play later or play now and pay later….

The  reason you take a vacation or days off is because you have worked hard for it.

The reason you celebrate with a bonus is because you earned it.

The reason you celebrate your promotion is because you have all labored all year for it. Change your work ethic for the kingdom. Laborers are few in the kingdom because of our work ethic. Grow yourself into do­ing some thing productive for the kingdom everyday no matter small it is. Buy a cup of coffee for a homeless person. Send an encouraging text to a long lost friend. Share an uplifting scripture verse with someone who is ailing from a sickness.

Become Heroic: 

As church leaders, men and women of God….we can be heros to others…. We ought to live and model …a life…worth copying…

If we live and model the life of Jesus and the apostles, we can become more heroes to others…..if you don’t have a hero in your life…..wouldn’t it be nice to find one in the church…..where men and women live heroic lives worth copying. Just being consistent, punctual, fair minded, rational, prayerful, positive, available, etc can make you heroic.

Become Anointed:

Anointed means To be favored. To be set apart. It’s the anointing of a king to have a position of leadership. Unusual favor, unusual blessing. The church offers prayer services, bible study, worship, etc...ask your pastor to help you grow and develop a life that flows with Gods anointing...it will impress him or her.

Become a man or wo­man of God, equipped for the work of the ministry. Begin today right where you are.

Pastor Matthew Singh: New Haven Ministries, Email: msingh@newhaven ministries.org
