Getting Your Resume Out There

You’ve worked hard to create a great resume. Now how do you put it in front of the employers you’d like to work for? Here are several steps we suggest to get your resume out:

- Post your resume online: Post your resume on ChristianJobs.com so Christian employers can view it whenever they have opportunities available.

- Browse the classifieds: Respond to ads found online or in local newspapers.

- Apply online: By forwarding your resume directly to employers, you have an advantage over the competition. Employers who have your resume on file may call you before an open position is ever advertised.

- Network: Talk with family and friends to learn about potential opportunities and have them forward your resume to the hiring manager for you.

Regardless of how an employer receives your resume, follow up with them! This will remind the employer that they received your resume and demonstrate your interest in working for their organization.
