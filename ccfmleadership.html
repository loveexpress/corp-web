NYC CCFM Leadership Alliance

10th Anniversary Leadership Conference 2010

Bishop Michael Blue

On Wednesday-Friday, August 4th-6th, the NYC CCFM (Christian Covenant Fellowship of Ministries) Leadership Alliance will hold it’s 10th Annual Leadership Conference at New Gethsemane Christian Fellowship Ministries Soul Saving Station, 145-41 228th Street, Rosedale, NY 11422. Day and evening sessions will held. All are welcome to attend.

In keeping with the scriptural foundation for the NYC CCFM Leadership Alliance, “And let us consider one another to provoke unto love and to good works...” (Hebrews 10:24 KJV), the Alliance comes together once again in their Annual Leadership Conference. The conference is a time for Pastors, Secondary Leaders, Future Leaders and Youth to receive empowerment, enlightenment and instruction. Overseer Wesley Knight commented on this 10th Anniversary Conference by saying “We believe that God is about to fulfill His promises to us. The things that He has promised will manifest this year.”

This conference schedule includes Guest Preachers: Bishop Michael Blue, Bishop Bobby Hilton, Guest Psalmists: Maurette Brown-Clarke, Pastor Wendi Wyatt. Evening Services 7:30pm nightly. Day seminars being held Wednesday, Thursday & Friday, with a special Pastors’ session. Youth Explosion, Friday, Aug. 6th, 12 midnight-2am with Prayz1, Richie Righteous & other musical guests.

Call NYC CCFM Leadership Alliance C/O New Creation Christian Church, 1060 Broadway, Brooklyn, NY 11221 Overseer Wesley Knight (917) 554--7390; agoodknights@yahoo.com. The NYC CCFM Leadership Alliance is an affiliation of the Christian Covenant Fellowship of Min. (CCFM) under the leadership of Bishop Michael A. Blue, Chief Prelate & Overseer Wesley Knight, Alliance Overseer. This alliance of Pastors in the 5 boroughs of NY have joined together for the empowerment, enriching & encouraging of senior leaders & secondary leadership along with preparing future leaders with tools necessary to succeed. The NYC CCFM Leadership Alliance has been established since 2001.
