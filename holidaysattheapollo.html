“Holidays at the Apollo”

The Apollo Theatre presents a series of holiday programs designed to celebrate family, culture and community. Throughout its excellence, and a creative catalyst for Harlem, the city of NY, and the nation. This year’s holiday programming represents the range and vibrancy of the Theatre’s artistic offerings. Highlights: Apollo Stars of Tomorrow (Special Performances by Iconic Boys) is a special edition of the Theater’s signature program Amateur Night, showcasing recent winners of the show’s Child Stars of Tomorrow category featuring an incredibly talented array of instrumentalists, singers, & dancers from ages from 10-15. Tickets for the Stars of Tomorrow Concert: $19, $25 and $49 and can be purchased at the Apollo Theater Box Office, Family packs available. (Coca-Cola Holiday Caravan: Free and open to the public) December 10th, 12pm-5pm.

The Coca-Cola Holiday Caravan will park under the Apollo’s iconic marquee from 12pm-5pm, offering a variety of holiday-themes activities, choir performances and a visit from Santa. Free & open to the public December 10th.

Ballet Hispanico - This special show will feature for the first time the world premiere of Artistic Director Eduardo Villar first work for Ballet Hispanico, an exuberant homage to salsa Queen and Apollo Legend Celia Cruz entitled “Asuka.” The performances will feature live music by Arturo O’Farrill and the Afro Latin Jazz Orchestra. December 17th.

Kwanzaa Celebration Regeneration Night - Apollo’s 5th Annual event honoring the principles of Kwanzaa through music and dance. Featuring renowned dance company Abdel Salaam’s Forces of Nature Dance Theatre,which is celebrating it’s 30th Anniversary. Guest performance by the noted trombonist Craig S. Harris and the Nation of Imagination, December 30th.

Tickets are $16; Groups (10 or more); $14 Tickets can be purchased at the Apollo Theater Box Office, 253 West 125 Street; call (212) 531-5305 or via Ticketmaster at 1-800-745-3000, www.ticketmaster.com. Mon, Tues, Thurs, Fri-10am-6pm; Wednesday-10am-8:30pm; Saturday, 12pm-6pm.
