Open Heavens Conference

Releasing the Apostolic and Prophetic

by Dr. Cindy Jacobs

During a very critical time for our nation I want to extend an urgent appeal for you to join us for our October Open Heavens Conference in New York. We will be joined by prophetic friends Dr. Cindy Jacobs, James Goll, Robert Stearns and more.

The church is in a very critical hour. Not only is the moral foundation of America fractured, but the moral foundation of the church is fractured as well. The church has a mandate to be spiritual salt and light. In order to see God’s Kingdom advance in the earth the church is in a desperate need of another Great Awakening. Not only must we be awakened to God’s presence and power, but we must also be awakened to the fear of the Lord with a return to biblical moral standards.

As we gather October 21-23, 2010 to seek the face of the Lord, God’s grace and power will be there to empower us to shine as God has called us to shine. We will be full of His glory and grace. There is a deep shaking taking place and God is calling His people to build an altar before Him.

Join us for this strategic and timely gathering to see a sift within the church and within our nation towards a better, more glorious future.

God told Abraham in Genesis 18 that if He could only find 10 righteous people, He would spare the entire city. Your presence matters! Your position in Christ’s righteousness can save a nation.

Our goal is to gather 1,000 of God’s people Oct. 21-23 to stand in the gap for the church and for America. We must take our place as watchmen on the wall to see a nation saved and God’s purposes fulfilled in His people for the establishing of His Kingdom in the earth.

Your presence is needed. Your voice must be heard. No more delay. May God send His Spirit now for Jesus’ sake!.

Thurs-Sat, Oct. 21-23, Open Heavens Apostolic & Prophetic Conference at Melville Marriott Hotel, 1350 Old Walt Whitman Road, Melville, NY 11747. Special Guests include: Matt Sorger, Cindy Jacobs, James Goll, Robert Stearns & more.

For more information contact Denise Loffredo Matt Sorger Ministries, PO Box 1648, Selden, NY 11784 or email info@mattsorger.com or call 631 696-4950 or visit www.mattsorger.com.
