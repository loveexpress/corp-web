East Coast Leadership Conference

Robert Stearns, Rev. Samuel Rodriguez, Dr. A.R. Bernard,
Dr. Mark Chironna, Clem Salerno

Eagles Wings’ present the East Coast Leadership Con­fe­rence, Thursday-Sa­tur­day, July 16th-18th with Robert Stearns, Rev. Sa­muel Rodriguez, Dr. A.R. Bernard, Dr. Mark Chiron­na and Clem Sa­lerno (Host Pastor). Wor­ship by Ste­phen Jenks, Ryan & Sara Delling and Aaron Ders­tine.

The theme of this year’s ECLC is “Restore,” Isaiah 49:8. Get ready to experience a fresh encoun­ter with God for yourself, your family, your community, your church and region. Times of powerful teaching, passionate worship and fervent prayer will strengthen you spiritually while opportunities for fellowship and dialogue will provide re­fresh­ment for your soul. Our pra­yer is that your whole family would leave this gathering refreshed, encouraged and equipped to bring God’s transforming truth and hope to your re­gion.

The Conference will be held at Calvary Ta­ber­nacle, 69 Myrtle Street, Cranford, New Jersey 07016. Register online at eagleswings.to/ eclc2015 or call 1-800-51-WINGS!
