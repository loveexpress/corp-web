Harvest Crusade

New York City-Area Churches to host Harvest with Greg Laurie Outreach at Madison Sqaure Garden, Oct. 19.

After bringing a preview rally to New York City last year that drew 9,000 people, Harvest with Greg Laurie will return to the city to bring its first large-scale evangelistic outreach to the area. Called “New York Harvest 08,” the outreach has been more than a year in the making and is being hosted by more than 75 area churches, October 19, at Madison Square Garden.

The NY Harvest 08 will feature a message from Southern California pastor and evangelist Greg Laurie. Called the “evangelist of the future” by Billy Graham, Laurie draws on contemporary means to describe the relevance of faith in today’s world at each Harvest event. Laurie’s talks encompass topics that appeal to people of any age and answer questions like: Why am I here? Is God real? What happens when I die?

In addition to a message from Laurie, the NY Harvest 08 will feature top Christian music from popular artists MercyMe, Relient K, and the Katinas.

With a trademark ability to present a straightforward gospel message in a culturally relevant format, drawing on the latest in current events, contemporary Christian music, and technology, Greg Laurie’s Harvest events have drawn more than 3.7 million people to stadiums and arenas around the world since 1990. In addition to serving as founder and evangelist for the Harvest Crusade events, Greg Laurie is the senior pastor at Harvest Christian Fellowship in Riverside, Calif., one of the largest churches in the U.S. He also serves on the board of directors for the Billy Graham Evangelistic Association and Samaritan’s Purse.

The Harvest outreach at Madison Square Garden is $10 per person, and audio podcasts and blog accounts of each message will also be available at http://blog.greglaurie.com/ New York Harvest 08 will be interpreted into Spanish and signing for the deaf will be provided. For more details, access http://ny.harvest.org/.

New York Harvest 08 will take place at Madison Sqaure Garden and is open to the public. Tickets are $10 each and may be purchased at Ticketmaster. For group sales of ten or more, go to http://ny.harvest.org/ or call 1-800-CRUSADE.
