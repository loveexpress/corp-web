Abounding Grace Ministries

Grace to Grace: Celebrating the Future.

When Abounding Grace came to the neighborhood thirty years ago, we brought a mandate to love the poor, addicted, and broken. Romans 5:20 challenged us: “Where sin abounds, grace abounds more.” The same impulse to minister to drug addicts, dealers, and prostitutes as evangelists, compelled Pastors Rick and Arlene Del Rio to start a church for street people in 1992; to race to Ground Zero on 9/11 as the World Trade Center was collapsing and most others were running away; and to innovate public school ministry in 2006.

Vision: Today we are again inspired by Romans to manifest the Kingdom of God in ways we’ve never seen before. The next chapter for Abounding Grace means empowering our next generation of leaders to evolve a church that was built from the streets of one of New York City’s most notorious neighborhoods to one that loves both sides of the street equally well.

“From Grace to Grace” will provide the public unveiling of what that evolution is going to look like. Join us.

Abounding Grace Ministries Anniversary (20 Years as a Church / 30 Years as a Ministry) Saturday, October 27th, 6pm-10pm at Wallace Hall at St. Ignatius Loyola Parish, 980 Park Avenue at 84th Street, New York, NY 10028 (Manhattan). Keynote Speaker: Pastor Jim Cymbala of The Brooklyn Tabernacle; Music by: Joseph Henry Cortese and a Band of Roadians of Crossroads Tabernacle, Bronx, NY. Silent Auction and surprise guests. Dinner Program to follow. Event proceeds go to the Project Promised Land Capital Campaign.

Call (212) 614-0370 or www.agmin.org.
