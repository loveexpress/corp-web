FEATURES

Music Review
April 2014

by Stacy Wendell

 Praise God from whom all blessings flow. I am so exci­ted to be back with you sharing heavenly soundtracks. This month as we welcome the return of Spring and the beauty and warmth it offers there are beautiful blooms in the music world. Our first CD is the much anticipated latest project on RCA Inspiration from Donnie McClurkin entitled “Duets.” This soulful project features inspirational duets with some of the finest Gospel singers in the industry including: Dorinda Clark Cole, Israel Houghton, Marvin Sapp & Lady Tramaine Haw­kins, just to name a few. I highly suggest you check this one out. The track “I Am Amazed” has been encouraging hearts for months on the radio.

Next up is James Fortune & Fiya with their latest project, a live recorded CD entitled “Live Through It.” This project may cause you to break out into spontaneous praise and worship wherever you are. It’s a truly powerful, anointed collection of songs. I was truly blessed, especially by the track “Just Smile;” a must have for any Gospel collection. 

Our final CD this month is a yearly favorite “Wow Gospel 2014” is jammed packed with music from all your favorite Gospel Artists of today; included in the mix are Hezekiah Walker, Wil­li­am Murphy, Tasha Cobbs, and of course a nod to the legends such as Shirley Ceasar, Vanessa Bell Armstrong, Marvin Sapp, and Kirk Franklin. This is a powerful compilation of music, you will not be disappointed. Great for those long drives. Pick this one up!

Well that’s all for now. Look out next month for the return of the Local Artist of the Month, you don’t want to miss it! God is LOVE.

Stacy Wendell is the Traditional Gospel Music Director for WFDU 89.1 FM and the host of Gospel Revelations heard every Monday morning from 1am-6am and every other Sunday from 7am-8am Tune in and start your day off with a blessing. Stacy is a proud member of Trinity Baptist Church in Hackensack, NJ (Rev. Jonathan B. Whitfield, Pastor) where she serves as an Associate Minister. Stacy graduated from the NY Theological Seminary Certificate program in Christian Ministry, and is currently attending Somerset Christian College in the Christian Counseling program.
