New York CityServe

For the past 18 months, interest and momentum has grown among 500+ church and marketplace leaders in the NYC metro area around a long-term effort to serve the city and proclaim the Good News of Jesus Christ.

This May, these leaders will come together - borough by borough - to continue the dialogue about this unified, locally-led initiative. This will also be the first chance for many other leaders to hear the vision.

After 18 months of regu­lar visits with church and marketplace lea­ders from every borough – plus CT, NJ, Long Island, and Westchester County – we are finally ready, with confidence, to officially move ahead with a long-term effort to mobilize and encourage community service across the region, including a series of catalytic outreach/celebration events culminating in a larger City­Fest celebration.

Here are some of the NEXT STEPS:

• Establish Formal Leadership Teams – The formation of the leadership teams for both CityServe and CityFest has begun and will continue for some months. The first official Executive Committee meeting is on June 10. The city needs all of you engaged as teams from around various focus areas of service (homelessness, human trafficking, etc), geographical areas (borough, neighborhood) as well as around ethnic groups.

• Strategic Borough Gatherings for pastors and leaders (May 13-22) – This will be the first chance for many leaders to directly hear the vision. Please help spread the word! I know many of you have taken part in one or more vision-casting meetings. These meetings in May are your opportunity to bring other pastors and leaders to hear the vision and get involved.

• The official launch of NY CityServe (September 23 @10am) – Mark your calendar and plan on joining us at Calvary Baptist (W. 57th St.) for this special gathering. Hundreds of pastors and lea­ders from the entire region will come together to launch this major city-wide effort.

• NY CityServe in Full Swing (October 2014 – Summer 2015) – The first phase of this ongoing effort.

• Re:New Gatherings (January–February 2015) – We will inspire and equip followers of Christ throughout the region through these intimate gatherings led by Luis Palau, Francis Chan, and others. Similar conferences are be­ing planned by the Youth Leadership team for high school and college students.

• Regional Outreaches (April–May 2015) – Gathering together as the body of Christ, regional outreaches will take place across the region as local leaders work together to share the Good News in meaningful ways in their neighborhood and borough.

• NY CityFest (June 2015) – While the exact dates and location for NY CityFest may not be known until later this year, we are looking at June 2015 as the ideal target. Locations from Central Park or Flushing Meadows, to the Barclays Center or MSG are all being considered.

• The budget for this whole effort is also still being developed, but interest has been high and gifts and pledges have already been raised both locally and nationally.
