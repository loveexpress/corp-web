Healed @ Work Conference

by Drew Campbell

Oct. 18-20, the first-ever "Healed @ Work Conference" will be held at the Sturbridge Worship Center in historic Sturbridge, Massachusetts to bring healing to workplace problems, relationships, and issues. The conference will feature top notch speakers from across the U.S., who have decades of experience in bringing emotional healing to wounded and broken people. Presenters will share through general sessions, workshops, and personal sessions. Breakout groups will be held for top management, middle management, employees, and entrepreneurs.

Keynote speakers: Pat Baldridge, Rev. Dan Lee, Rev. H.B. London, Dr. Ed Smith, Rev. Ken Unger. Music: Michele Wagner, Sturbridge Worship Ctr. Praise & Worship Team.

For Registration and more details call 1-800-JOY-9075 or visit www. HealedAtWork.org.
